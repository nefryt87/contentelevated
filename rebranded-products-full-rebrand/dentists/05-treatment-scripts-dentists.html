Treatment Plan Presentation, Insurance & Reputation Templates Present treatment plans that get accepted, handle insurance conversations, and respond to reviews professionally HIPAA reminder: Never reference a specific patient treatment, diagnosis, or clinical detail in

any public response to a review.

SECTION 1: TREATMENT PLAN PRESENTATION

SCRIPTS

Dentist Presenting a Major Treatment Plan

SCRIPT — DENTIST PRESENTING TREATMENT PLAN

DENTIST: [First Name], I want to walk you through what I found and why I'm recommending this treatment — I want to be honest with you so you can make the best decision for your health. DENTIST: What I found: [plain-language explanation. No jargon. Use visual aids where possible.]

DENTIST: Why this matters: [consequence of delaying — honest, not fear-based. E.g., Right now it's a filling. If we wait until it's causing pain, it becomes a root canal — more expensive and more invasive.]

DENTIST: What I'd recommend: [specific treatment in plain language — what it involves, how long it takes, what they'll feel] DENTIST: Our treatment coordinator will walk you through the investment and insurance coverage. Would you like to chat with her now?

[Clinicians present. Coordinators close. Never mention total cost yourself — let the coordinator do it with full financial context and financing options.] Treatment Coordinator Cost Presentation

SCRIPT — TREATMENT COORDINATOR COST CONVERSATION

COORDINATOR: [First Name], Dr. [Name] has recommended [treatment]. I want to walk you through the investment and what your insurance covers. COORDINATOR: Total fee: $[amount]. Based on your insurance plan, we estimate

they'll cover approximately $[amount], leaving your estimated out-of-pocket at around $[amount]. Note — that's an estimate pending actual insurance response. COORDINATOR: We offer payment plan / CareCredit / interest-free financing if spreading the cost would be helpful.

SECTION 2: INSURANCE COMMUNICATION

TEMPLATES

Pre-Authorization Update Email

SUBJECT Update on your pre-authorization — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I wanted to update you on the pre-authorization we submitted to [Insurance Company] for your treatment. [Option A: Good news — your insurance has approved coverage for $ [amount]. Your estimated out-of-pocket will be $[amount].]

[Option B: We're still waiting on a response — expected within [timeframe]. We'll contact you as soon as we hear back.] [Option C: Your insurance has returned a partial approval / denied the claim. Here's what that means and your options: [explanation].]

If you have any questions in the meantime, please call us at [Phone]. [Practice Name] | [Phone] Treatment Plan Non-Acceptance Follow-Up
