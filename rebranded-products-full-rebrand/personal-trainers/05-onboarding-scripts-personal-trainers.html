SECTION 2: NUTRITION COACHING ADD-ON

SCRIPTS

Introducing Nutrition Coaching

SCRIPT — NUTRITION COACHING ADD-ON CONVERSATION

YOU: Can I share an observation?

CLIENT: Sure.

YOU: Your training has been excellent — your consistency and effort in the gym are genuinely impressive. But I think your results are being capped by what's happening outside the gym. YOU: Nutrition is responsible for at least 50 to 70 percent of body composition /

energy levels / recovery / progress toward your goal. You can't out-train a poor diet — and I mean that structurally, not critically. YOU: I offer nutrition coaching that focuses on practical sustainable changes, not a

strict diet. A lot of my clients who added it saw significantly better results within 4-6 weeks. YOU: It's [$amount] as an add-on to your current package. Want me to tell you more about what it includes? [Present this when results plateau or when a client mentions

nutrition frustration. Timing is everything.]
