SECTION 2: STYLE CONSULTATION GUIDE

How to Run a Great First Consultation

✓ STEP 1 — LOOK (before touching the hair):

✓ Look at their hair growth pattern, natural parting, and density. Note any cowlicks or

areas that will need to be worked with rather than against.

✓ Look at their face shape: oval / round / square / oblong / heart-shaped. This informs

length and proportions.

✓

✓ STEP 2 — LISTEN (before suggesting):

✓ 'What are you thinking today?' — open question first.

✓ 'How long since your last cut?' — tells you the current length relative to their growth

rate.

✓ 'What do you do at home to style it?' — tells you how much effort they will put in.

✓ 'Any reference photos?' — worth asking every time, even with regulars.

✓

✓ STEP 3 — ADVISE (honestly and specifically):

✓ Tell them what is achievable given their hair type and current length.

✓ If a reference won't suit them: 'I can give you a similar feel — the way I'd do it for

your hair type is [alternative].'

✓ Be honest about maintenance: 'This style will need a cut every [X] weeks to keep it

sharp.'

✓

✓ STEP 4 — CONFIRM (before you start):

✓ 'So we're going with [brief description] — does that sound right?'

✓ This prevents any misunderstanding before the first clipper pass.

✓ [The barbers with the fewest complaints and the most loyal clients are the ones

who listen before they cut and confirm before they start. 90 seconds of consultation saves 20 minutes of disappointment.]

SECTION 4: LOYALTY PROGRAMME SYSTEM

Simple Loyalty Options for Barbershops

✓ OPTION A — STAMP CARD (lowest friction):

✓ Physical or digital stamp card. Every [X] cuts = 1 free cut or service upgrade.

Simple, proven, clients love the tangible progress.

✓ Implementation: print cards at a local print shop or use a free app like Stamp Me or

Loopy Loyalty.

✓

✓ OPTION B — SUBSCRIPTION / MEMBERSHIP (highest revenue predictability):

✓ [X] cuts per month for $[flat rate] — discounted from individual cut price.

✓ Example: $[X]/month for 2 cuts (normally $[X] each = $[savings] savings). Auto-

renewing via [Square / Fresha / your booking system].

✓ Best for: clients who come every 2-3 weeks consistently. Creates predictable

monthly income for you.

✓

✓ OPTION C — REFERRAL REWARD (best for growth):

✓ Every new client a regular sends gets [X]% off their first cut. The referring client gets

[X]% off their next cut.

✓ No loyalty card needed — just track it manually or via notes in your booking system.

✓

✓ OPTION D — VIP PRIORITY BOOKING:

✓ Regulars who book [X] visits per month get priority access to new appointment slots

before they go public.

✓ No cost to you — just first-access messaging. Makes regulars feel valued without a

financial discount.
