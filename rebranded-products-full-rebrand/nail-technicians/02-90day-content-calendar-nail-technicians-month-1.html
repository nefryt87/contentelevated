NAIL TECHNICIAN

90-Day Content Calendar 12 weeks of Mon/Wed/Fri content for Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest — with monthly AI caption prompts

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

Instagram is your primary booking platform — nail art photos and videos drive DMs and bookings more directly than almost any other beauty niche. TikTok is your reach engine — nail process videos and nail care education go viral regularly. Pinterest drives longer-

tail discovery — 'autumn nail ideas' and 'minimalist nail art' searches. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly with the AI prompt.

MONTH 1: PORTFOLIO & NAIL ART

INTRODUCTION

Write 8 Instagram captions for a nail technician specialising in [your style]. Topics: your nail art philosophy, a set you're most proud of, your most popular service, a nail care tip, a trend you love, your booking process, client prep guide, a direct booking

CTA. Under 150 words each, fun and specific.

WEEK 1: YOUR STYLE & SPECIALITY

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram Your nail art philosophy: what you believe about nail care, why you love your speciality, who you do your best work for. Wed Pinterest Portfolio pin: '[Your style] nail art' — keyword-rich description, pin to your best nail art board.

Fri TikTok 'My signature nail art style explained' — show examples, describe the technique, explain who it suits.

WEEK 2: NAIL EDUCATION

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram 'The biggest mistake clients make with their nails between appointments' — practical tip that shows expertise. Wed Pinterest Nail care tips board pin: 'How to make your gel manicure last longer' — evergreen, highly saveable content.

Fri TikTok 'Things I would never do to my nails as a nail tech' — authentic, direct, high engagement format.
