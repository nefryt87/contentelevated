Build a PT brand that earns trust, drives referrals, and fills your schedule. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Video guide • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: PRACTICE POSITIONING

Physical therapy is chosen twice — once by the referring physician and once by the patient. Your brand needs to work for both audiences. Physicians want to refer to a competent, communicative, outcomes-focused clinician. Patients want to go somewhere

approachable, effective, and worth their time and insurance co-pay. Your brand can speak to both. Your Practice Promise We help [patient type — e.g., active adults / post-surgical patients / athletes / seniors] [recover from or manage — condition type] so they can [return to

meaningful activity — e.g., get back to sport / live without chronic pain / move independently again] through [your approach — e.g., evidence-based, hands-on physical therapy / individualized plans of care that go beyond symptom management].

Example: 'We help active adults in [City] recover from injury and get back to the activities they love — through individualized, hands-on physical therapy that treats the root cause, not just the symptoms.' Specialty or Niche Options

• Orthopedic and sports PT

• Post-surgical rehabilitation

• Pelvic floor and women's health PT

• Neurological rehabilitation

• Pediatric PT

• Vestibular and balance PT

• Chronic pain management

• Occupational health / workers' comp

• Geriatric / fall prevention

• Aquatic therapy

SECTION 3: TYPOGRAPHY

USE FONT NOTES

Headlines Montserrat Bold Strong, clean, and active — conveys clinical expertise with a modern edge. Free on Google Fonts. Subheadings Montserrat Medium Consistent hierarchy — familiar enough to read easily, distinct enough to organize

content.

Body Copy Open Sans Regular Universally readable — excellent for patient- facing communications and print materials. CTA / Emphasis Montserrat SemiBold For appointment CTAs, key outcomes, and important callouts. Use purposefully.

SECTION 5: 7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Day 1 — Foundation

✓ Choose color palette (A or B)

✓ Select fonts in Canva Brand Kit

✓ Write your Practice Promise

✓ Update or schedule new clinic and team photos

Days 2–3 — Build Templates

✓ Create Instagram post template

✓ Design exercise demo Reel cover template

✓ Build Facebook cover photo

✓ Create patient testimonial card template

✓ Design one educational infographic

Days 4–5 — Update All Profiles

✓ Update Google Business Profile (photos, description, confirm hours and

specialties)

✓ Update Facebook profile + cover photo

✓ Update Instagram bio with Practice Promise + booking/contact link

✓ Update website team photos and bios

✓ Update email signatures for all clinical staff

Days 6–7 — First Week of Content

✓ Batch-create 3 posts using your 90-Day Calendar

✓ Generate captions with the monthly AI prompt

✓ Film one short exercise demo or 'what to expect' video

✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri

✓ Post first piece of content and engage with every comment in first hour

Patients trust the practices they see most. Show up consistently, educate generously, and let your outcomes speak.
