EVENT PLANNER

BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build an event planning brand that attracts dream clients and commands premium investment. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Niche clarity • Pinterest guide • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: POSITIONING

Event planning clients aren't just hiring someone to manage logistics — they're trusting you with one of the most important days or moments of their life. Your brand communicates before a client ever speaks to you whether you're someone worth that trust.

Your Planning Brand Promise I help [client type — e.g., couples / companies / families] create [type of event — e.g., weddings / corporate events / luxury celebrations] that [emotional outcome — e.g., feel effortless to experience and impossible to forget] through [your approach

— e.g., meticulous planning, creative vision, and flawless execution]. Example: 'I help couples in [City] create weddings that feel completely, authentically them — without the stress, the overwhelm, or a single detail out of place.' Specialty/Niche Options

• Full-service wedding planning

• Partial planning and day-of coordination

• Luxury and destination weddings

• Corporate events and conferences

• Social events (galas, birthday celebrations, baby showers)

• Intimate micro-weddings and elopements

• Multicultural and fusion celebrations

• Sustainable and eco-conscious events

• LGBTQ+ inclusive planning

• Virtual and hybrid events

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Days 1-2 — Foundation

✓ Choose your color palette (A or B)

✓ Select fonts and add to Canva Brand Kit

✓ Write your Brand Promise in one specific sentence

✓ Define your niche and ideal event type

Days 3-4 — Build Templates

✓ Create Instagram post template

✓ Build Pinterest pin template

✓ Design proposal/pricing PDF

✓ Create client welcome guide cover

✓ Build vendor thank-you card

Days 5-6 — Update All Profiles

✓ Update Instagram bio with Brand Promise + inquiry link

✓ Create or refresh Pinterest profile and key boards

✓ Update LinkedIn if doing corporate events

✓ Update Google Business Profile with new photos and description

✓ Add email signature with website and Instagram handle

Day 7 — First Week of Content

✓ Batch-create 3 posts using your 90-Day Calendar

✓ Generate captions using the monthly AI caption prompt

✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri

✓ Pin at least 5 event images to Pinterest with AI-written descriptions
