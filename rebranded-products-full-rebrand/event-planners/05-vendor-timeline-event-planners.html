SCRIPT — INTRODUCING THE BUDGET CONVERSATION

YOU: Before we go further, I want to have an honest conversation about budget — because it shapes every decision we'll make together, and I'd rather be transparent now than have it become a problem later.

YOU: Can you share a comfortable range for the total event investment? There's no wrong answer — it just helps me know what's realistic and where to focus our energy. [If client hesitates or says 'I'm not sure']

YOU: That's completely normal — most people haven't planned an event like this before. Would it help if I shared typical ranges for events similar to what you're describing? [Give realistic market ranges. Never let planning proceed without

a budget conversation — it always creates problems later.]
