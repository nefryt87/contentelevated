#1 No System for Tracking Renewals The Revenue You're Silently Losing

THE PROBLEM

Most solo brokers track renewals in their head, a basic spreadsheet, or not at all. The result: clients receive their renewal notice from the carrier before hearing from their broker. The carrier looks proactive. You look absent.

WHY IT COSTS YOU

Industry data shows that 82% of clients are open to switching brokers when they feel underserved. A missed renewal call is the single most common trigger. Every

client you fail to contact 30–60 days before renewal is a client at risk.

THE FIX (Do This This

Week)

Build a renewal tracking system this week. At minimum, create a spreadsheet with every client's renewal date. Sort by date. Set calendar reminders 90, 60, and 30 days out for each client. Your goal: no client should ever receive their renewal

#2 Proposals That Look Like Everyone Else's Your Proposal Is a First Impression

THE PROBLEM

Most broker proposals are either: a plain Word document with plan options pasted in, or a carrier-generated PDF with your name slapped on the front. Neither inspires confidence.

WHY IT COSTS YOU

Clients are often comparing proposals from multiple brokers simultaneously. A visually professional, well-structured proposal signals expertise and trustworthiness before the client reads a single word. A plain doc signals 'I'm just another broker.' THE FIX (Do This This

Week)

Create a proposal template you're proud to send. It should include: a professional cover page with your branding, a current situation summary, a clear plan comparison table showing cost and savings side-by-side, your recommendation

#5 No Brand — Just a Name and a License Number Professionalism Is a Competitive Advantage

THE PROBLEM

Many independent brokers operate without a consistent brand identity. No defined visual style, no clear brand voice, no professional digital assets. The result: every

client touchpoint looks slightly different, which unconsciously signals

inconsistency and lack of attention to detail.

WHY IT COSTS YOU

Larger agencies invest heavily in brand because they know it builds trust before the first conversation. As a solo broker, you don't need a $10,000 rebrand — you need a consistent, professional look across your LinkedIn profile, email signature,

proposals, and any content you post. THE FIX (Do This This

Week)

Define your brand this week. Choose: a color palette (2–3 colors), a font pairing (one heading font, one body font), a professional headshot, and a clear tagline that describes who you help and how. Use Canva to build your assets — it's free

and takes less than 2 hours to set up a complete brand kit. ■ IN THE TOOLKIT: The Brand Kit Guide in the toolkit walks you through exactly this process — with 3 personality archetypes, color palettes, font pairings, and a 7-asset brand checklist.
