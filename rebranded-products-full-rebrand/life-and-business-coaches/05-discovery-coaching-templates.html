SECTION 1: DISCOVERY CALL PREPARATION

Pre-Discovery Call Questionnaire

✓ What is your #1 goal or desired outcome right now?

✓ What is getting in the way of achieving it?

✓ On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is solving this? What is at stake if nothing changes?

✓ What have you already tried? What worked, and what didn't?

✓ What does success look like to you 6 months from now — specifically?

✓ Have you worked with a coach before? If yes, what was that experience like?

✓ What do you need to see or hear on this call to feel confident moving forward?

✓ Is there anything else you'd like me to know before our call?

✓ [Send this 24 hours before the call. Clients who complete it arrive with clarity and

commitment — and the call converts at a significantly higher rate.] Pre-Discovery Call Confirmation Email

SUBJECT Our call is coming up — a couple of things, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], Really looking forward to our call on [date] at [time]. To make the most of our time, I'd ask you to do two things before we talk: 1. Complete the short questionnaire: [link] — it helps me understand where you are and where you want to go

2. Spend a few minutes thinking about what success looks like 6 months from now — specific and tangible This call is a real conversation, not a sales pitch. I genuinely want to understand if and how I can help — and I'll be straight with you either way.

See you on [date]. [Your Name] | [Business Name]

Hi [First Name], I've been thinking about you and I want to check in directly. Over the last [timeframe] I've noticed we haven't been making the same progress or your commitments from sessions haven't been translating to

action. I could be reading it wrong — but if I'm not, I'd rather name it than work around it. Resistance and stuckness are normal parts of any growth process. But I want to understand what's actually in the way for you right now — not just on the surface, but underneath.

Can we have a real conversation about this at our next session? I'm not going anywhere — but I want to make sure we're working on the right thing. [Your Name] When a Client Wants to Quit Prematurely

SUBJECT Before you decide — a personal ask, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I hear you — and I want to respect your decision. Before you finalize it, I'd ask for one thing: a 20-minute call, just the two of us. Not to talk you into anything. Not to sell you on continuing. Just to understand

what's driving this — because in my experience, the impulse to stop almost always happens right before a breakthrough, not after. If after our call you still want to stop, I'll support that fully. But I'd feel I wasn't doing my job if I let you go without one real conversation.

Would [day] work? [Your Name] [Never guilt or pressure. Offer the conversation, then let them decide.]

SECTION 3: TESTIMONIAL EXTRACTION

Testimonial Request Email

SUBJECT A personal ask — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], Working with you on [their goal/journey] has been one of the highlights of my practice. Watching you [specific result or transformation] has been genuinely meaningful. I have a personal ask: would you be willing to share a few words about your

experience working together? Even 3-4 sentences about where you were before, what changed, and what you'd say to someone considering coaching. You can reply here or I can send a short form if that's easier. No pressure at all.

Thank you for being such a wonderful client. [Your Name] Guided Testimonial Questions

✓ What was going on in your life or business before we started working together?

✓ What made you decide to invest in coaching at this time?

SECTION 4: GROUP PROGRAM LAUNCH

SEQUENCE

Launch Email 1 — Announcement (7 Days Out)

SUBJECT Something I've been building for you — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I've been working on something I'm genuinely excited about. On [launch date], I'm opening enrollment for [Program Name] — a [X]-week group program / mastermind / intensive for [specific audience] who want to [specific outcome].

I'm keeping this cohort small — [X] people maximum — because the quality depends on it. I'll share all the details on [date]. Just wanted to give you the heads up — you'll be among the first to know. [Your Name] Launch Email 2 — Full Details (Launch Day)

SUBJECT [Program Name] is open — here's everything, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], [Program Name] is now open for enrollment. Who it's for: [specific description of ideal participant] What you'll get: [3-4 specific, tangible outcomes] What's included: [key program elements] Starts: [date] | Investment: $[amount] | Spots: [X]

If you've been thinking about [goal], this was built for exactly where you are. Enroll here: [link] Questions? Reply here — I read every one. [Your Name] Launch Email 3 — Last Chance (24 Hours Before Close)
