BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a coaching brand that attracts aligned clients and commands premium investment. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Niche clarity • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING

Coaching is a trust business. Your brand communicates — before a single word is spoken — whether you are someone a potential client can trust with their most vulnerable goals. The coaches who command premium rates and attract aligned clients

without constantly chasing leads have one thing in common: a clear, consistent, and authentic brand that speaks directly to one person. Your Coaching Promise I help [specific person — e.g., high-achieving women / first-generation

entrepreneurs / executives in transition / creatives building a business] [achieve specific transformation — e.g., build a business that doesn't consume their life / step into leadership without losing themselves / finally trust their own direction]

even if [biggest objection/fear — e.g., they've tried everything else / they don't believe they're 'the type' to invest in themselves / they've been burned by coaching before]. Example: 'I help high-achieving women build lives they actually want — not the

ones that look good on paper — even if they've spent years putting everyone else's needs first.' Coaching Niche Options — Pick One and Own It

• Life transitions (divorce, career change, empty nest, relocation)

• Executive and leadership coaching

• Business coaching for early-stage entrepreneurs

• Revenue and sales coaching for service providers

• Confidence and identity coaching

• Relationship and dating coaching

• Purpose and career clarity coaching

• Mindset and high performance coaching

• Grief and loss coaching

• Parenting and family coaching

SECTION 3: TYPOGRAPHY

USE FONT NOTES

Headlines (Authority) Cormorant Garamond Bold Elegant authority — signals sophistication and depth. Perfect for premium coaching brands. Free on Google Fonts. Headlines (Accessible) Nunito Bold Warm and modern — more approachable for life coaching and transformation brands.

Body Copy Nunito Regular Highly readable, friendly tone — ideal for

client-facing communications.

CTA / Emphasis Nunito ExtraBold For CTAs, key quotes, and stat callouts. Use purposefully and sparingly.

SECTION 5: HEADSHOT & CONTENT PHOTO

GUIDE

Headshot Guidelines

• Background: Clean, intentional — a well-lit office, a natural outdoor setting, or a

simple solid backdrop

• Outfit: Wear what you'd wear to coach a high-value client — authentic to your

brand, not a costume

• Expression: Warm, confident, and present — the energy you bring to a session

• Multiple shots: Get 3–5 usable options — different expressions, slight angle

• Consistency: Use the same era of photos across platforms — don't mix a 5-year-

engagements
