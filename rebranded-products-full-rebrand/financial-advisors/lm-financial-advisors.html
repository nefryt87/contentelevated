Free Mini Guide 5 communication strategies that convert more prospects and deepen client loyalty Note: All communication strategies should be reviewed through your firm's compliance process before use. The

advisors growing their books fastest aren't necessarily the best investors — they're the most consistent communicators. The Post-Meeting Follow-Up The consultation that doesn't convert within 48 hours rarely converts at all. A personalized same-day follow-up

that mirrors the prospect's own language and proposes a clear path forward converts significantly better than a generic 'great to meet you' email.  Summarize their situation in plain language (2-3 sentences in their own words)

 Recommend a specific approach — not a vague 'let's work together'  Propose one clear, frictionless next step Write a post-consultation follow-up email for a financial advisor. The prospect [Name] is [age] and wants to [goal — e.g., retire in 10 years / protect their family

/ grow their business exit proceeds]. Summarize their situation, recommend a general approach, and propose a specific next step. Compliance-review-ready. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name]. The Life Event Outreach System

The advisors with the strongest client loyalty show up at the right moments in their clients' lives — without being asked. Five life events trigger the most valuable financial planning conversations:  Marriage or divorce — insurance, beneficiaries, estate planning

 New child — life insurance, education savings, updated will  Inheritance — tax strategy, investment approach, estate implications  Job change or promotion — 401k rollover, income protection review  Business sale — capital gains strategy, retirement funding, estate planning

Write a life event outreach email from a financial advisor to client [Name] who recently [life event]. Acknowledge the moment personally, mention one financial planning consideration relevant to this event, and invite a brief call to discuss.

Warm and non-salesy. Compliance-review-ready. Under 150 words. The CPA Referral Partnership One strong CPA relationship can send 5-15 qualified referrals per year. The advisors with the most CPA partners maintain them with a monthly value touch — not sporadic check-ins.
