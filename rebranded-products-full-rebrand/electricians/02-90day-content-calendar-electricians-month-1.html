HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

Facebook builds community and trust. Google Business Profile drives local search ranking — every post improves your visibility. Nextdoor drives neighbourhood referrals — the most direct call-to-job pipeline for residential electricians. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Use

the monthly AI prompt to batch all captions in one session.

Write 8 Facebook and Google Business posts for an electrical contractor. Topics: company introduction, licensed vs. unlicensed warning, a completed job story, EV charger education, panel age warning signs, a safety tip, a seasonal electrical tip, a
