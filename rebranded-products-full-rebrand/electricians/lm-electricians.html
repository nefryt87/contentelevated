PROMPT 1: Estimate Follow-Up Email

Write a professional estimate follow-up email for my electrical contracting business. Customer [Name] received an estimate for [job type] on [date] for $[amount]. I want to: (1) confirm they received it, (2) briefly explain what's included that a lower bid might

not cover — licensed, insured, permit pulled if required, [X]-year workmanship guarantee, (3) make the next step obvious. Professional and warm, not pushy. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

PROMPT 2: EV Charger Campaign Email

Write a proactive EV charger campaign email for my electrical contracting business to past residential customers. I want to: (1) mention EV growth and that homeowners can charge significantly faster with a Level 2 charger, (2) briefly explain installation — what it

involves and typical timeframe, (3) mention investment range $[X]-$[X], (4) invite them to call for a free assessment. Professional and helpful, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

panel upgrades, (3) note response time commitment and licensing, (4) propose a brief call. Under 150 words. Professional and confident. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].
