EMAIL & FOLLOW-UP TEMPLATE

PACK

25 done-for-you templates for every stage of the loan journey Lead nurture • Pre-approval • Realtor outreach • Referrals • Re-engagement

HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your borrower's name, loan type, and your

NMLS/company info.

• The tone is intentionally warm and conversational — resist making them

more formal.

• Personalize the opening line for highest response rates — AI can do this in

seconds.

• Subject lines are tested for open rates — don't over-edit them.

• Save top performers as templates in Gmail or your CRM for one-click

reuse.
