SECTION 1: PRE-APPROVAL & DOCUMENT

REQUEST TEMPLATES

Pre-Approval Document Checklist Email

SUBJECT Documents needed for your pre-approval — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], To get your pre-approval moving, I'll need the following documents. The faster I have them, the faster I can have your letter ready.

INCOME DOCUMENTATION:

- Last 2 years W-2s (or 1099s if self-employed)

- Last 30 days pay stubs (most recent 2)

- If self-employed: Last 2 years federal tax returns (all pages including all

schedules)

ASSET DOCUMENTATION:

- Last 2 months bank statements (all pages — even blank ones)

- Investment / retirement account statements (most recent)

IDENTITY:

- Government-issued photo ID

- Social security number (for credit pull authorization)

OTHER IF APPLICABLE:

- Divorce decree / separation agreement

- Bankruptcy discharge papers if filed in the last 7 years

- Gift letter if receiving down payment assistance from family

Easiest way to share: [secure upload link / email / app]. Any questions — call or text me: [Phone]. [Your Name] | [Company Name] Missing Documents Follow-Up Email

SUBJECT One more item needed for your pre-approval — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I have most of what I need for your pre-approval — just one more item: Outstanding: [specific document or documents needed] As soon as I have this, I can complete your file and get your letter out to you. Would you be able to send it by [date]?

SECTION 2: RATE LOCK COMMUNICATION

TEMPLATES

Rate Lock Confirmation Email

SUBJECT Your rate is locked — important details inside, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], Great news — your rate has been locked. Here are the details: Locked rate: [X]% | APR: [X]% Lock period: [X days] — expires: [date] Loan type: [Conventional / FHA / VA / Jumbo] Loan amount: $[amount] Estimated monthly payment (P+I): $[amount]

What this means: Your rate is guaranteed regardless of market movement — as long as we close by [expiry date]. What still needs to happen before closing: [checklist of outstanding items] Any questions, I'm one call away: [Phone]. [Your Name] | [Company Name]

Rate Lock Expiry Warning Email

SUBJECT Your rate lock expires in [X] days — action needed, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], Important: your rate lock expires on [date] — [X] days from now. To protect your locked rate, we need to close by that date. Here's what's still outstanding: [list any pending items]. If the closing date is at risk, please call me today — [Phone]. Extensions are

possible but not guaranteed, and acting before expiry gives us more options. [Your Name] | [Company Name] | [Phone]
