MORTGAGE BROKER

AI Prompt Cheat Sheet 10 AI prompts to nurture leads, win realtor referrals, and close more loans

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace [brackets] with your details. Each prompt takes under 5 minutes to run — and replaces 30-60 minutes of writing. Lead Nurture Prompts Pre-Approval Follow-Up

Write a follow-up email for a mortgage lead named [Name] who got pre-approved [X] days ago but hasn't found a property yet. Mention current rate environment in one sentence, share one practical house-hunting tip, and keep the relationship warm.

Under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name]. Rate Drop Alert Write a rate alert email for my mortgage leads who have been in the pipeline for [X] months. Subject should create urgency without being clickbait. Body: what rates are

doing, what this means for their buying power (give a real example — e.g., 'a 0.5% rate drop on a $400K loan saves $120/month'), and a CTA to reconnect. Under 150 words. Realtor Partnership Prompts New Realtor Introduction

Write a professional introduction email to a real estate agent named [Name] at [Brokerage]. I'm a mortgage broker at [Company] specializing in [specialty — e.g., first-time buyers / jumbo loans / VA loans]. Mention how smooth, on-time closings

benefit their clients and reputation. Propose a quick coffee or call. Under 120 words. Monthly Value Touch Write a monthly value email from a mortgage broker to a realtor named [Name]. Include: one brief market update (rates, inventory, buyer demand), one useful stat

for their client conversations, and a soft offer to co-market or run a first-time buyer workshop together. Professional, conversational, under 150 words. LinkedIn Post Write 3 LinkedIn posts for a mortgage broker. Topics: 3 things first-time buyers wish

they'd known before applying, why getting pre-approved before house hunting matters more than most people think, and one mortgage myth that's costing buyers money right now. Each under 150 words, educational, ends with a question.
