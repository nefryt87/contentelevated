SECTION 2: INSTAGRAM DM CONVERSION

SCRIPTS

Responding to How Much Do You Charge?

DM SCRIPT — HOW MUCH DO YOU CHARGE?

Response: Hi [Name]! Thanks for reaching out! My rates vary depending on the services and number of people — I'd love to get you an exact quote. Could you tell me:

- What's the occasion? (Wedding, event, prom, etc.)

- Roughly how many people would need makeup?

- What date are you looking at?

Once I have those details I can put together pricing for you! [Never quote a price in the first DM. Get the details first — it prevents sticker shock before they understand the value.] Following Up on an Unanswered Inquiry

DM SCRIPT — UNANSWERED INQUIRY FOLLOW-UP

Day 3 follow-up: Hi [Name]! Just following up on your inquiry — I wanted to make sure my

message didn't get buried. I'd love to chat about your event. Are you still looking for a makeup artist?

Day 7 follow-up: Hey [Name]! Last follow-up from me — just making sure you had

everything you needed. If you've found someone already, no worries at all! If not, I'm still available / just had a cancellation for that date. [Two follow-ups maximum. Warm and not desperate. Most conversions happen on follow-up 1 or 2 — never on zero follow-up.]

When a Lead Says I'll Think About It

DM SCRIPT — I'LL THINK ABOUT IT

Response: Totally understand — take all the time you need! Just so you know, [date] is popular and I can only provisionally hold it for [24/48] hours before it opens to other inquiries. I can hold your date while you decide — no commitment, no charge. Just let me know!

SECTION 3: BRIDAL DAY KIT CHECKLIST

Pre-Wedding Night Kit Check

✓ SKIN PREP: Primer (hydrating + setting varieties), setting spray, color correctors

✓ FOUNDATION: Client's exact shade match + 2 flanking shades, concealer, contour,

highlight

✓ EYES: Transition shades, mid-tones, darks, shimmer/glitter options, waterproof

mascara, eyelash glue + lashes (multiple styles)

✓ BROWS: Pomade, pencil, brow gel, scissors, tweezers

✓ LIPS: 3+ options matching approved trial shade, lip liner, gloss

✓ TOOLS: Full brush set (cleaned and sanitized), beauty blenders, palette knives,

palette

✓ SETTING: Setting spray x2, loose powder, blotting papers

✓ CLIENT TOUCH-UP KIT: Blotting papers, lip match, small brush, compact mirror —

leave with bride

✓ HYGIENE: Brush cleaner spray, disposable wands and applicators, hand sanitizer

✓ LIGHTING: Ring light or portable daylight light if venue lighting is uncertain

✓ ADMIN: Signed contract copy, final payment reminder, emergency contact note

✓ [Build this checklist into your pre-wedding night routine. The one thing you forget

will be the one thing you need on the day.]
