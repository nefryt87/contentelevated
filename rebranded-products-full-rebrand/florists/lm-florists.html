5 Free AI Prompts to Grow Your Studio Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Wedding Floral Inquiry Response

Write a warm, enthusiastic inquiry response email for my floral studio. The bride [Name] reached out about wedding florals for her wedding on [date] at [venue]. She mentioned: [paste details — style, flowers she loves, mood board description]. My studio aesthetic:

[your style]. I want to: (1) make her feel I genuinely understand her vision, (2) briefly describe how I would interpret her style, (3) mention what a consultation looks like, (4) invite her to book a call. Warm and evocative. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 2: Post-Consultation Proposal Follow-Up

Write a post-consultation proposal follow-up email for my floral studio. Client [Name], consultation was [date]. Proposing florals for [date] wedding at [venue], approximate investment $[amount]. I want to: (1) recap the vision we discussed in 2-3 evocative

sentences, (2) briefly summarise the key arrangements, (3) present the investment and next step confidently. Evocative and warm. Under 250 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 3: Corporate Flower Subscription Proposal

Write a corporate flower subscription proposal for my floral studio. Client: [Company] at [office/hotel/restaurant]. Proposing weekly arrangements for [locations]. Investment: $[amount] per week covering [what is included]. I want to: (1) describe the impact of

beautiful flowers on a professional environment, (2) present the service clearly, (3)

offer a trial arrangement. Professional and compelling. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your

Name], [Studio Name].
