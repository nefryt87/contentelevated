5 Free AI Prompts to Fill Your Schedule Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: New Client Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional inquiry response for my dog walking business. The potential

client [Name] reached out about [service type]. Details they shared: [paste key details —

dog name, breed, age, any behaviour notes, schedule]. My experience: [brief]. I want to: (1) make them feel their dog will be in safe loving hands, (2) briefly describe my approach, (3) ask 2-3 questions to understand their dog, (4) invite them to a

meet-and-greet. Warm and reassuring. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

PROMPT 2: Walk Update Message

Write a walk update message to send a pet owner mid-walk or just after. Dog: [Dog Name]. Walk details: [route, duration, weather, any notable moments]. I want this to: (1) feel personal and specific to their dog, (2) share one genuine moment from the walk, (3)

confirm the dog is happy and safe, (4) be warm and brief. Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name].

PROMPT 3: Google Review Request

Write a post-service review request message for my dog walking business. Client [Name], their dog [Dog Name]. I want to: (1) share one genuine observation about their dog, (2) express how much I enjoyed working with them, (3) make a warm personal ask for a Google

review, (4) include the direct link. Warm and personal, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

PROMPT 4: Vet / Groomer Partnership Introduction
