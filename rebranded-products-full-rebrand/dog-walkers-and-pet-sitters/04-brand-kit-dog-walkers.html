WHY YOUR BRAND BUILDS TRUST BEFORE

YOU EVER MEET THE DOG

Pet owners choosing a dog walker are making one of the most trust-intensive decisions they make. Before they DM or call, they look at your Instagram, your Google profile, your website. A professional, warm, and consistent visual brand communicates that you

are organised, reliable, and genuinely care — before a single conversation. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for dog walkers and pet sitters.

7 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Happy Dog Walk Photo Post

Search 'pet Instagram post template' or 'dog photo frame'. A photo of a happy dog on the walk fills the frame. Your business name small at the bottom. Minimal overlay. The dog is the content — your brand just needs to be consistently present. Post one of

these every single day. No other content type builds a following faster for a dog walker.

Template 2: Walk Update Card

Search 'pet update card template' or 'daily update Instagram'. Design a branded 'Walk Update' card: your logo, a space for the dog's name, 'Today we...' and a few icons (route, duration, happy meter). Send as a story or message after walks. Clients love

these and share them — they become your best organic marketing.

Template 3: 5-Star Review Graphic

Search 'pet business review post' or 'client testimonial template'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a review. The reviewer's first name. Your business name. A paw print or simple dog icon. Post every new 5-star review — aim for one per

week. These are your most powerful trust-building content.

Template 4: Service & Pricing Card

Search 'pet services menu' or 'dog walker pricing Instagram'. Clean layout: your services, brief descriptions, prices. Your business name and contact at the bottom. Pin it as your first Instagram post. Add to your GBP profile. Eliminates the most common

first message ('how much do you charge?').

Template 5: Instagram Story — Availability Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'pet sitter story template'. Vertical. A happy dog photo or park background. Text: 'NEW SLOTS AVAILABLE in [Area]' + 'DM to book your free meet-and-greet.' Your business name. Post every time you open new

availability — generates immediate inquiries.

Template 6: Seasonal Dog Safety Tip

Search 'pet care tip Instagram' or 'dog safety graphic'. Clean, readable. 'Summer safety tip: [tip]' or 'Winter walk warning: [tip]'. Your business name. These save at very high rates, reach new audiences, and position you as a knowledgeable, caring professional.

Template 7: Google Business Profile Banner

Search 'local business Google cover' or 'pet business banner'. A professional banner for your GBP profile: your business name, your service area, a happy dog photo, your contact details. Your GBP banner is seen by every person who finds you in local search

— make it warm and professional.

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit.

✓ Day 2: Update Instagram bio, profile photo, and create 6 story highlights: Dogs I

Walk, Reviews, Services, About Me, Walk Updates, Booking.

✓ Day 3: Create your service and pricing card using Template 4 — pin it as your first

Instagram post.

✓ Day 4: Set up or update your Google Business Profile — add all services, photos,

and your first GBP post.

✓ Day 5: Create 5 review graphics using Template 3 — schedule one per week for 5

weeks.

✓ Day 6: Create a seasonal safety tip graphic using Template 6 — post this week.

✓ Day 7: Create a walk update story template using Template 2 — use it on your next

walk.

✓ [Consistency is everything for a dog walking business. Pet owners who see you

posting daily walk updates, safety tips, and happy dogs build trust over weeks and months before they even need your service — then you are their first call.]
