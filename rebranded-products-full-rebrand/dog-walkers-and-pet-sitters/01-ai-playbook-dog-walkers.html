DOG WALKER

The AI Playbook for Dog Walkers & Pet Sitters Build a fully booked client roster, earn 5-star reviews on every platform, grow through referrals, and create the trust-based client experience that keeps dogs — and their owners — coming back

INTRODUCTION

The dog walkers and pet sitters with fully booked schedules and waitlists aren't just the ones who love dogs most. They respond to every inquiry within hours with a message that makes pet owners feel immediately at ease. They build a consultation and

onboarding process that communicates professionalism and genuine care before they ever hold a leash. They send update photos and check-ins during every walk or sit that turn one-time clients into loyal advocates. They generate consistent 5-star reviews on

Google, Rover, and Wag that compound into inbound demand. And they build referral relationships with vets, groomers, and trainers that fill their schedule without advertising. This playbook covers every high-leverage scenario — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

CHAPTER 1: INQUIRY RESPONSE & NEW

CLIENT CONVERSION

The Inquiry Response System Pet owners reaching out for a dog walker are making a trust decision — they're handing over a family member. Your first response sets the tone. A warm, knowledgeable, and

reassuring reply within a few hours converts dramatically better than a delayed or generic one.

PROMPT — NEW CLIENT INQUIRY RESPONSE

Write a warm, professional inquiry response for my dog walking business. The potential client [Name] reached out about [service — e.g., daily dog walks / pet sitting / boarding]. They mentioned: [paste key details — dog's name, breed, age,

any notes about behaviour, schedule they need]. I want to: (1) make them feel immediately at ease that their dog will be in safe, loving hands, (2) briefly describe my approach and experience, (3) ask 2-3 questions to understand their dog's needs,

(4) invite them to a meet-and-greet. Warm, reassuring, and enthusiastic. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

CHAPTER 2: THE CLIENT EXPERIENCE SYSTEM

Walk Updates & Photo Check-Ins The single most effective thing a dog walker can do to create loyal clients and generate reviews is send a photo update during or after every walk. Pet owners who receive a

happy photo of their dog mid-walk feel immediate relief, gratitude, and loyalty. This costs two minutes and generates reviews, referrals, and retained clients.

PROMPT — WALK UPDATE MESSAGE

Write a walk update message to send a pet owner mid-walk or just after. Dog: [Dog's Name]. Walk details: [route / duration / weather / any notable moments — e.g., 'he found a brilliant puddle' / 'she made a new friend at the park']. I want this

message to: (1) feel personal and specific to their dog, (2) share one genuine moment from the walk, (3) confirm the dog is happy and safe, (4) be warm and brief. Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name].

CHAPTER 3: REVIEWS & REFERRAL SYSTEM

Generating 5-Star Reviews Reviews on Google, Rover, Wag, and Facebook are the primary way new pet owners discover and trust dog walkers. The walkers with 50+ 5-star reviews fill their schedules from inbound demand alone. Most walkers never systematically ask.

PROMPT — REVIEW REQUEST MESSAGE

Write a post-service review request message for my dog walking business. Client [Name], their dog [Dog's Name]. Service: [what was provided]. I want to: (1) share one genuine observation about their dog, (2) express how much I enjoyed working

with them, (3) make a warm personal ask for a review on [Google / Rover / Facebook], (4) include the direct link. Warm and personal, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name]. Building Referral Partnerships

Veterinarians, groomers, dog trainers, and pet supply shops all serve the same pet owner. One active referral relationship with a local vet can generate 5-15 new clients per year. These relationships are almost entirely untapped by most dog walkers.

Dog content is among the most engaged content on Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. A consistent presence with genuine, personality-led pet content attracts new clients, keeps existing ones engaged, and generates referrals. The dog walkers with full

schedules post consistently across 4 pillars: dog photos/videos (always works), education (safety, training tips, breed info), behind-the-scenes (your day, your routine), and social proof (reviews, client testimonials).

PROMPT — INSTAGRAM CAPTION BATCH

Write 5 Instagram captions for a dog walker and pet sitter. Topics: (1) a specific dog you walked recently — their personality, a funny or sweet moment from the walk (anonymise if needed), (2) a dog safety or care tip that shows expertise, (3) behind-

the-scenes of your day — what a typical walk day looks like, (4) a client testimonial or review highlight, (5) a direct booking CTA with your current availability. Each under 150 words, warm and personality-led. Business name: [Business Name].

POLICIES

Communicating Policies Professionally

PROMPT — CANCELLATION POLICY COMMUNICATION

Write a professional, friendly cancellation policy message for my dog walking business. Policy: I require [X] hours / days notice to cancel without charge. Cancellations within [X] hours are charged [X]%. I want to communicate this firmly

but warmly — framed as necessary to protect availability for all clients. Under 100 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

PROMPT — HOLIDAY / AVAILABILITY NOTICE

Write a holiday availability notice for my dog walking business. I will be unavailable from [dates]. I want to: (1) notify clients well in advance, (2) recommend they book early for any needed coverage during this period, (3) if applicable, mention a trusted

colleague who covers for me. Warm and practical. Under 100 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].
