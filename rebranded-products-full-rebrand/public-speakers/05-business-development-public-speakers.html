SECTION 2: SPEAKER FEE GUIDE

How to Set and Hold Your Speaking Fee

SPEAKER LEVEL TYPICAL FEE RANGE WHAT GETS YOU HERE

Emerging / New Speaker $500 — $2,500 Active speaking calendar, developing signature talk, building testimonials. Local and regional bookings. Established Speaker $2,500 — $7,500 Recognised expertise, 20+ paid engagements, strong testimonials, active

LinkedIn presence, professional reel. Professional Speaker $7,500 — $20,000 Published author OR media profile OR significant industry credibility. National conference bookings. Bureau representation. Celebrity / Authority Speaker

$20,000+ Major media presence, bestselling book, C- suite credibility, international demand. Bureau representation essential.

✓ FEE-SETTING PRINCIPLES:

✓ 1. Set a fee you can hold. If you quote it and immediately reduce it when pushed, it

was never your real fee.

✓ 2. Your fee is not just for the 45 minutes on stage. It is for years of expertise, hours

of preparation, customisation for their specific audience, and the intellectual property you are sharing.

✓ 3. Raise your fee after every 10 paid engagements. Each booking is evidence that

the market values your work at that rate. Demand above that rate is evidence it will bear more.

✓ 4. Have a non-negotiables list: expenses (travel, accommodation) are always in

addition to your fee. Never include expenses in your fee quote.
