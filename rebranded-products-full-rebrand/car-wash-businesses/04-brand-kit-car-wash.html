WHY YOUR BRAND IS THE FIRST REASON

CUSTOMERS CHOOSE YOU

When a driver searches 'car wash near me' they see your Google profile, your photos, and your reviews before they ever visit. Your brand — your colours, your signage aesthetic, your social media presence — communicates quality before a single water jet

is fired. A clean, confident, consistent visual identity builds the kind of trust that converts search results into regular customers and members. This kit gives you two palettes,

typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for car wash businesses.

7 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Before/After Transformation Post

Search 'before after car detail Instagram' or 'vehicle transformation post'. Two-panel: dirty car left, clean car right. Your business name and location at the bottom. A compelling caption about the difference. These are your highest-performing content

type — post every great transformation result. Before/afters drive more inquiries than any other content for a detailing business.

Template 2: Membership Value Card

Search 'membership card Instagram' or 'subscription offer post'. Clean layout: your membership name, key benefits as bullet points, monthly price, 'SAVE $[X]/MONTH' callout. Your business name and sign-up link. Use your accent colour (blue or yellow)

for the price. Post this monthly as a pinned post and update seasonally.

Template 3: Google Business Profile Weekly Post

Search 'local business update post' or 'Google business post template'. Clean, simple. Your service, a CTA, your address. Post every Monday — each GBP post improves your ranking for 'car wash near me'. 10 minutes per week, significant long-term impact.

Include a photo of your facility or a clean car every time.

Template 4: Seasonal Promotion Banner

Search 'car wash promotion banner' or 'seasonal offer social media'. Bold, high-visibility. Your offer in the largest font. Dates. Your business name. Use your high-visibility accent colour (Electric Blue or Yellow) for the background. Post to Facebook, Instagram, and

GBP simultaneously when running any promotion.

Template 5: 5-Star Review Graphic

Search 'Google review Instagram' or 'customer testimonial local business'. 5 stars at the top. The review text. Reviewer's first name. Your business name and 'Google Review' label. Post every new 5-star review — aim for one per week. Potential customers read

these more than any other content type.

Template 6: Staff Spotlight Post

Search 'team member spotlight Instagram' or 'employee of month post'. Employee photo (with consent), their name, their role, one genuine thing about them. Your business name. These humanise your brand and build community connection —

customers who recognise staff return more often.

Template 7: Fleet/Corporate Enquiry Post

Search 'fleet service business post' or 'corporate account advertising'. Professional layout: 'WE SERVICE FLEETS' in large text. Key benefits: priority scheduling, volume

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit.

✓ Day 2: Update your Google Business Profile — new photos (inside, outside, team),

updated hours, all services listed.

✓ Day 3: Create your membership value card using Template 2 — pin it as your first

Facebook and Instagram post.

✓ Day 4: Create your first GBP post using Template 3 — publish immediately.

✓ Day 5: Create 5 review graphics using Template 5 — schedule one per week for 5

weeks.

✓ Day 6: Set up a seasonal promotion banner using Template 4 for your current or

upcoming offer.

✓ Day 7: Create a staff spotlight post using Template 6 for your longest-serving or

most recognisable team member.

✓ [For a local car wash, your Google Business Profile is your most important

marketing asset. A fully optimised GBP with regular posts, many reviews, and professional photos will generate more new customers than any paid advertising at this stage.]
