PERSONAL STYLIST

Brand Kit + Canva Template Guide

Two signature palettes, editorial typography, 8 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND IS YOUR FIRST STYLING

STATEMENT

Potential clients Google a personal stylist before they ever send an inquiry — and the first thing they evaluate is whether your brand reflects the taste and expertise they're hoping to hire. A cohesive, beautiful visual identity signals: this stylist has the eye, the

confidence, and the premium positioning I'm looking for. This kit gives you two palettes,

typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for personal stylists and

fashion consultants.

Typography Pairing

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — the editorial serif of choice for luxury fashion brands worldwide. BODY: Jost — geometric sans-serif with warmth. Clean and modern without being cold. ACCENT: Pinyon Script or Alex Brush — for personal touches, taglines, and quote

graphics. Use sparingly.

8 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Before & After Client Transformation

Search 'before after comparison Instagram' or 'transformation post'. Two-column layout: before (what their wardrobe looked like / how they felt) on left, after (the capsule / the looks / the confidence) on right. Your logo bottom corner. These generate more saves

and DMs than any other post type.

Template 2: Style Tips Carousel

Search 'fashion tips carousel' or 'style advice slides Instagram'. 5-7 slides: cover ('The 5-Piece Capsule Formula'), one tip per slide, final CTA slide with your name and

booking link. Highly shareable — often goes semi-viral in style communities.

Template 3: Outfit Formula Graphic

Search 'outfit formula template' or 'styling guide Instagram'. Clean flat-lay style layout showing how to combine 3-5 pieces into multiple outfits. Label each piece. Add your logo. These save at extremely high rates — they're genuinely useful reference graphics.

Template 4: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'fashion testimonial quote' or 'client transformation quote'. Feature the most emotional or specific line from a review. First name and service type below. Your business name. Build trust before any conversation starts.

Template 5: Pinterest Style Board

Search 'mood board Pinterest' or 'fashion inspiration collage'. Grid of 6-9 curated images representing a specific style aesthetic, season, or wardrobe goal. A well- keyworded Pinterest board can generate inquiries years after posting.

Template 6: Instagram Story — Booking CTA

Search 'Instagram story booking' or 'consultation story template'. Vertical. A beautiful photo of you or your styling work as background. Overlay: 'Now booking [season] styling sessions' + 'limited availability'. Link sticker. Simple, clean, on-brand.

Template 7: TikTok Text Overlay Template

Search 'TikTok text graphic' or 'social media reel cover'. For the cover frame of your TikToks: your key point in large text, your name small at bottom, your brand colour background. Viewers see this before pressing play — make it impossible not to click.

Template 8: Highlight Covers

Search 'Instagram highlight covers fashion' or 'Instagram story icons'. Create 6-8 round covers: Services, About, Reviews, Before & After, Style Tips, Shop My Looks, Contact. Consistent palette. These are among the first things a profile visitor sees.

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit (colours + fonts saved).

✓ Day 2: Update Instagram bio, profile photo, and create 8 highlight covers using

Template 8.

✓ Day 3: Create 5 Pinterest boards with branded covers using Template 5. Update

Pinterest bio with keywords.

✓ Day 4: Create your master carousel template using Template 2. Apply to your next

style tips post.

✓ Day 5: Create 3 testimonial graphics using Template 4. Schedule one per week for

the next 3 weeks.

✓ Day 6: Create a TikTok cover template using Template 7 — apply to your next 5

TikTok uploads.

✓ Day 7: Update your website, email signature, and any print materials with your

palette.

✓ [Consistency across Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok, and email creates a premium

impression that justifies premium pricing.]
