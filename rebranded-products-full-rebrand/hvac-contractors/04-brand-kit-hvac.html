BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build an HVAC brand that homeowners trust before they ever call. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Google Business Profile guide • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: POSITIONING

HVAC is a trust business. Homeowners are inviting you into their homes, often during a stressful breakdown, and handing over thousands of dollars. Your brand — your truck, your uniform, your website, your Google profile — communicates whether you're

someone worth trusting before they ever speak to you. Your Brand Promise We help [homeowners / property managers / local businesses] in [City/area] [stay comfortable year-round / avoid system breakdowns / maximize the life of their

HVAC investment] through [honest, reliable, expert service with no surprises]. Example: 'We keep [City] families comfortable year-round — with expert HVAC service, honest pricing, and a guarantee that the job is done right the first time.' Service Positioning Angles

• Residential comfort and reliability: 'Your family deserves to be comfortable —

whatever the weather outside'

• Emergency service: 'When your system fails, we answer the phone and show up

fast'

• Energy efficiency: 'Lower energy bills without replacing your system — expert

efficiency upgrades'

• Service agreements: 'Prevent the breakdown before it happens — proactive

maintenance saves you money'

• New construction: 'Reliable HVAC for homebuilders who can't afford callbacks'

• Commercial: 'Keeping your business comfortable so your team can focus on

what matters'

Magnetic or standard — bold and legible The Door Hanger Play

• After every job, leave door hangers on the 5-6 nearest neighbors

• Message: 'We just serviced your neighbor at [street/neighborhood] — if you

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Days 1-2 — Foundation

✓ Choose color palette and add to Canva Brand Kit

✓ Select fonts (Montserrat + Open Sans)

✓ Write your Brand Promise

✓ Define your primary service positioning angle

Days 3-4 — Build Templates & Update Profiles

✓ Create GBP/Facebook post template with job photo frame

✓ Build seasonal campaign flyer (spring/fall tune-up)

✓ Update Google Business Profile — photos, description, services, hours

✓ Update Facebook page cover photo and description

✓ Design door hanger template

Days 5-7 — First Week of Content

✓ Batch-create 3 posts using the 90-Day Calendar

✓ Generate captions with the monthly AI prompt

✓ Post one job photo to GBP with a keyword-rich caption

✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri and engage with every comment

The best HVAC brand isn't the loudest. It's the most trusted. Show up consistently. Do the job right. Ask for the review. Repeat.
