SECTION 2: MEMBERSHIP CONVERSION

SCRIPTS

In-Person Membership Introduction

SCRIPT — INTRODUCING MEMBERSHIP AFTER A SESSION

YOU: Before you head off — can I mention something quickly?

CLIENT: Sure.

YOU: I've noticed [specific observation — e.g., how much tension you carry in your neck and shoulders] and I think consistency would make a significant difference for you. YOU: We have a membership option that makes regular sessions really easy — $

[amount] a month, covers [X] sessions, and you get priority booking so you're never waiting. YOU: A lot of my regular clients say it's the best thing they've done for their wellbeing — not just the savings, but having it locked in means they actually stick to it.

YOU: Want me to tell you more about how it works? [Frame it as a wellness recommendation from a professional, not a sales offer.] Objection: Can't Commit to Every Month

SCRIPT — FLEXIBILITY OBJECTION

YOU: That's fair — and the membership is flexible enough to work around that. Sessions roll over for [X months] so if you travel or life gets busy, you won't lose them. YOU: The main benefit is the savings and the priority booking — you're locking in the

current rate and always having access to a time on my schedule. [Know your terms before this conversation. Flexibility is a genuine selling point — lead with it.] Objection: A Bit Expensive

SCRIPT — PRICE OBJECTION

YOU: I understand — it is an investment. Can I put it in context quickly? YOU: An individual session is $[amount]. The membership works out to $[per- session cost] per session — you're saving $[X] every time you come in. Over a year of monthly visits, that's $[total savings].
