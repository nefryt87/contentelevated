PLUMBING CONTRACTOR

BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a plumbing brand that homeowners trust — and call first. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Nextdoor & GBP strategy • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: POSITIONING

Homeowners don't choose plumbers on price. They choose on trust — whoever they've heard of, whoever their neighbor recommended, whoever shows up when they search 'plumber near me' and has 80 reviews. Your brand is your trust infrastructure. Build it

consistently and your phone rings first. Your Brand Promise We help [homeowners / landlords / property managers] in [City/area] [fix plumbing problems fast / prevent plumbing emergencies / maintain their plumbing for less

than emergency repairs cost] — with honest pricing, licensed work, and a guarantee that we leave the place cleaner than we found it. Example: 'We keep [City] homes running smoothly — with licensed plumbing,

honest pricing, and a crew that shows up on time and cleans up after itself.' Service Positioning Angles

• Emergency response: 'When your pipe bursts at 2am, we answer the phone'

• Preventive maintenance: 'The annual checkup that prevents the $3,000

emergency'

• Water heater experts: 'Water heater installation, same day, done right,

guaranteed'

• Drain specialists: 'We clear it once and show you why it happened'

• Remodels and upgrades: 'From dripping faucet to full bathroom plumbing — we

do it all'

• Commercial: 'Reliable plumbing for property managers who can't afford surprises'

The Nextdoor Strategy

• Create a business profile on Nextdoor — it's free and underused by most

• When homeowners post asking for plumber recommendations, your profile and

reviews appear

• Answer genuine plumbing questions in neighborhood groups without selling —

this builds trust organically

• One Nextdoor recommendation from a real neighbor converts at 3-5x the rate of

a Google ad

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Days 1-2 — Foundation

✓ Choose color palette and add to Canva Brand Kit

✓ Select fonts (Montserrat + Open Sans)

✓ Write your Brand Promise

✓ Define your primary service positioning angle

Days 3-4 — Build Templates & Update Profiles

✓ Create GBP/Facebook post template with before/after job frame

✓ Build seasonal campaign flyer (next upcoming season)

✓ Update Google Business Profile — photos, description, services, hours, service

area

✓ Update Facebook page — cover photo, about section, services

✓ Create Nextdoor business profile if not already active

✓ Design door hanger template

Days 5-7 — First Week of Content

✓ Batch-create 3 posts using the 90-Day Calendar

✓ Generate captions with the monthly AI caption prompt

✓ Post one job photo to GBP with a keyword-rich caption today

✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri content

✓ Engage with every comment in the first hour after posting

The most trusted plumber in your market wins — not the cheapest. Show up consistently. Do great work. Ask for the review. Repeat forever.
