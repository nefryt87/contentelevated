WEEK 4: DIRECT SHOP CTA

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram 'How to order from [Shop Name]' — demystify the Etsy process for people who've never bought handmade before. Wed Pinterest Gift guide pin: 'Gifts for [specific recipient — e.g., mum / teacher / best friend / new homeowner]' — high gift search traffic.

Fri Facebook Direct shop link post: 'If you've been meaning to visit our Etsy shop — here is what is new this month.' + link.

MONTH 2: PRODUCT EDUCATION & VALUE

Write 8 Instagram captions for an Etsy seller. Topics: how to care for your products, what makes handmade different from mass-produced, the materials you use and

why, a custom or personalised order story, your packaging and unboxing

experience, answering a common customer question, a product comparison or buyer's guide, a seasonal or occasion CTA. Under 150 words each.

WEEK 5: PRODUCT EDUCATION

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram 'How to care for your [product]' — practical tips that show expertise and help customers get the most out of their purchase. Wed Pinterest Care guide pin: '[Product] care tips' — evergreen content that saves well and builds trust.

Fri Instagram 'What makes handmade [product] different from a high-street alternative' — makes the value case clearly and specifically.

WEEK 6: MATERIALS & CRAFT

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram The materials you use — where they come from, why you chose them, what they contribute to the finished piece. Wed Pinterest Material spotlight pin: '[Material] jewellery / home decor / stationery' — searches by material are common on Pinterest.

Fri Instagram Custom order story: describe a recent personalised order (anonymised) — the brief, the process, the reaction.
