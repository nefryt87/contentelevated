WHY YOUR BRAND MAKES BUYERS CHOOSE

YOU OVER 7 MILLION OTHER SELLERS

On Etsy, buyers frequently find multiple listings for similar products. Your brand — your shop banner, your product photography style, your social media aesthetic — is what makes them choose you. A cohesive, distinctive visual identity communicates

professionalism, care, and quality before a buyer reads a single word of your listing. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for Etsy sellers.

Template 1: Etsy Shop Banner

Search 'Etsy shop banner template' or 'online store banner'. 3360×840px (Etsy shop banner size). Your shop name in your heading font, a brief tagline describing what you sell, a beautiful product photo or lifestyle image as background. Update seasonally — a

fresh banner signals an active, current shop.

Template 2: Product Flat Lay Post

Search 'product flat lay Instagram' or 'handmade product post'. Your product styled beautifully on a clean background (white, linen, marble, wood — whatever suits your aesthetic). Your shop name in small type, bottom corner. No cluttered backgrounds —

the product is the hero. Create 10+ of these for your core products.

Template 3: Gift Guide Carousel

Search 'gift guide Instagram carousel' or 'product collection slideshow'. Cover slide: '[Occasion] Gift Guide from [Shop Name]'. 1 product per slide: product name, price, brief benefit. Final slide: 'Shop at [Etsy link]'. Create one for every major gift occasion —

Christmas, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, birthdays. These save at very high rates.

Template 4: Behind-the-Scenes Story

Search 'process story Instagram' or 'maker behind the scenes'. Your hands at work, materials laid out, work in progress. Brief text overlay: 'Making your [product] with love.' or 'This week in the studio.' Your shop name. Post to Instagram stories 3-5x per week

— this humanises your brand more than any other content.

Template 5: Pinterest Product Pin

Search 'Pinterest product pin template' or 'Etsy product Pinterest'. Tall format (2:3 ratio — 1000×1500px). Product photo at top, product name and your shop name below, price optional, CTA: 'Shop at [Etsy link]'. This is the standard Pinterest pin format — create

one for every product and pin to relevant boards.

Template 6: Sale / Promotion Announcement

Search 'sale announcement Instagram' or 'discount post template'. Bold, eye-catching. '[X]% OFF' in large text. Brief details of what's on sale and for how long. Shop name and link. Use your accent colour (Amber or Terracotta) heavily for these — high contrast drives attention.

Template 7: Customer Review Graphic

Search 'customer review Instagram' or 'testimonial post handmade'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a 5-star review. Customer's first name or just '5-star review'. Your shop name. These are incredibly powerful social proof — schedule one

per week. Buyers read other buyers' words more than any other content.

Template 8: Packaging Reveal / Unboxing Post

Search 'product packaging Instagram' or 'unboxing post template'. Show your packaging beautifully — tissue paper, stickers, ribbon, thank-you card. Your shop name in corner. 'Every order packed with love.' These build perceived value and make buyers

excited about the full experience of ordering from you.

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit. Order any packaging

materials that need to match (ribbon, tissue, stickers).

✓ Day 2: Update your Etsy shop banner using Template 1. Update your shop logo if

applicable.

✓ Day 3: Update your Instagram bio, profile photo, and create 3 Instagram highlight

covers: Shop, Behind the Scenes, Reviews.

✓ Day 4: Create 10 product flat lay post overlays using Template 2 — apply to your 10

best product photos.

✓ Day 5: Set up or refresh your Pinterest profile. Create 5 boards and pin 10 products

to each with keyword-rich descriptions.

✓ Day 6: Create 5 customer review graphics using Template 7 — schedule one per

week for 5 weeks.

✓ Day 7: Design your packaging thank-you card / insert using your palette. Get it

printed at [local print shop / Moo / Vistaprint].

✓ [A consistent brand across your Etsy shop, Instagram, and Pinterest is the visual

shorthand that tells buyers they are dealing with a professional, established seller who cares about every detail.]
