How to Use These Templates

Each template below is ready to copy and paste directly into a text message. Replace anything in [brackets] with your specific details. The more personal you make it, the better it performs.

One rule: only send these if you mean them. These texts work because they feel personal — not automated. Take 10 seconds to add a detail specific to that client before you hit send.

Template 1 — 4-Week Reminder (Color Client)

When to send: Send this 4 weeks after a color appointment. Works for balayage, highlights, root touch-ups, any color service. Hey [name]! It's been about a month since your last color — just wanted to check in

and see how it's holding up. I have some openings coming up if you're thinking about coming in. Just reply and I'll send you some times! Pro tip: Keep it casual. Don't say 'your roots are showing' — say 'how's it holding up.' Let them come to that

conclusion themselves.

Template 2 — 4-Week Reminder (Cut Client)
