How to Use This Library

Every prompt in this library is written specifically for hair salons and beauty businesses. Brackets like [THIS] indicate fields you fill in with your specific details. The more specific you are, the better the output you'll get from ChatGPT or any AI tool.

Bookmark this document and come back to it whenever you need to produce something quickly. Each prompt is a starting point — feel free to adapt the language to match your voice. 1 Copy the prompt

Select the prompt text, copy it, and paste it directly into ChatGPT (chat.openai.com) or your preferred AI tool. 2 Fill in the brackets Replace every [BRACKETED FIELD] with your specific details before hitting send. The more detail, the better. 3 Refine if needed

If the first output isn't quite right, don't start over. Just reply: 'Make it shorter,' 'More casual,' or 'Try a different angle.' 4 Save your best outputs Keep a running document of AI outputs you love — these become your template library over time.

Prompt #3 — Promotional Post (Non-Salesy)

Write a social post promoting [SERVICE] at my salon. Make it feel like a helpful recommendation rather than a sales pitch. Lead with who the service is best for and what problem it solves. End with a soft CTA. Tone: warm and genuine. Under 120 words.

Prompt #4 — Reel/TikTok Script

Write a 30-45 second TikTok/Instagram Reel script for a hair stylist about [TOPIC]. Include: a hook in the first 2 seconds (text overlay or spoken), the main content in 3-4 punchy points, and a call to action at the end. Format as a spoken script with timing notes.

Prompt #5 — Engagement Post

Write an Instagram engagement post that asks my salon followers a question about [HAIR TOPIC/DECISION]. The question should be easy to answer (multiple choice or one-word), feel genuinely curious rather than forced, and hint that I'll respond to everyone. Under 80 words.

Prompt #6 — Story Sequence (5 Slides)

Write a 5-slide Instagram Story sequence promoting [SERVICE/OFFER]. Slide 1: hook/problem. Slide 2: agitate the problem. Slide 3: introduce the solution. Slide 4: proof or details. Slide 5: CTA with link. Each slide: 1-2 sentences max.
