5 Free AI Prompts for Childcare Professionals Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Family Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional response to a family inquiry for my nanny services. The family [Name] reached out about [role type]. Details shared: [paste key details]. My experience: [brief]. I want to: (1) make them feel immediately at ease that I am warm, experienced,

and trustworthy, (2) briefly describe my approach to childcare, (3) ask 2-3 questions to understand the role, (4) propose a call or meeting. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name].

PROMPT 2: Post-Interview Follow-Up

Write a post-interview follow-up message to a family I just interviewed with. Family [Name], interview was on [date]. Key things that resonated: [specific things you loved about the role or family]. I want to: (1) thank them warmly, (2) express specific

enthusiasm for the role and why it feels like a great fit, (3) mention my availability and readiness to move forward. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name].

PROMPT 3: End of Day Update Message

Write a daily end-of-day update message from a nanny to parents. Children: [names and ages]. Day summary: [what we did, meals, naps, outdoor time, any developmental moments]. I want this to: (1) give parents a genuine picture of their child's day, (2) highlight one

specific sweet or developmental moment, (3) mention any practical notes, (4) be warm and personal. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name].

PROMPT 4: Nanny Profile Bio

Write a professional nanny profile bio for a childcare platform or CV. My experience: [years, age groups]. My qualifications: [certifications]. My approach: [philosophy in 2-3 sentences]. I want the bio to: (1) feel warm and genuine, (2) communicate professional

competence, (3) tell parents specifically why I would be a wonderful fit for their family. Under 300 words.
