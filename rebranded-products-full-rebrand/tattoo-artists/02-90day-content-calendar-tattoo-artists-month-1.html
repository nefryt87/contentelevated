TATTOO ARTIST

90-Day Content Calendar 12 weeks of Mon/Wed/Fri content for Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest — with monthly AI caption prompts

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

Instagram is your primary booking platform — it's where clients discover you, vet your portfolio, and DM to inquire. TikTok is your fastest reach growth channel and attracts a younger audience actively considering their first or next tattoo. Pinterest drives long-tail

SEO discovery — 'blackwork forearm tattoo ideas' and similar searches. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly.

MONTH 1: PORTFOLIO & ARTISTRY

Write 8 Instagram captions for a tattoo artist with a [style] aesthetic. Topics: a piece you're most proud of recently, your journey into tattooing, your speciality and who you work best with, a piece that challenged you and what you learned, your studio

environment, your consultation process, aftercare advice, a direct booking CTA. Under 150 words each, specific and genuine.

WEEK 1: YOUR ARTISTRY & STORY

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram A recent piece you're proud of: describe the design, the client's vision, and what made it satisfying to create. Wed TikTok 'How I got into tattooing' — your origin story. Humanises you, builds parasocial connection.

Fri Pinterest Portfolio pin: '[Your style] tattoo on [placement]' — keyword-rich, pin to your style-specific board.

WEEK 2: STYLE & SPECIALITY

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram Your speciality: what makes your style distinct, what draws you to it, the type of designs that make you excited. Wed Instagram Process post: stencil application, or a mid-session photo with a brief note on what's happening and why.

Fri TikTok 'Types of tattoo styles explained' or 'what is [your style] tattooing' — educational, positions your expertise.
