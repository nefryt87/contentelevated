INTERIOR DESIGNER

5 Free AI Prompts to Win More Clients Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30–60 minutes of writing and produces copy in your voice — not generic AI filler.

PROMPT 1: Same-Day Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional inquiry response email for my interior design studio. The potential client [Name] reached out about [project type — e.g., full home redesign / kitchen renovation / new build furnishing]. They mentioned: [paste 2-3 key details from

their inquiry]. My design style is [your style — e.g., warm minimalism / eclectic maximalism / transitional]. I want to: (1) acknowledge their specific project, (2) briefly describe how I'd approach it, (3) ask one qualifying question about their timeline and

investment range, (4) invite them to a 20-minute discovery call. Warm and confident. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 2: Monthly Realtor Value Touch

Write a monthly value email from an interior designer to a realtor referral partner named [Name] at [Brokerage]. Share: (1) one interior design or staging insight relevant to the current market, (2) one trend their buyers or sellers should know about. Professional,

genuinely useful, under 150 words. It should feel like useful information from a knowledgeable colleague — not a marketing email. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 3: Post-Discovery Call Follow-Up

Write a post-discovery call follow-up email for my interior design studio. I just spoke with [Name] about their [project type]. Key things they shared: [list 3-4 things — goals, concerns, lifestyle, timeline]. I'm going to propose [scope description] at [investment

range or flat fee]. Before the proposal lands, I want this email to: (1) mirror back their stated goals, (2) briefly explain my process and why it produces better outcomes than DIY or a cheaper option, (3) set the expectation that my proposal will arrive in [timeframe].

Warm and confident. Under 250 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 4: Batch Instagram Captions (5 at once)

Write 5 Instagram captions for an interior designer with a [style — e.g., warm minimalist / maximalist / transitional] aesthetic. Topics: (1) the most common furniture layout mistake clients make before hiring a designer, (2) how to choose a rug size for a living

room (give the actual rule), (3) what 'timeless design' actually means vs. what's just trendy, (4) a behind-the-scenes post about the sourcing process, (5) a before/after reveal framing. Each caption should be educational, specific, and end with a soft CTA or

question. Under 150 words each. Studio name: [Studio Name].

PROMPT 5: Post-Project Review Request

Write a post-project review request email for my interior design studio. Client [Name] just completed their [project type]. The project resulted in [2-3 key outcomes — e.g., 'a complete transformation of your main living area', 'a kitchen that finally functions for

how your family actually lives']. I want to: (1) acknowledge what we accomplished together, (2) make a personal and genuine ask for a Google / Houzz review, (3) give them the direct link. Warm and non-transactional. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].
