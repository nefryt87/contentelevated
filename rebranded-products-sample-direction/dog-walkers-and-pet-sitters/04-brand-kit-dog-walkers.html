You Ever Meet The Dog

Pet owners choosing a dog walker are making one of the most trust-intensive decisions they make. Before they DM or call, they look at your Instagram, your Google profile, your website. A professional, warm, and consistent visual brand communicates that you are organised, reliable, and genuinely care — before a single conversation. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for dog walkers and pet sitters.

Action System

Search 'pet Instagram post template' or 'dog photo frame'. A photo of a happy dog on the walk fills the frame. Your business name small at the bottom. Minimal overlay. The dog is the content — your brand just needs to be consistently present. Post one of these every single day. No other content type builds a following faster for a dog walker.

Template 2: Walk Update Card

Search 'pet update card template' or 'daily update Instagram'. Design a branded 'Walk Update' card: your logo, a space for the dog's name, 'Today we...' and a few icons (route, duration, happy meter). Send as a story or message after walks. Clients love these and share them — they become your best organic marketing.

Template 4: Service & Pricing Card

Search 'pet services menu' or 'dog walker pricing Instagram'. Clean layout: your services, brief descriptions, prices. Your business name and contact at the bottom. Pin it as your first Instagram post. Add to your GBP profile. Eliminates the most common first message ('how much do you charge?').

Template 5: Instagram Story — Availability Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'pet sitter story template'. Vertical. A happy dog photo or park background. Text: 'NEW SLOTS AVAILABLE in [Area]' + 'DM to book your free meet-and-greet.' Your business name. Post every time you open new availability — generates immediate inquiries.
