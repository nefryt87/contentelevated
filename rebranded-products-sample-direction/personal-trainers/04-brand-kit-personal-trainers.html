Section 1: Brand Positioning

The most successful personal trainers aren't the most credentialed — they're the most clearly positioned. When someone visits your profile, they should immediately know who you help, what result you deliver, and why you're different. Your Brand Promise I help [specific person — e.g., busy moms / men over 40 / new runners / postpartum women] [achieve specific result — e.g., lose their first 20 lbs / build functional strength / run their first half marathon] without [biggest fear — e.g., spending hours in the gym / giving up the foods they love / getting injured]. Example: 'I help busy moms over 35 get strong and lose the baby weight — without crash diets or 2-hour workouts.'
