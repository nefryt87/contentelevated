Script — Contraindication Identified

PRACTITIONER: I appreciate you sharing that with me. [Medication / condition] is something I want to approach carefully because it can affect how we proceed safely. PRACTITIONER: Here's what I'd recommend: [Option A — adjust treatment plan / Option B — wait X weeks / Option C — get physician clearance first]. PRACTITIONER: I want to make sure we're doing this in a way that's right for your health, not just your timeline. Does that make sense? [Never dismiss a contraindication to close a booking. Script — Pregnancy Contraindication

PRACTITIONER: I'm so happy for you — and I want to take the best possible care of both of you. PRACTITIONER: [Treatment name] is something we hold off on during pregnancy — it's a standard precaution across the industry. It's not about a specific risk; it's about being appropriately cautious during such an important time. PRACTITIONER: What we absolutely can do during pregnancy: [list pregnancy-safe services — e.g., facials, brow/lash services, skincare consultations]. PRACTITIONER: And I'd love to get you in for [treatment] after delivery — I'll make sure you're first on my list. [Always offer an alternative. Never make the client feel turned away — make her feel protected and valued.]
