Section 2: Patch Test Protocol

When to Patch Test ✓ ALL new clients — before their first ever lash extension appointment ✓ Clients who have not had lash extensions in more than 6 months ✓ Clients who have had a recent change in medication or health status ✓ Clients with a known history of sensitive skin or allergies ✓ Clients switching to a new adhesive type ✓ [Patch testing protects your clients and protects your business. A sensitivity reaction after a full set is preventable — a patch test takes 15 minutes.] Patch Test Process ✓ Step 1: Apply 5-10 lash extensions to the outer corner of one eye ✓ Step 2: Document the date, adhesive brand/batch number, and client name in your records ✓ Step 3: Advise the client to monitor for redness, itching, swelling, or irritation over the next 24-48 hours ✓ Step 4: Ask them to contact you immediately if they notice any reaction ✓ Step 5: If no reaction occurs: proceed with the full appointment ✓ Step 6: If a reaction occurs: remove extensions, do not rebook
