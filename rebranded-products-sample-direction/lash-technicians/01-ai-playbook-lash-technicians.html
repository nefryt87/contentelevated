The AI Playbook for Lash Technicians Fill your books with returning clients, reduce no-shows, upsell brow and lash treatments, and build a personal brand that keeps your DMs full

02

Working page

Introduction

The lash technicians with fully booked calendars and repeat clients aren't just the most technically skilled. They follow up after every appointment to check retention and client satisfaction. They reach out at the exact right moment — 2-3 weeks for classic fills, 3-4 weeks for volume — before clients have time to look elsewhere. They educate their clients on aftercare in a way that produces better retention and happier long-term customers. They run flash promotions and seasonal campaigns that fill quiet periods. And they post consistently on Instagram with before/afters and lash education content that drives DMs. This playbook covers every high-leverage scenario for lash technicians and combined lash/brow specialists — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

Conversion

The DM & Inquiry Response System Most lash clients discover their tech on Instagram and make first contact via DM. Your response speed and tone sets the tone for the entire client relationship. A warm, specific, informative response converts dramatically better than a generic one.
