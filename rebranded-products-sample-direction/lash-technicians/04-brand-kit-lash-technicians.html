Action System

Search 'before after beauty Instagram' or 'eye transformation post'. Two-panel: bare eye on left, lashed eye on right. Minimal text: your studio name and the lash style used. This is your most important content type — it proves results instantly. Create one for every style you offer (classic, hybrid, volume, lift) and rotate them throughout your content calendar.

02

Working page

Template 2: Lash Style Comparison Carousel

Search 'comparison carousel Instagram' or 'beauty guide slides'. 4-5 slides comparing classic vs. hybrid vs. volume lashes. Each slide: style name, brief description, who it suits, a relevant photo. Final slide: your booking CTA. These save at extremely high rates — potential clients use them to decide which style to book.

03

Working page

Template 3: Lash Education Graphic

Search 'eye tutorial graphic' or 'beauty education Instagram post'. A single educational fact or tip about lash care. 'Oily skin and lash retention: here is what I do differently.' Clean, informative, easy to read at a glance. Share regularly — these build authority and trust over time.

04

Working page

Template 4: Instagram Story — Availability Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'beauty appointment story'. Vertical. A soft lash close-up photo as background. Dark or light overlay panel. 'LASH FILLS AVAILABLE [MONTH]'. Your DM or booking link. Post every time you open new fill slots — at least weekly.

05

Working page

Template 5: Testimonial Graphic

Search 'client review beauty' or 'testimonial quote lash'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a review — something about their morning routine changing, their confidence, or their reaction to seeing the results. First name below. Your studio name. Schedule one per week.

06

Working page

Template 6: Tiktok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'beauty TikTok thumbnail' or 'Reel first frame template'. For the first frame of every video: your hook text in large font ('I tried mega volume for the first time', 'The worst lash aftercare mistake'), your studio name small at bottom, your brand colour as background. This determines click-through — make it irresistible.

Template 7: Lash Aftercare Card

Search 'beauty aftercare card' or 'client card template'. Design a digital (and optionally printable) aftercare reference card: the do's and don'ts for the first 24-48 hours, ongoing care, what's normal to expect. Share via email and stories after every appointment. Clients who follow aftercare have better retention and better outcomes.
