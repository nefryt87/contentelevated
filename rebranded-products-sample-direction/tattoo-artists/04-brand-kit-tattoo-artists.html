Clients choose tattoo artists based on Instagram before they ever reach out. Your brand — your colour palette, typography, how your feed looks — communicates who your ideal client is. Template 2: Flash Day Announcement Graphic

Search 'flash sale announcement' or 'event promotion Instagram'. Bold, high-impact layout. 'FLASH DAY' in your largest heading font. Date, time, and booking instructions. A preview of 1-2 designs. Your studio name. Post this to feed and stories 5-7 days before your flash day and again the morning of.

Working page

Template 3: Instagram Story — Booking Open

Search 'availability announcement story' or 'booking open Instagram story'. Vertical. Dark background. Bold text: 'BOOKING [MONTH]' or 'NOW TAKING APPOINTMENTS'. Dates available. Your name. Link sticker to your booking form. Post to stories every time you open a new booking window.

Working page

Template 4: Tiktok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'TikTok thumbnail template' or 'Reel cover graphic'. For the first frame of every video: your key message in large text (e.g., 'Tattoo time-lapse', 'Day in my studio', 'Flash designs — which one?'). Your studio name small at bottom. Brand colour background. Viewers decide whether to watch based on this frame.
