Pre-Discovery Call Questionnaire ✓ What is your #1 goal or desired outcome right now? ✓ What is getting in the way of achieving it? ✓ On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is solving this? What is at stake if nothing changes? ✓ What have you already tried? What worked, and what didn't? ✓ What does success look like to you 6 months from now — specifically? ✓ Have you worked with a coach before? Hi [First Name], I've been thinking about you and I want to check in directly. Over the last [timeframe] I've noticed we haven't been making the same progress or your commitments from sessions haven't been translating to action. I could be reading it wrong — but if I'm not, I'd rather name it than work around it. Resistance and stuckness are normal parts of any growth process. But I want to understand what's actually in the way for you right now — not just on the surface, but underneath. Can we have a real conversation about this at our next session? I'm not going anywhere — but I want to make sure we're working on the right thing. [Your Name] When a Client Wants to Quit Prematurely

02

Working page

Subject Before You Decide — A Personal Ask, [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I hear you — and I want to respect your decision. Before you finalize it, I'd ask for one thing: a 20-minute call, just the two of us. Not to talk you into anything. Not to sell you on continuing. Just to understand what's driving this — because in my experience, the impulse to stop almost always happens right before a breakthrough, not after. If after our call you still want to stop, I'll support that fully. But I'd feel I wasn't doing my job if I let you go without one real conversation. Would [day] work? [Your Name] [Never guilt or pressure. Offer the conversation, then let them decide.]
