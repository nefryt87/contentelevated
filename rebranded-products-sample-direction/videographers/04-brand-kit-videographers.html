Your Reel Does

Clients vet a videographer's brand before they ever watch a frame of footage. A cohesive, intentional visual identity communicates taste, professionalism, and a level of craft that justifies premium pricing. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for videographers working across wedding, corporate, and commercial markets.

Template 2: Client Testimonial Graphic

Template 3: Instagram Story — Inquiry Cta

Search 'Instagram story video booking' or 'cinematographer story template'. Vertical. A beautiful video still as background. Dark overlay. Text: 'Now booking [season] weddings' or 'Taking on new corporate clients'. Your studio name. Link sticker. Post to stories weekly during peak inquiry season.

Template 4: Youtube Thumbnail

Search 'YouTube thumbnail cinematic' or 'video thumbnail template'. 1280×720px. A strong emotional still from your film, the title in large readable text, your logo small in a corner. YouTube thumbnails are your most important piece of graphic design — they determine whether people click your films.

Template 5: Linkedin Professional Banner

Search 'LinkedIn banner videographer' or 'video production LinkedIn cover'. 1584×396px. Your best commercial film still, your studio name, your speciality, your city. This is seen by every business contact and potential corporate client who visits your profile.

Template 6: Package Comparison Graphic

Search 'pricing table comparison' or 'service package table'. Three columns, your three packages. Each column: package name, key inclusions as bullet points, price. Your studio name at top. Use this in proposals, on your website, and in Instagram carousels. Removes price uncertainty before a consultation.
