The AI Playbook for Videographers Convert more inquiries, close premium packages, build vendor partnerships, and create a client experience that generates referrals — for wedding, corporate, and commercial work

02

Working page

Introduction

The videographers booking premium projects consistently aren't just the best behind the lens. They respond to inquiries the same day with copy that makes a client feel understood. They follow up on ghosted leads. They maintain photographer and planner relationships that fill their calendar without advertising. They present their packages with confidence. And they deliver films with an experience that turns clients into raving referrers. This playbook covers every high-leverage scenario — wedding, corporate, and commercial — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

03

Working page

Conversion

The Same-Day Response System Clients booking videographers — especially for weddings — often contact 3-5 studios simultaneously. The first professional, personalised, and enthusiastic response wins a disproportionate number of consultations.

04

Working page

Prompt — Wedding Videography Inquiry Response

Write a warm, enthusiastic inquiry response email for my videography studio. The couple [Names] reached out about wedding videography for their [date] wedding at [venue]. They mentioned: [paste details — style they want, what matters to them, any specific moments they mentioned]. My videography style is [your style — e.g., cinematic documentary / romantic film / editorial]. I want to: (1) make them feel like I genuinely read their inquiry, (2) briefly describe how I'd approach their specific day, (3) mention my packages, (4) invite them to a video call or phone call. Warm, excited, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].
