Section 1: Brand Positioning

Borrowers don't just choose the lowest rate — they choose the lender they trust most to get them to the closing table. Your brand communicates that trust before you ever speak. Your Brand Promise I help [target client — e.g., first-time buyers / move-up buyers / self-employed professionals] [achieve outcome — e.g., get into their first home / access the equity they've built] without [pain point — e.g., the confusion and stress that usually comes with it]. Example: 'I help first-time buyers in [City] get into their home with confidence — no confusing jargon, no surprises at the closing table.'

Action System

• Business Card — Search: 'mortgage loan officer business card' — always include NMLS# and company name • Flyer — Search: 'home loan program flyer' — great for open houses and realtor offices • Rate Sheet — Search: 'financial comparison table Canva' — customize for product comparisons • Email Signature — Search: 'professional email signature banner Canva' — add NMLS# and compliance language Compliance Note Always include your NMLS# on all marketing materials. Review your state licensing requirements for any required disclosures on digital advertising. When in doubt, check with your branch manager or compliance team before publishing.
