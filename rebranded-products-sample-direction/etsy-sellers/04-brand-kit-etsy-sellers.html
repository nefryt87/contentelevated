You Over 7 Million Other Sellers

On Etsy, buyers frequently find multiple listings for similar products. Your brand — your shop banner, your product photography style, your social media aesthetic — is what makes them choose you. A cohesive, distinctive visual identity communicates professionalism, care, and quality before a buyer reads a single word of your listing. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for Etsy sellers.

Action System

Search 'Etsy shop banner template' or 'online store banner'. 3360×840px (Etsy shop banner size). Your shop name in your heading font, a brief tagline describing what you sell, a beautiful product photo or lifestyle image as background. Update seasonally — a fresh banner signals an active, current shop.

02

Working page

Template 2: Product Flat Lay Post

Search 'product flat lay Instagram' or 'handmade product post'. Your product styled beautifully on a clean background (white, linen, marble, wood — whatever suits your aesthetic). Your shop name in small type, bottom corner. No cluttered backgrounds — the product is the hero. Create 10+ of these for your core products.

03

Working page

Template 3: Gift Guide Carousel

Search 'gift guide Instagram carousel' or 'product collection slideshow'. Cover slide: '[Occasion] Gift Guide from [Shop Name]'. 1 product per slide: product name, price, brief benefit. Final slide: 'Shop at [Etsy link]'. Create one for every major gift occasion — Christmas, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, birthdays. These save at very high rates.

04

Working page

Template 4: Behind-The-Scenes Story

Search 'process story Instagram' or 'maker behind the scenes'. Your hands at work, materials laid out, work in progress. Brief text overlay: 'Making your [product] with love.' or 'This week in the studio.' Your shop name. Post to Instagram stories 3-5x per week — this humanises your brand more than any other content.

05

Working page

Template 5: Pinterest Product Pin

Search 'Pinterest product pin template' or 'Etsy product Pinterest'. Tall format (2:3 ratio — 1000×1500px). Product photo at top, product name and your shop name below, price optional, CTA: 'Shop at [Etsy link]'. This is the standard Pinterest pin format — create one for every product and pin to relevant boards.

06

Working page

Template 6: Sale / Promotion Announcement

Search 'sale announcement Instagram' or 'discount post template'. Bold, eye-catching. '[X]% OFF' in large text. Brief details of what's on sale and for how long. Shop name and link. Use your accent colour (Amber or Terracotta) heavily for these — high contrast drives attention.

Template 7: Customer Review Graphic

Search 'customer review Instagram' or 'testimonial post handmade'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a 5-star review. Customer's first name or just '5-star review'. Your shop name. These are incredibly powerful social proof — schedule one per week. Buyers read other buyers' words more than any other content.

02

Working page

Template 8: Packaging Reveal / Unboxing Post

Search 'product packaging Instagram' or 'unboxing post template'. Show your packaging beautifully — tissue paper, stickers, ribbon, thank-you card. Your shop name in corner. 'Every order packed with love.' These build perceived value and make buyers excited about the full experience of ordering from you.
