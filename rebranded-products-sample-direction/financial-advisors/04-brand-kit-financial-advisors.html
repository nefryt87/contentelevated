Section 1: Brand Positioning

Financial advisory is the ultimate trust business. Clients are handing you their life savings, their retirement security, and their family's financial future. Your brand must communicate — before a single word is spoken — that you are credible, honest, and genuinely on their side. Your Advisory Promise I help [specific client — e.g., business owners / families in their 40s–50s / corporate executives] [achieve financial outcome — e.g., build wealth that creates choices / retire with confidence / preserve and transfer wealth to the next generation] by [your approach — e.g., creating a comprehensive financial plan tailored to their life / cutting through complexity with a clear, personalized strategy]. Example: 'I help business owners in [City] build the wealth and the exit strategy that makes their next chapter possible — not just their portfolio, but their whole financial life.' Ideal Client Profile Options • Pre-retirees (55–65) with $500k–$2M investable assets • Business owners planning for exit or succession • Corporate executives with complex equity compensatio
