What Gets You Here

Entry Level / New Nanny $15 — $20 1-2 years experience, basic first aid, comfortable with a single child. Experienced Nanny $20 — $28 3-5 years, first aid certified, experience with multiple ages, strong references. Professional Nanny $28 — $38 5+ years, early childhood qualification or equivalent, stellar references, speciality experience. Specialist / Senior Nanny $38 — $50+ SEN experience, newborn specialist, bilingual, Montessori trained, or nanny manager role. Rates vary by location — major metro areas (NYC, SF, LA, London) command 20- 40% higher rates than national averages. Always research your local market on Care.com, UrbanSitter, and local nanny Facebook groups. Your rate is a floor, not a ceiling — negotiate upward based on your unique value.
