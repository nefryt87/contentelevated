You Shortlisted

Families shortlist nannies based on their profile, their photo, and the first impression of their communication before they ever have a conversation. A polished, warm, and consistent professional presence — on platforms, on LinkedIn, and in your written materials — communicates the same qualities that make a great nanny: care, organisation, warmth, and professionalism. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and template strategies built specifically for nannies and childcare professionals.

Template 2: Nanny Cv / Profile Document

Search 'nanny CV template' or 'childcare professional resume'. Clean, warm layout. Your photo (professional, warm). Your name and contact. Your summary (use your platform bio). Experience (reverse chronological, with ages of children cared for). Qualifications and certifications. References available on request. Keep to 1-2 pages — concise and readable. 03

Working page

Template 3: Credential / Certification Graphic

Search 'certification badge Instagram' or 'professional credentials post'. A clean graphic featuring your key credentials: 'First Aid Certified', 'DBS Cleared', 'Paediatric CPR', '[X] Years Experience'. Your name. Use as a pinned Instagram post, on your LinkedIn, and in your platform profile. Parents scan this type of content quickly — make it visually easy to read.

04

Working page

Template 4: Activity Idea Post

Search 'children activity Instagram' or 'parenting tip post'. A clean, engaging graphic: '[Age] Activity: [Activity Name]' with a brief description. Your name or handle. Post these regularly — parents save them, share them, and remember the professional who consistently provides value. Positions you as an expert, not just a carer.

05

Working page

Template 5: Testimonial / Reference Graphic

Search 'professional testimonial Instagram' or 'nanny review post'. Feature a specific, warm quote from a past family (with their permission). Their first name and children's ages (optional). Your name. These are your most powerful trust signals — post every new reference or testimonial you receive. Parents trust other parents more than any other marketing.

Working page

Template 6: Availability Announcement

Search 'professional available post' or 'nanny available LinkedIn'. Clean graphic: 'NOW AVAILABLE: [Role Type] in [Area] from [Date]'. Your key credentials (experience level, certifications, DBS). Your contact details. Post this on LinkedIn, Instagram, and update your platform profile simultaneously every time you open for new inquiries.
