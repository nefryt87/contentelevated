Template 2: Membership Value Card

Search 'membership card Instagram' or 'subscription offer post'. Clean layout: your membership name, key benefits as bullet points, monthly price, 'SAVE $[X]/MONTH' callout. Your business name and sign-up link. Use your accent colour (blue or yellow) for the price. Post this monthly as a pinned post and update seasonally.

02

Working page

Template 3: Google Business Profile Weekly Post

Search 'local business update post' or 'Google business post template'. Clean, simple. Your service, a CTA, your address. Post every Monday — each GBP post improves your ranking for 'car wash near me'. 10 minutes per week, significant long-term impact. Include a photo of your facility or a clean car every time.

03

Working page

Template 4: Seasonal Promotion Banner

Search 'car wash promotion banner' or 'seasonal offer social media'. Bold, high-visibility. Your offer in the largest font. Dates. Your business name. Use your high-visibility accent colour (Electric Blue or Yellow) for the background. Post to Facebook, Instagram, and GBP simultaneously when running any promotion.

04

Working page

Template 5: 5-Star Review Graphic

Search 'Google review Instagram' or 'customer testimonial local business'. 5 stars at the top. The review text. Reviewer's first name. Your business name and 'Google Review' label. Post every new 5-star review — aim for one per week. Potential customers read these more than any other content type.

05

Working page

Template 6: Staff Spotlight Post

Search 'team member spotlight Instagram' or 'employee of month post'. Employee photo (with consent), their name, their role, one genuine thing about them. Your business name. These humanise your brand and build community connection — customers who recognise staff return more often.

7-Day Brand Launch Checklist
