Chapter 2: Your First 3 AI Wins — This Week

Start here. Three quick wins — each under an hour to implement — that will immediately impact your practice. • Win #1 — Inquiry Response System: Use AI to write a warm, detailed response to new inquiries that answers the most common questions, explains your approach, and invites a discovery call — all in one message. Response time is one of the biggest factors in converting inquiries to clients. • Win #2 — Weekly Client Check-In: AI writes personalized weekly check-in messages for every active client in under 5 minutes. Clients follow plans better when they understand them.

Chapter 3: Filling Your Client Roster With The

Right People A full client roster starts with a clear message about who you help and a system for following up with every inquiry. AI gives you both. • Niche Clarity Messaging: The most successful nutrition practitioners are clearly positioned — weight loss for women over 40, sports nutrition for endurance athletes, gut health and IBS, diabetes management, pediatric nutrition. AI helps you write messaging that speaks directly to your ideal client. • The Discovery Call Sequence: A 3-message sequence from inquiry to booked discovery call — warm response, value-add follow-up, and soft urgency close. Most practitioners follow up once. This system follows up three times without feeling pushy. • Post-Discovery Call Conversion: After a discovery call, AI writes a personalized follow-up that recaps the client's goals, outlines your approach, and proposes the right package — making saying yes feel like the obvious next step.
