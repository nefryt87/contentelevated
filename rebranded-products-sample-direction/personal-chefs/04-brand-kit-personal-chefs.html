Calibre Before Your Food Does

High-value clients hiring a private chef make their first judgement based on your digital presence — your website, your Instagram, your email signature. A polished, cohesive visual identity communicates the same thing as a beautifully plated dish: precision, care, and a standard worth paying for. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for personal chefs and private dining professionals.

Action System

Search 'food photography Instagram post' or 'restaurant food post template'. Your best dish photography fills the frame. Minimal text overlay: your name or studio in small type, bottom corner. Clean and editorial. Food photography is your portfolio — let the image do the work. Create 10 of these with consistent overlay style.

Working page

Template 2: Seasonal Menu Card

Search 'restaurant menu design' or 'dinner menu template'. A beautifully formatted seasonal menu — courses listed elegantly. Your name at top, seasonal notation at bottom. Use Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond for the dish names. Post as an Instagram feed image and story slide when launching a seasonal menu. Pin to Pinterest.

03

Working page

Template 3: Behind-The-Scenes Story Template

Search 'food story template' or 'chef behind the scenes story'. Vertical format. Your photo at a farmers market, in the kitchen, or plating. Brief overlay text: 'Preparing for tonight's private dinner' or 'This morning's market haul.' Your name. Post to stories several times per week — it humanises the experience.
