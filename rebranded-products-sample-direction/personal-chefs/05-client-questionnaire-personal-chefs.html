✓ SECTION 1 — OPEN WITH THE VISION (not the price): ✓ Write 2-3 evocative sentences that paint the picture of the dining experience. Use sensory language. Make the client see, smell, and taste it before they read a single detail. ✓ Example: 'Imagine your guests arriving to the scent of slow-roasted lamb and wild herbs, a table set with candlelight and fresh flowers, and an evening that unfolds as naturally as conversation between old friends.' ✓
