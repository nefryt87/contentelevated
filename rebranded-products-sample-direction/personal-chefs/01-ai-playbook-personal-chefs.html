Introduction

The personal chefs with full client rosters and premium rates aren't just the most technically skilled in the kitchen. They respond to every inquiry professionally and specifically — making potential clients feel understood before they've ever tasted the food. They present proposals that communicate value before they present a number. They maintain relationships with realtors, concierge services, and event planners who send warm referrals year-round. They build a personal brand on Instagram and YouTube that positions them as the expert their ideal client is searching for. And they create a client experience so consistently excellent that every meal becomes a referral. This playbook gives you 7 chapters covering every high-leverage scenario — from private dining to weekly meal prep to corporate catering — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.
