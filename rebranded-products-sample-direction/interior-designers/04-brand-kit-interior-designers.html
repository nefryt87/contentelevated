Palette 1 Typography Pairing

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — elegant serif that communicates luxury and editorial refinement. Use at 28-48pt for Instagram and Pinterest titles. BODY: Jost — clean, geometric sans-serif with warmth. Use at 12-16pt for captions, proposal documents, and email body text. ACCENT: Playfair Display Italic — for pull quotes, testimonials, and moment-of-impact copy.

Action System

Search these terms in Canva's template library. After finding a template you like, replace all colours with your palette and all fonts with your typography pairing. Save as a 'Brand Template' in Canva for future reuse.

02

Working page

Template 1: Before & After Interior Design Post

Search: 'before after interior' or 'room transformation Instagram'. Two-panel layout. Left: before photo. Right: after photo. Headline: '[Room type] Transformation — [City]'. Your logo bottom right. Use Warm Linen or Soft White background for text panels.

03

Working page

Template 2: Project Reveal Carousel

Search: 'interior design carousel' or 'home decor slideshow'. 5-8 slides: cover slide (project name + your logo), 3-5 room photos, final slide with your CTA and contact. Consistent font treatment across all slides.

04

Working page

Template 3: Pinterest Room Mood Board

Search: 'mood board Pinterest' or 'interior design collage'. Grid of 6-9 images: fabric swatches, paint colours, furniture silhouettes, inspiration imagery. Add a title in your heading font. Rich keywords in the pin description for discoverability.

05

Working page

Template 4: Educational Tip Graphic

Search: 'interior design tips Instagram' or 'home decor education post'. Clean layout with a headline question, 3-4 bullet answers, and your studio name. These save/share more than any other post type.

06

Working page

Template 5: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search: 'testimonial quote Instagram' or 'client review post'. Quote in Playfair italic or Libre Baskerville. Client first name and project type below. Subtle background pattern using your accent colour.

07

Working page

Template 6: Linkedin Article Header

Search: 'LinkedIn article banner' or 'professional blog header'. 1200×627px. Clean, architectural feel. Space for a headline and your studio name. Use Charcoal or Deep Forest as background.

08

Working page

Template 7: Pinterest Profile Board Covers

Search: 'Pinterest board cover' or 'Pinterest highlight'. Square format, one per board: 'Client Projects', 'Process & Behind the Scenes', 'Colour Stories', 'Sourcing & Materials', 'Trend Watch'. Consistent across all boards.

7-Day Brand Launch Checklist

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette (Warm Editorial or Modern Monochrome) and install fonts in Canva ✓ Day 2: Update Instagram bio, profile photo, and highlight covers using your palette ✓ Day 3: Update Pinterest profile and create 5 board covers using Template 7 ✓ Day 4: Update LinkedIn banner image and 'About' section to reflect your positioning ✓ Day 5: Create 3 educational tip graphics (Template 4) using your brand colours — schedule for next week ✓ Day 6: Create a Project Reveal carousel template in Canva — save it as your branded master template ✓ Day 7: Update your website contact page and email signature with your brand colours and typography ✓ [Once your brand is set up in Canva, every future post takes 5-10 minutes — not an hour.]
