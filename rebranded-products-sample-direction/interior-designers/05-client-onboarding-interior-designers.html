Script — Presenting Your Fee Confidently

YOU: Before I put together the proposal, I want to have an honest conversation about investment — because it shapes everything we'll do together. YOU: For a project like yours — [brief description of scope] — you're typically looking at a design fee in the range of $[X] to $[X], depending on complexity. YOU: The reason I'm sharing this before the proposal is that I'd rather have this conversation now than send you something that doesn't work. Does that range feel aligned with what you were expecting? [Listen. Don't jump in.
