Prompt — Wedding Floral Inquiry Response

Write a warm, enthusiastic inquiry response email for my floral studio. The bride [Name] reached out about wedding florals for her wedding on [date] at [venue]. She mentioned: [paste details — style she described, flowers she loves, any mood board details, budget indication]. My studio speciality and aesthetic: [your style — e.g., romantic garden / architectural modern / wild meadow / tropical lush]. I want to: (1) make her feel like I genuinely read her inquiry and understand her vision, (2) briefly describe how I'd interpret her style, (3) mention what a consultation looks like, (4) invite her to book a call or consultation. Warm and evocative, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].
