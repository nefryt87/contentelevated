Impression With Every Booking Decision

Event organisers and meeting planners evaluate a speaker's brand before they watch their reel. Your website, your LinkedIn profile, your one-sheet — these communicate your credibility, your positioning, and whether you are the right speaker for their event before they have seen you speak. A polished, cohesive visual identity adds perceived value and justifies premium fees. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for professional speakers.
