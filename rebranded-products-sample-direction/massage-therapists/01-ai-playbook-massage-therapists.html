Chapter 3: Filling Your Schedule Year-Round

A full schedule requires consistent rebooking follow-up, systematic outreach to lapsed clients, and enough local visibility to attract new ones consistently. • The Rebooking System: Every client gets a post-appointment nudge suggesting their optimal return interval. This single habit is the highest ROI action for any massage practice. • Lapsed Client Reactivation: AI writes a warm win-back message for clients who haven't booked in 6–12 weeks — a seasonal offer, new service, or personal check-in. • Package and Membership Offers: AI writes an offer email for massage packages or memberships — presenting the value clearly and making signing up feel like the smart choice. • Seasonal Booking Campaigns: Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, holiday season, New Year wellness — AI writes targeted outreach for every booking peak of the year.
