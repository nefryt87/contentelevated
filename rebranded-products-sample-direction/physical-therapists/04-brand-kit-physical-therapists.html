Section 1: Practice Positioning

Physical therapy is chosen twice — once by the referring physician and once by the patient. Your brand needs to work for both audiences. Physicians want to refer to a competent, communicative, outcomes-focused clinician. Patients want to go somewhere approachable, effective, and worth their time and insurance co-pay. Your brand can speak to both. Your Practice Promise We help [patient type — e.g., active adults / post-surgical patients / athletes / seniors] [recover from or manage — condition type] so they can [return to meaningful activity — e.g., get back to sport / live without chronic pain / move independently again] through [your approach — e.g., evidence-based, hands-on physical therapy / individualized plans of care that go beyond symptom management]. Example: 'We help active adults in [City] recover from injury and get back to the activities they love — through individualized, hands-on physical therapy that treats the root cause, not just the symptoms.'
