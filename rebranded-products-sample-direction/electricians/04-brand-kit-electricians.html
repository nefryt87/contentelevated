Action System

Search 'business update post' or 'service announcement'. Clean layout: your company colours, a bold headline (e.g., 'Now offering EV charger installation in [City]'), 2-3 bullet points, your phone number, and license number. GBP posts improve local search ranking — post weekly.

02

Working page

Template 2: Facebook Job Completion Post

Search 'before after service post' or 'completed job announcement'. Two-column: left side 'The Challenge', right side 'The Result'. Include job type, neighbourhood, your company name. These drive more engagement and enquiries than any other Facebook post type.

03

Working page

Template 3: Educational Safety Graphic

Search 'infographic safety tips' or 'educational checklist post'. '5 Signs Your Panel Needs Upgrading' or 'Home Electrical Safety Checklist'. Clean, readable, shareable. These save and share more than any other content type on Facebook.

04

Working page

Template 4: 5-Star Review Post

Search 'testimonial graphic' or 'review post'. Screenshot your best Google review, clean up the background, add your company logo and colours. Post every new 5-star review you receive. Social proof is your most powerful marketing tool.

05

Working page

Template 5: Truck/Vehicle Signage Template

Search 'vehicle wrap text' or 'truck lettering'. Even if you're designing your own vehicle wrap, Canva lets you visualise the layout: company name, phone number, license number, key services. Your truck is your most-seen billboard — it should match your digital brand.

06

Working page

Template 6: Facebook / Google Banner

Search 'Facebook cover photo business' or 'Google Business header'. Wide format. Your best job photo, company name, tagline ('Licensed. Insured. Reliable.'), phone number. This is the first impression on every platform.

07

Working page

Template 7: Door Hanger / Flyer

Search 'door hanger template' or 'service flyer'. After completing a job in a neighbourhood, leave door hangers on nearby homes: 'We just did electrical work on your street — here's how to reach us.' High-conversion local marketing that costs almost nothing.
