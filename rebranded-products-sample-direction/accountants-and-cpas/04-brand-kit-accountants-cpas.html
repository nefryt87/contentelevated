Your Credentials Do

High-value accounting clients — business owners, executives, high-net-worth individuals — make their shortlist based on first impression before they ever review credentials. A professional, consistent visual identity communicates the same thing as a strong balance sheet: order, trustworthiness, and attention to detail. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva template strategies built specifically for accounting and CPA practices.
