Section 1: Brand Positioning

The most booked MUAs aren't necessarily the most skilled — they're the most clearly positioned and consistently visible. Your brand communicates who you are, who you serve, and why you're the only choice before a client ever reaches out. Your Beauty Brand Promise I help [client type — e.g., brides / women of color / editorial models / prom queens] feel [emotional outcome — e.g., confident / timeless / bold / like the most beautiful version of themselves] through [your approach — e.g., luxurious, long-wear makeup tailored to their features and vision].
