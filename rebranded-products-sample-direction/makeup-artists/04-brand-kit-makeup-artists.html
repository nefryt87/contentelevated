Section 1: Brand Positioning

Customize before use
The most booked MUAs aren't necessarily the most skilled — they're the most clearly positioned and consistently visible. Your brand communicates who you are, who you serve, and why you're the only choice before a client ever reaches out. Your Beauty Brand Promise I help [client type — e.g., brides / women of color / editorial models / prom queens] feel [emotional outcome — e.g., confident / timeless / bold / like the most beautiful version of themselves] through [your approach — e.g., luxurious, long-wear makeup tailored to their features and vision]. Example: 'I help brides in [City] feel effortlessly beautiful on their wedding day — with a look they'll love in photos for the rest of their life.' Niche Options — The More Specific, The More Bookable • Bridal and wedding party makeup • Editorial and fashion/print • Film, TV, and commercial • Special occasion (galas, proms, events) • Mature skin and age-positive beauty • Melanin-rich and textured skin specialist • Airbrush specialist • Natural and clean beauty • Bold, avant-garde, and creative looks • On-location and destination weddings
