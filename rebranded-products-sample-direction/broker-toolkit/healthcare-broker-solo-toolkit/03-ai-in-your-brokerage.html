Renewal Reminder Email Write a friendly, professional email to a small business client reminding them that their group health plan renews in [X weeks]. Their company is [COMPANY NAME] with [# employees]. Mention rates are going up [X]% industry-wide and I'd like to schedule a 20-minute review call to explore options. Tone: warm, not alarmist. Write a plain-English explanation comparing an HMO, PPO, and HDHP for a business owner with [# employees] who are mostly [age range]. Budget is tight. Focus on what matters most to a small employer. Keep it under 200 words.

AI Compliance Guardrails

IMPORTANT: AI tools are assistants, not advisors. Always review AI-generated content before sending. Never input client names, SSNs, health information, or PHI/PII into AI tools unless using a HIPAA-compliant platform with a signed BAA. ! Never input PHI into free AI tools Use generic placeholders like [CLIENT] when drafting. Real names + health data belong in your secure CRM only. ! Always review AI-generated advice AI can be confidently wrong about carrier rules, state regs, or ACA specifics. Use it to draft — not to advise. ! Keep records of all client communications Don't rely on AI chat history as your compliance record.
