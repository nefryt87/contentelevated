AI Compliance Guardrails

IMPORTANT: AI tools are assistants, not advisors. Always review AI-generated content before sending. Never input client names, SSNs, health information, or PHI/PII into AI tools unless using a HIPAA-compliant platform with a signed BAA. ! Never input PHI into free AI tools Use generic placeholders like [CLIENT] when drafting. Real names + health data belong in your secure CRM only. ! Always review AI-generated advice AI can be confidently wrong about carrier rules, state regs, or ACA specifics. Use it to draft — not to advise. !
