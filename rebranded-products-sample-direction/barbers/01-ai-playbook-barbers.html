Working page

Content That Builds A Chair Full Of Fans

TikTok and Instagram are the most powerful booking tools a barber has. The accounts with full books post consistently across 4 pillars: transformations (before/after cuts), process (the technique, the tools), education (tips for clients, style guides), and personality (your vibe, your shop culture, your story). Clients book the barber they feel like they already know.

Prompt — Tiktok / Reel Concept Ideas

Write 5 TikTok or Instagram Reel concept ideas for a barber. Each should have: (1) a scroll-stopping hook for the first 2 seconds, (2) a brief description of the content, (3) a caption. Topics: a transformation video (before/after), a technique breakdown, a 'what to tell your barber' guide, a day-in-the-life at the barbershop, a style guide for a specific hair type or face shape. Under 80 words each. Barbershop name: [Barbershop Name].
