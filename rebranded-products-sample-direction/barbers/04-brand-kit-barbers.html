Choose Your Chair

Clients choose a barber on TikTok and Instagram before they ever step through the door. Your brand — the way your profile looks, your aesthetic, your visual consistency — communicates your skill level, your vibe, and whether you are the right barber for them. A distinctive, confident brand builds a following that walks through your door. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for barbers and barbershops.

Template 1: Before/After Transformation Post
