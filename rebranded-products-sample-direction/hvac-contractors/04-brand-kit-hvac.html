Section 1: Positioning

HVAC is a trust business. Homeowners are inviting you into their homes, often during a stressful breakdown, and handing over thousands of dollars. Your brand — your truck, your uniform, your website, your Google profile — communicates whether you're someone worth trusting before they ever speak to you. Your Brand Promise We help [homeowners / property managers / local businesses] in [City/area] [stay comfortable year-round / avoid system breakdowns / maximize the life of their HVAC investment] through [honest, reliable, expert service with no surprises]. Example: 'We keep [City] families comfortable year-round — with expert HVAC service, honest pricing, and a guarantee that the job is done right the first time.' Typography

7-Day Brand Launch Checklist

Days 1-2 — Foundation ✓ Choose color palette and add to Canva Brand Kit ✓ Select fonts (Montserrat + Open Sans) ✓ Write your Brand Promise ✓ Define your primary service positioning angle Days 3-4 — Build Templates & Update Profiles ✓ Create GBP/Facebook post template with job photo frame ✓ Build seasonal campaign flyer (spring/fall tune-up) ✓ Update Google Business Profile — photos, description, services, hours ✓ Update Facebook page cover photo and description ✓ Design door hanger template Days 5-7 — First Week of Content ✓ Batch-create 3 posts using the 90-Day Calendar ✓ Generate captions with the monthly AI prompt ✓ Post one job photo to GBP with a keyword-rich caption ✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri and engage with every comment The best HVAC brand isn't the loudest. It's the most trusted. Show up consistently. Do the job right. Ask for the review. Repeat.
