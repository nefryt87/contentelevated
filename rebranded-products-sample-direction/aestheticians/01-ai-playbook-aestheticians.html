The New Client Inquiry Response System First impressions set the tone for the entire client relationship. A warm, specific, knowledgeable response to a new client inquiry — whether via DM, email, or phone — converts at dramatically higher rates than a generic 'we'd love to help you!'

Write a warm, professional inquiry response email for my aesthetician practice. The potential client [Name] reached out about [treatment type — e.g., HydraFacial / chemical peel / Botox consult / microneedling / lash lift]. I want to: (1) acknowledge their specific concern and show I understand it, (2) briefly explain how [treatment] addresses it, (3) mention what a consultation looks like and what they can expect, (4) invite them to book. Warm and knowledgeable, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].
