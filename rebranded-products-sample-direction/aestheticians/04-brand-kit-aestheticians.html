Template 1: Before & After Result Post

Search 'before after skin Instagram' or 'skincare transformation post'. Two-panel layout: before skin on left, after on right (or describe in text if not showing client photos). Brief description of treatment and timeframe. Your name and studio. HIPAA/consent note: always get explicit written consent before sharing any client photos. These are the highest-converting post type for aestheticians.

02

Working page

Template 2: Skin Education Carousel

Search 'skincare tips carousel' or 'educational slides Instagram'. 5-7 slides: cover slide with bold question or hook ('Is your SPF actually working?'), one tip or insight per slide, final CTA slide with your name and booking link. Carousels with genuine educational value save at very high rates — clients bookmark them and share them.

03

Working page

Template 3: Treatment Spotlight Post

Search 'beauty treatment Instagram' or 'facial treatment post'. Clean layout: treatment name in large heading, a brief plain-language description, who it is for, the outcome, your booking link. Create one of these for each of your treatments and use them as evergreen content throughout the year.

04

Working page

Template 4: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'testimonial quote wellness' or 'client review skin'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a review. First name and treatment type below. Your studio name. These build trust before any conversation starts — schedule one per week.

05

Working page

Template 5: Instagram Story — Booking Cta

Search 'Instagram story booking wellness' or 'appointment available story'. Vertical. A soft, clean treatment room or product flat lay as background. Text: 'Now booking [month] appointments' + 'limited availability'. Your name and booking link. Post every time you open new booking windows.
