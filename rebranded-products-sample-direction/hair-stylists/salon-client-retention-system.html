The Follow-Up System One touchpoint after an appointment can dramatically increase the chance a client returns. Most stylists skip this entirely — which means you can stand out with minimal effort. The 24-Hour Follow-Up Text Send this the day after every appointment. Takes 30 seconds. Works every time: After color: Hey [name]! Just checking in — how are you loving your hair today? Hope you're getting lots of compliments Remember: wait 48 hours before washing, and use sulfate-free shampoo to keep that color vibrant as long as possible! After a big change (chop, color transformation, etc.): Hey [name]! Still thinking about your transformation yesterday — you looked absolutely amazing. How are you feeling about it today? First-day nerves are normal after a big change, but I have a feeling you're going to love it more every day After a regular maintenance appointment: Hey [name]! Thanks so much for coming in yesterday — always love seeing you! Let me know if you have any questions about the products I recommended. See you in [X] weeks! The Loyalty & Referral System Your most loyal clients are your most valuable marketing asset. They refer friends, leave reviews, and stay through price increases because they trust you. This system is designed to recognize, reward, and activate them. Identifying Your VIP Clients A VIP client meets at least 2 of these criteria: • Books 4+ times per year • Average ticket is above your menu average • Has referred at least one friend in the past year • Has left a Google review • Has been coming to you for 2+ years The 3 Referral Program Structures Structure How It Works Best For Give $20, Get $20 Referring client gets $20 off next visit when their friend books. New client gets $20 off first visit. Easy to explain and track. Works for any salon size. Service Upgrade Reward Referring client gets a complimentary add-on (gloss, scalp treatment, blowout) on their next visit. Protects margins better than discounts. Feels more premium. Points-Based Loyalty Clients earn points for visits, referrals, and reviews. Redeem for free services or products. Best for salons with a booking system that supports tracking.
