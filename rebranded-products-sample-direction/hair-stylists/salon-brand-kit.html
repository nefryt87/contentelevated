Brand Voice

Your brand voice is how your business sounds when it speaks. It is consistent across every caption, text, email, and response to a review. Clients who love your voice will come looking for it — and it is one of the hardest things for competitors to copy. Step 1 — Choose Your 3 Voice Attributes Pick 3 adjectives from the list below that describe how you want your brand to sound. For each one, add what it means in practice and what it does NOT mean: Warm · Direct · Expert · Playful · Confident · Nurturing · Edgy · Premium · Approachable · Educational · Empowering · Honest · Witty · Calm · Energetic Voice Attribute In Practice This Means... This Does NOT Mean...
