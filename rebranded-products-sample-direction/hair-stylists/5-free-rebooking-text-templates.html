Template 2 — 4-Week Reminder (Cut Client)

Customize before use
When to send: Send this 4 weeks after a haircut. Works especially well for shorter cuts and precision styles. Hey [name]! Just thinking about you — it's been about a month since your cut and I know [her style] starts to lose its shape right around now. Want to get you back in before it gets too grown out. I have some spots open — does [day] or [day] work? Pro tip: Reference the specific style if you remember it. Even a vague mention like 'your style' feels personal enough.

Just say the word! Pro tip: The emoji softens it. 'Just say the word' makes it effortless to respond. Never use the word 'overdue.'
