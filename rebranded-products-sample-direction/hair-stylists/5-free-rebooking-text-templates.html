Each template below is ready to copy and paste directly into a text message. Replace anything in [brackets] with your specific details. The more personal you make it, the better it performs. One rule: only send these if you mean them. These texts work because they feel personal — not automated. Take 10 seconds to add a detail specific to that client before you hit send.
