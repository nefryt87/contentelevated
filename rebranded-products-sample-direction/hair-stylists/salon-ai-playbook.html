Content, Captions, Email Campaigns, And How To Make AI Sound Like You — Not A Robot.

Training AI to Write in Your Voice The most common complaint about AI content is that it sounds generic. The fix is simple: you have to teach it your voice before asking it to write for you. Do this once and every piece of content it produces will feel like you wrote it. Voice training prompt — run this first: I'm going to give you 3 examples of captions I've written. Study the tone, word choice, sentence length, and personality. Then write all future social media content in this style. Example 1: [paste a caption you've written] Example 2: [paste another caption] Example 3: [paste another caption] Confirm you understand my voice.
