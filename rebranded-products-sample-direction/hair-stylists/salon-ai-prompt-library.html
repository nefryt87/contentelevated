How To Use This Library

Every prompt in this library is written specifically for hair salons and beauty businesses. Brackets like [THIS] indicate fields you fill in with your specific details. The more specific you are, the better the output you'll get from ChatGPT or any AI tool. Bookmark this document and come back to it whenever you need to produce something quickly. Each prompt is a starting point — feel free to adapt the language to match your voice. 1 Copy the prompt Select the prompt text, copy it, and paste it directly into ChatGPT (chat.openai.com) or your preferred AI tool. 2 Fill in the brackets Replace every [BRACKETED FIELD] with your specific details before hitting send. The more detail, the better. 3 Refine if needed If the first output isn't quite right, don't start over. Just reply: 'Make it shorter,' 'More casual,' or 'Try a different angle.' 4 Save your best outputs Keep a running document of AI outputs you love — these become your template library over time.
