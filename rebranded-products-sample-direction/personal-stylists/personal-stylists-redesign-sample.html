Signature Prompt

Build a premium style consultation summary.

Use this after an intake form or discovery call to turn scattered client details into a polished, confidence-building plan.

Personal Style Direction Prompt

Act as a premium personal stylist and client experience strategist. Based on this client intake: [paste notes], create a polished style direction summary. Include: their lifestyle needs, style goals, current wardrobe friction, 3 guiding style principles, suggested color/material direction, what to stop buying, what to invest in next, and a warm next-step note. Keep the tone refined, specific, encouraging, and practical.

Content ElevatedWeekly Rhythm

Implementation

Make the business feel as polished as the wardrobe.

A simple weekly cadence keeps the system usable: one piece of authority content, one client message, one consult improvement, and one relationship-building action.

01

Monday: publish a style principle

Share one outfit rule, capsule framework, or shopping mistake your ideal client needs to understand.

02

Tuesday: refine one intake question

Make your questionnaire more specific around lifestyle, budget, wardrobe frustration, and desired identity.

03

Thursday: send a warm follow-up

Follow up with one lead, past client, or referral partner using a message that feels personal and useful.

04

Friday: build one recommendation asset

Create a mini guide, outfit formula, shopping note, or rebooking prompt that improves the client experience.
