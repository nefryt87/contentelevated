Why Most Salon Owners Are Undercharging

If you set your prices by looking at what competitors charge, asking around, or going with your gut — you're not alone. Most stylists do. But here's the problem: none of those methods actually tell you whether you're making money.

Pricing without knowing your real costs means you could be fully booked and still not profitable. It means giving discounts you can't afford. It means never knowing if a slow week is fine or a financial emergency.

This calculator fixes that. Four simple tabs. Real numbers. Total clarity on what you need to charge, what you need to earn, and whether your current business model is working. What's Included Tab Name What It Does 1 Service Pricing Calculator
