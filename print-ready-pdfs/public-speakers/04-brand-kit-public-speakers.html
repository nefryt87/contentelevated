PUBLIC SPEAKER

Brand Kit + Speaker Materials Guide

Two signature palettes, authoritative typography, speaker one-sheet structure, 7 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND IS YOUR FIRST

IMPRESSION WITH EVERY BOOKING DECISION

Event organisers and meeting planners evaluate a speaker's brand before they watch their reel. Your website, your LinkedIn profile, your one-sheet — these communicate your credibility, your positioning, and whether you are the right speaker for their event

before they have seen you speak. A polished, cohesive visual identity adds perceived value and justifies premium fees. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for professional speakers.

ACCENT: Montserrat ExtraBold ALL CAPS — for key stats, credential callouts, 'BOOK NOW' CTAs.

Search 'thought leader quote Instagram' or 'professional insight post'. A compelling one- liner from your talk or your body of work. Your name and speaking topic below. Clean,

Template 4: Speaking Testimonial Graphic

Search 'speaker testimonial graphic' or 'professional review post'. Feature the most specific and authoritative testimonial you have — include the person's name, title, and company. Your name. These are your most powerful marketing asset. Create one for

every new testimonial you receive.

Template 5: Event Slide Template

Search 'keynote slide template' or 'presentation slide design'. Create a branded slide

layout, quote slide, data/stat highlight slide, thank you/contact slide. Consistent slides signal professionalism to organisers who see them in advance.

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit. Review your existing

headshots — book a professional session if needed.

✓ Day 2: Update your LinkedIn profile: new banner (Template 2), headline updated to

reflect your speaking niche, about section rewritten to speak to event organisers.

✓ Day 3: Write your three bio versions using the Chapter 2 prompt from Doc 1. Update

your website, LinkedIn, and one-sheet with the new bios.

✓ Day 4: Design your speaker one-sheet using Template 1. Get all testimonials,

credentials, and past client logos in one place first.

✓ Day 5: Create 5 testimonial graphics using Template 4 — schedule one per week

on LinkedIn for 5 weeks.

✓ Day 6: Create 5 quote/insight graphics using Template 3 — schedule them across

the next 2 weeks on LinkedIn.

✓ Day 7: Upload or update your speaker reel on YouTube and LinkedIn. This is the

single most important booking collateral you have.
