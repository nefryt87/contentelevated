PUBLIC SPEAKER

The AI Playbook for Public Speakers Book more paid engagements, negotiate your fee with confidence, build the LinkedIn presence that attracts event organisers, and create the speaker brand that commands premium rates

INTRODUCTION

The professional speakers filling their calendars with paid engagements aren't just the most inspiring on stage. They respond to every enquiry quickly with materials that make

event organisers feel certain they are making the right choice. They negotiate their fee with confidence rather than apologising for it. They maintain relationships with meeting

planners, speaker bureaus, and event organisers who book them year after year. They build a LinkedIn and content presence that positions them as the obvious expert in their space. And they follow up after every engagement to generate the testimonials,

referrals, and repeat bookings that compound over time. This playbook gives you 7 chapters covering every high-leverage scenario for professional speakers — keynote, workshop, corporate, conference, and virtual.

CONVERSION

CHAPTER 5: POST-ENGAGEMENT SYSTEM

Turning Every Engagement into Future Bookings The period immediately after a successful speaking engagement is when you are most top-of-mind with the organiser, most likely to receive a referral, and most likely to be re-

engaged. Most speakers never systematically follow up. The ones who do see compounding results.

PROMPT — POST-ENGAGEMENT FOLLOW-UP EMAIL

Write a post-engagement follow-up email for my speaking business. Organiser [Name] from [Organisation], event on [date]. I want to: (1) express genuine gratitude and one specific personal observation about the event or audience, (2) ask for

feedback — what landed, what could be adjusted, (3) make a soft ask for a testimonial or LinkedIn recommendation, (4) plant the seed for a repeat engagement or referral. Professional and warm. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name].
