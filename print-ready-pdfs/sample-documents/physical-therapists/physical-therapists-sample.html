48-Hour Appointment Reminder

Subject: Your PT appointment — [First Name] Hi [First Name], Just a reminder that your physical therapy appointment is [date] at [time] at [Practice Name]. Please wear comfortable clothing and arrive 5 minutes early. Let us know if any symptoms have changed. To reschedule, call [Phone] at least 24 hours in advance. See you soon! [Practice Name] | [Phone]

Hep Companion Email

Subject: Your home exercise program — [First Name] Hi [First Name], Your home exercise program is attached/available in [portal]. Why these exercises: [1-2 sentence clinical rationale in plain language]. Most common mistake: [one specific correction]. How often: [frequency]. Consistency with your HEP is one of the biggest factors in how fast you recover. Questions? Just reply. [Therapist Name] | [Practice Name]

Between-Visit Check-In

Subject: Quick check-in — how are you feeling, [First Name]? Hi [First Name], Just checking in between visits — how have your symptoms been since we last saw you? Any changes in your pain level? Able to complete your home exercises? Anything to address at your next appointment? Even a brief reply helps me prepare. [Therapist Name] | [Practice Name]
