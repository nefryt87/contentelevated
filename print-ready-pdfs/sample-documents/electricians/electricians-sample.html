01

Copy / paste prompt

Estimate Follow-Up Email

Write a professional estimate follow-up email for my electrical contracting business. Customer [Name] received an estimate for [job type] on [date] for $[amount]. I want to: (1) confirm they received it, (2) briefly explain what's included that a lower bid might not cover — licensed, insured, permit pulled if required, [X]-year workmanship guarantee, (3) make the next step obvious. Professional and warm, not pushy. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Ev Charger Campaign Email

Write a proactive EV charger campaign email for my electrical contracting business to past residential customers. I want to: (1) mention EV growth and that homeowners can charge significantly faster with a Level 2 charger, (2) briefly explain installation — what it involves and typical timeframe, (3) mention investment range $[X]-$[X], (4) invite them to call for a free assessment. Professional and helpful, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Property Manager Introduction Email

Write a professional introduction email from an electrical contractor to a property manager named [Name] at [Company]. I want to: (1) introduce my company and commercial electrical capabilities, (2) mention specialties like emergency response, code compliance, panel upgrades, (3) note response time commitment and licensing, (4) propose a brief call. Under 150 words. Professional and confident. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

Post-Job Review Request (Sms + Email)

Write a post-job review request text message AND email for my electrical contracting business. Customer [Name] just had [job type] completed. Text: under 60 words. Email: under 100 words. Both: personal non-automated feel, include direct Google review link [link], express genuine appreciation. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name].

Copy / paste prompt

Facebook & Google Post Batch (5 Posts)

Write 5 Facebook and Google Business Profile posts for an electrical contractor. Topics: (1) '5 signs your panel might need an upgrade' — specific signs in plain language, (2) why to hire a licensed electrician for [job type], (3) a completed job post from [neighbourhood], (4) EV charger education — Level 1 vs Level 2 for homeowners, (5) a seasonal safety tip. Each under 150 words, professional and useful. Company: [Company Name].
