01

Copy / paste prompt

New Client Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional inquiry response for my dog walking business. The potential client [Name] reached out about [service type]. Details they shared: [paste key details — dog name, breed, age, any behaviour notes, schedule]. My experience: [brief]. I want to: (1) make them feel their dog will be in safe loving hands, (2) briefly describe my approach, (3) ask 2-3 questions to understand their dog, (4) invite them to a meet-and-greet. Warm and reassuring. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Walk Update Message

Write a walk update message to send a pet owner mid-walk or just after. Dog: [Dog Name]. Walk details: [route, duration, weather, any notable moments]. I want this to: (1) feel personal and specific to their dog, (2) share one genuine moment from the walk, (3) confirm the dog is happy and safe, (4) be warm and brief. Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Google Review Request

Write a post-service review request message for my dog walking business. Client [Name], their dog [Dog Name]. I want to: (1) share one genuine observation about their dog, (2) express how much I enjoyed working with them, (3) make a warm personal ask for a Google review, (4) include the direct link. Warm and personal, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

Copy / paste prompt

Vet / Groomer Partnership Introduction

Write a professional introduction email from a dog walker to a local [vet / groomer / trainer] named [Name] at [Business]. I want to: (1) introduce my dog walking business, (2) mention our complementary services and shared client base, (3) propose a mutual referral arrangement, (4) offer to leave cards with their front desk. Friendly and professional. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Caption Batch (5 Captions)

Write 5 Instagram captions for a dog walker and pet sitter. Topics: (1) a specific dog walked recently with a funny or sweet moment, (2) a dog safety or care tip, (3) behind-the-scenes of a typical walk day, (4) a client testimonial or review highlight, (5) a direct booking CTA with current availability. Each under 150 words, warm and personality-led. Business name: [Business Name].
