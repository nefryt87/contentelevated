Prompt System

Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Dm Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional Instagram DM response for my nail studio. The client [Name] reached out about [service]. They mentioned: [paste key details from their message]. My style speciality is [your style]. I want to: (1) make them feel like I genuinely read their message, (2) briefly describe how I would approach their request, (3) ask one qualifying question if needed or invite them to book. Warm and excited, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name / Studio Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

48-Hour Appointment Reminder

Write a 48-hour appointment reminder message for my nail studio. Client [Name], appointment [date] at [time] for [service]. I want to: (1) remind them warmly, (2) confirm the key details, (3) mention my cancellation policy briefly. Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name / Studio Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Rebooking Reminder At Optimal Window

Write a rebooking reminder message for a nail client. Client [Name] had [service] on [date]. It has been [X] weeks. I want to: (1) reference their nails specifically, (2) mention that now is the ideal time to book, (3) share booking link or availability. Brief and warm, under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name / Studio Name].

Seasonal Nail Art Campaign Email

Write a seasonal nail art campaign email for my nail studio. Season or occasion: [e.g., Christmas / Valentine's Day / Summer]. Featured designs: [describe 2-3]. I want to: (1) create genuine excitement about the designs, (2) mention limited availability to create urgency, (3) make it easy to book. Fun and enthusiastic, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Caption Batch (5 Captions)

Write 5 Instagram captions for a nail technician specialising in [your style]. Topics: (1) a nail set you are most proud of this month, (2) a common nail myth you want to correct, (3) how to make your gel manicure last longer — practical tips, (4) your favourite design trend right now, (5) a direct booking CTA with your current availability. Each under 150 words, fun and specific. Studio name: [Studio Name].
