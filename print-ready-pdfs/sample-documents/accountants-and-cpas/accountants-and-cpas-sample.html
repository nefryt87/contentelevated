Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

Post-Consultation Follow-Up Email

Write a post-consultation follow-up email for my CPA practice. The prospect [Name] came in about [matter type — e.g., business tax planning / personal tax return / new business setup]. Key things they shared: [list 2-3 things]. I want to: (1) summarize their situation in plain language showing I listened, (2) outline my recommended approach in 2-3 sentences, (3) present a clear next step. Professional but warm, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Monthly Referral Partner Value Touch

Write a monthly value email from a CPA to a referral partner — [attorney / financial advisor] named [Name] at [Firm]. Include: (1) one brief tax or accounting insight relevant to their clients right now, (2) a soft mention of any relevant case type I'm available for. Professional, genuinely useful, under 150 words. Should feel like useful information from a knowledgeable colleague. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Tax Season Kickoff Email

Write a tax season kickoff email for my CPA practice to send to clients in January or February. Cover: (1) our timeline for the season, (2) what documents clients need to gather and submit, (3) our deadline for receiving documents to guarantee on-time filing, (4) how to submit documents. Professional, warm, and organised, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

04

Copy / paste prompt

Advisory Services Introduction To Existing Client

Write an email introducing advisory and tax planning services to an existing compliance client [Name] who is a [business owner / high-income individual]. I want to frame it as: this is what we do for clients who want to get ahead of their tax situation rather than just report it. Include 1-2 specific examples of planning opportunities relevant to their situation. Invite a 30-minute planning call. Not salesy — under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Linkedin Post Batch (5 Posts)

Write 5 LinkedIn posts for a CPA specialising in [specialty]. Topics: (1) the most common tax mistake [business owners / high-income individuals] make — specific, (2) a recent IRS or tax law update in plain language, (3) what advisory-level accounting looks like vs. just filing returns, (4) an anonymised client outcome story, (5) a direct consultation CTA. Each under 200 words, educational and specific, ending with a question or insight. Firm name: [Firm Name].
