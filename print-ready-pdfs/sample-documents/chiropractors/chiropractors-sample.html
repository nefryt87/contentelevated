New Patient Welcome Email

Subject: Welcome to [Practice Name] — [First Name] Hi [First Name], We're so glad you've chosen [Practice Name] for your care. Your first appointment is [date] at [time]. Please arrive 10 minutes early to complete your intake forms. What to bring: [list], what to wear: comfortable clothing, what to expect: [brief overview of first visit]. Any questions? Call us at [Phone]. See you soon! [Practice Name]

Post-Adjustment Check-In

Subject: How are you feeling, [First Name]? Hi [First Name], Just checking in after your adjustment yesterday — how is your [area] feeling? Some mild soreness in the first 24 hours is normal.
