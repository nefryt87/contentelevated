Prompt System

Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

Speaking Enquiry Response Email

Write a professional, enthusiastic response to a speaking enquiry. The event organiser [Name] from [Company] reached out about speaking at [event name] on [date] for [audience description]. They mentioned: [paste key details]. My speaking speciality: [your topic]. I want to: (1) express genuine enthusiasm, (2) briefly describe how my talk addresses their specific audience, (3) ask 2-3 clarifying questions, (4) mention my fee range if appropriate. Under 250 words. Sign as [Your Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Post-Call Proposal Email

Write a post-call proposal email for a speaking engagement. I spoke with [Name] from [Organisation] about speaking at [event] on [date]. Audience: [description]. Proposed talk: [title and brief description]. Duration: [X minutes]. Fee: $[amount] covering [what is included]. I want this to: (1) confirm what we discussed specifically, (2) describe the talk's value compellingly, (3) present the fee confidently, (4) outline next steps. Under 300 words. Sign as [Your Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Post-Engagement Follow-Up Email

Write a post-engagement follow-up email for my speaking business. Organiser [Name] from [Organisation], event on [date]. I want to: (1) express genuine gratitude with one specific personal observation, (2) ask for feedback on what landed and what could be adjusted, (3) make a soft ask for a testimonial or LinkedIn recommendation, (4) plant the seed for a repeat engagement or referral. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name].

Copy / paste prompt

Speaker Bureau Introduction Email

Write a professional introduction email from a public speaker to a speaker bureau. Bureau name: [name]. My speaking topic: [topic]. My credentials: [key credentials]. My fee range: $[X]-$[X]. I want to: (1) introduce myself and my speaking niche, (2) explain why I would be a strong fit for their roster, (3) mention relevant past engagements, (4) propose a brief call. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Linkedin Post Batch (5 Posts)

Write 5 LinkedIn posts for a professional speaker specialising in [your topic]. Topics: (1) a counterintuitive insight from your speaking topic that most people get wrong, (2) a story from a recent keynote and the moment you knew it landed (anonymise the client), (3) a data point from your topic that surprises your audience, (4) a practical takeaway professionals can apply today, (5) a direct booking CTA describing who you speak for and how to enquire. Each under 200 words, specific and thought-leading. Your name: [Name].
