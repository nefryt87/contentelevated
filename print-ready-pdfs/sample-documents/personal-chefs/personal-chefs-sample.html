01

Copy / paste prompt

Private Dining Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional inquiry response email for my personal chef business. The client [Name] reached out about [service — e.g., private dinner party / weekly meal prep / vacation chef]. They mentioned: [paste key details]. My culinary style: [your style]. I want to: (1) make them feel like I genuinely read their inquiry, (2) briefly describe how I would approach their specific event or service, (3) ask 1-2 clarifying questions, (4) invite them to a discovery call. Warm, professional, enthusiastic. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Event Dining Proposal Email

Write a private dining proposal email for my personal chef business. Client [Name], event: [description], date: [date], guests: [X], location: [venue]. Menu: [brief description]. Investment: $[amount] including [what is covered]. I want this proposal to: (1) paint a vivid picture of the dining experience, (2) communicate the value of having everything handled, (3) present the investment confidently, (4) outline the next step. Professional and evocative. Under 300 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Weekly Meal Prep Check-In

Write a weekly meal prep client check-in message for my personal chef business. Client [Name], this week I prepared: [brief menu summary]. I want to: (1) let them know what is in the fridge and any reheating notes, (2) ask for feedback on this week's meals, (3) mention anything seasonal or exciting coming up next week. Warm and conversational. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

Luxury Realtor Introduction Email

Write a professional introduction email from a personal chef to a luxury real estate agent named [Name] at [Brokerage]. I want to: (1) introduce my private chef service and typical client profile, (2) mention how my service elevates a luxury home purchase or listing experience, (3) propose a referral arrangement or co-marketing idea. Professional and specific. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Business Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Caption Batch (5 Captions)

Write 5 Instagram captions for a personal chef specialising in [your style]. Topics: (1) a recent private dinner — the menu, the occasion, what made it special (anonymised), (2) a seasonal ingredient you love right now and how you are using it, (3) what clients say is the biggest benefit of having a personal chef beyond the food, (4) a behind-the-scenes look at sourcing for a client, (5) a direct inquiry CTA describing who you love cooking for. Each under 150 words, warm and sensory. Business name: [Business Name].
