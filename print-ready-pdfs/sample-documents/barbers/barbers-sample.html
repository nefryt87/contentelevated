01

Copy / paste prompt

New Client Dm Response

Write a warm, professional DM response for my barbershop. A potential new client [Name] messaged asking about [booking / prices / availability / a specific style]. I want to: (1) make them feel welcome and confident they are coming to the right place, (2) answer their question directly, (3) make it easy to book. My booking process: [how clients book]. My price range: $[X]-$[X]. Under 100 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

Copy / paste prompt

24-Hour Appointment Reminder

Write a 24-hour appointment reminder message for my barbershop. Client [Name], appointment tomorrow [date] at [time]. I want to: (1) remind them warmly, (2) confirm the key details, (3) mention my cancellation policy briefly, (4) share my direct number. Under 60 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

Rebooking Reminder At Optimal Window

Write a rebooking reminder message for a barbershop client. Client [Name] had their last cut [X weeks ago]. I want to: (1) mention their hair is at the point where a fresh cut will look sharp again, (2) make it easy to book, (3) feel personal not automated. Under 70 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

Copy / paste prompt

Tiktok / Reel Concept Ideas (5 Concepts)

Write 5 TikTok or Instagram Reel concept ideas for a barber. Each should have: (1) a scroll-stopping hook for the first 2 seconds, (2) a brief description of the content, (3) a caption. Topics: a transformation video, a technique breakdown, a what-to-tell-your-barber guide, a day-in-the-life at the barbershop, a style guide for a specific hair type or face shape. Barbershop name: [Barbershop Name].
