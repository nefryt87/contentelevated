Prompt System

Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

— 4-Week Reminder (Color Client)

When to send: Send this 4 weeks after a color appointment. Works for balayage, highlights, root touch-ups, any color service. Hey [name]! It's been about a month since your last color — just wanted to check in and see how it's holding up. I have some openings coming up if you're thinking about coming in. Just reply and I'll send you some times! Pro tip: Keep it casual. Don't say 'your roots are showing' — say 'how's it holding up.' Let them come to that conclusion themselves.

02

Copy / paste prompt

— 4-Week Reminder (Cut Client)

When to send: Send this 4 weeks after a haircut. Works especially well for shorter cuts and precision styles. Hey [name]! Just thinking about you — it's been about a month since your cut and I know [her style] starts to lose its shape right around now. Want to get you back in before it gets too grown out. I have some spots open — does [day] or [day] work? Pro tip: Reference the specific style if you remember it. Even a vague mention like 'your style' feels personal enough.

03

Copy / paste prompt

— 6-Week Overdue (Any Service)

When to send: Send this when a client is 6+ weeks overdue. Warm and slightly playful — no guilt. Hey [name]! Miss seeing you in the chair It's been a little while — [service] tends to start showing some wear right around now. I've got a couple spots open this week and next if you want to get back in. Just say the word! Pro tip: The emoji softens it. 'Just say the word' makes it effortless to respond. Never use the word 'overdue.'

Copy / paste prompt

— After A Big Transformation

When to send: Send this 24–48 hours after a major color, cut, or style change. While they're still in the glow. Hey [name]! Still thinking about your [transformation] from yesterday — you looked absolutely incredible. How are you feeling about it today? I have a feeling you're going to love it more every day Let me know if you have any questions about your routine! Pro tip: This text does three things: reinforces their confidence, opens a homecare conversation, and makes them feel like more than just a client.
