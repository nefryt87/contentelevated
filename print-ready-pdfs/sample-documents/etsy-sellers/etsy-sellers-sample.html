Prompt System

Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

Etsy Listing Title Optimisation (5 Options)

Write 5 optimised Etsy listing title options for my product: [describe your product in detail — material, style, size, what it is, who it is for]. My current title is: [paste current title if you have one]. I want titles that: (1) lead with the most searched keywords, (2) include specific descriptors buyers use when searching — material, style, occasion, recipient, (3) are under 140 characters, (4) feel natural to read. Include a mix — some leading with product type, some with occasion or recipient. Provide each title on its own line.

02

Copy / paste prompt

Etsy Listing Description

Write a compelling Etsy listing description for my product: [describe product — what it is, materials, dimensions, variations, what makes it special]. Target buyer: [describe your ideal customer — e.g., a bride looking for personalised wedding gifts / a home decorator who loves minimalist style]. I want the description to: (1) open with a hook addressing the buyer's need or occasion, (2) describe the product clearly with all key details, (3) answer the most common buyer questions, (4) include relevant keywords naturally, (5) end with a reassuring next step. Under 400 words. Shop name: [Shop Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Post-Delivery Follow-Up Message (Review Request)

Write a post-delivery follow-up Etsy message for my shop. The customer [Name] ordered [product] and it should have arrived approximately [X days ago]. I want to: (1) check in and make sure they received it and are happy, (2) provide any relevant care or use instructions briefly, (3) make a warm personal ask for a review explaining why it matters to a small shop, (4) invite them to contact me if anything is not perfect. Warm and genuine, under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Shop Name].

04

Copy / paste prompt

Seasonal Sale Social Media Posts (3 Posts)

Write 3 social media posts announcing a seasonal sale on my Etsy shop. Sale: [X]% off everything / [specific products] for [duration]. Occasion: [e.g., Black Friday / Christmas / Valentine's Day / Spring launch]. I want the posts to: (1) create genuine excitement and urgency, (2) highlight the best products in the sale, (3) include a clear CTA with my shop link. One post for Instagram, one for Pinterest, one for Facebook. Each under 150 words. Shop name: [Shop Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Caption Batch (5 Captions)

Write 5 Instagram captions for my Etsy shop. Products or topics: [list 5 things to feature — e.g., a specific product, behind-the-scenes making, a customer photo, a seasonal gift guide, a shop CTA]. For each: (1) tell the story behind the product or post, (2) include relevant keywords or hashtag suggestions, (3) end with a CTA pointing to my Etsy shop. Each under 150 words, warm and personal. Shop name: [Shop Name], Etsy link: [link].
