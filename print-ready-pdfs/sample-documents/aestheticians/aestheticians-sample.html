Prompt System

Start with the client moment.

Use these prompts when the customer is asking, deciding, rebooking, reviewing, or needing a reason to say yes.

01

Copy / paste prompt

New Client Inquiry Response Email

Write a warm, professional inquiry response email for my aesthetician practice. The potential client [Name] reached out about [treatment type]. They mentioned: [paste key details — skin concern, what they hope to achieve]. I want to: (1) acknowledge their specific concern and show I understand it, (2) briefly explain how the treatment addresses it, (3) describe what a consultation looks like, (4) invite them to book. Warm and knowledgeable, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

02

Copy / paste prompt

Post-Treatment Check-In (3-5 Days)

Write a post-treatment check-in message for my aesthetician practice. Client [Name] had [treatment type] on [date]. I want to: (1) check in on how their skin is responding, (2) ask about any specific side effects they are noticing, (3) give one helpful tip relevant to where they will be in their healing, (4) plant the seed for their next appointment without being pushy. Warm and caring, under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

03

Copy / paste prompt

Treatment Series Recommendation Email

Write an email recommending a treatment series to a client after their first session. Client [Name] just had their first [treatment]. Their skin concern: [concern]. I want to: (1) compliment their skin's response to today's treatment, (2) explain why a series of [X] sessions produces significantly better results than a single treatment, (3) present the series investment vs. individual pricing, (4) offer to book all sessions now to guarantee availability. Warm and educational, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

04

Copy / paste prompt

Google Review Request

Write a post-treatment review request message for my aesthetician practice. Client [Name] just had [treatment]. Send 3-5 days after. I want to: (1) check in on how they are feeling about their skin, (2) make a warm personal ask for a Google review, (3) include the direct Google review link. Genuine and non-automated, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

05

Copy / paste prompt

Instagram Caption Batch (5 Captions)

Write 5 Instagram captions for a med spa aesthetician. Topics: (1) a common skin myth you want to debunk — specific and backed by your expertise, (2) what clients with [skin type or concern] should know before booking any treatment, (3) a before-and-after result story (anonymised, described in words), (4) a behind-the-scenes look at your consultation process and what you look for, (5) a direct booking CTA with a limited availability hook. Each under 150 words, warm and authoritative. Spa name: [Studio/Spa Name].
