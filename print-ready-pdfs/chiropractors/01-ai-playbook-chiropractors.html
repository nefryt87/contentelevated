Chapter 1: Why AI Is the Chiropractor's Practice

Growth Tool Most chiropractors are exceptional at patient care — and overwhelmed by everything else. Appointment reminders, follow-up calls, re-engagement outreach, social media,

patient education, review requests — the list never ends. AI doesn't treat patients. But it handles every task that prevents you from focusing on the ones who need treating.

• The No-Show Problem: The average chiropractic practice loses 15–20% of

appointments to no-shows and last-minute cancellations. AI-powered reminder and reconfirmation messages cut that number significantly.

• The Drop-Off Gap: Most new chiropractic patients attend 2–3 visits before

dropping off — regardless of their care plan. Poor communication between visits is the #1 reason. AI keeps you present without demanding more of your time.

• The Referral Silence: Happy patients refer — but only when prompted. Most

practices never ask systematically. AI builds that ask into every patient journey automatically.

• What AI Handles vs. What You Handle: AI communicates, educates, follows

up, and reminds. You diagnose, treat, and build relationships. The boundary matters.

Chapter 2: Your First 3 AI Wins — This Week

Start here. These three quick wins take under an hour to implement and will impact your practice immediately.

• Win #1 — Appointment Reminder Sequence: Use AI to write a 3-message

reminder sequence: 48 hours before, morning of, and a no-show re-engagement message. Reduce cancellations and fill gaps faster.

• Win #2 — New Patient Welcome Email: Every new patient should receive a

warm, informative welcome email before their first visit — what to expect, what to bring, and why chiropractic care works. AI writes this in 2 minutes. Patient anxiety drops. Show-up rates improve.

• Win #3 — Review Request Message: Google reviews drive new patient

acquisition more than almost any other factor. AI writes a friendly, non-pushy review request that goes out after a patient's 3rd visit — the sweet spot for authentic positive reviews.

Chapter 3: Filling Your Schedule and Reducing

No-Shows A full schedule doesn't happen by accident. It's the result of consistent communication that keeps existing patients engaged and makes it easy for new patients to say yes.

• The New Patient Welcome Sequence: A 3-email sequence that goes out

before and after a new patient's first visit — setting expectations, reinforcing the value of their care plan, and prompting them to book their next appointment.

• The Reactivation Campaign: Every practice has a list of patients who stopped

coming in. AI writes a 3-message reactivation sequence that feels personal, not mass-marketed — and converts dormant patients back to active at a meaningful rate.

• The Cancellation Recovery Message: When a patient cancels, most practices

just update the schedule. AI writes a same-day recovery message that acknowledges the cancellation, offers a rebooking option, and keeps the relationship warm.

Chapter 4: Patient Retention — Turning One-Visit

Patients Into Long-Term Care Plans The lifetime value of a patient who completes a full care plan vs. one who drops off after 2 visits is enormous. The difference is almost always communication — specifically, what happens between visits.

• The Between-Visit Check-In: A short message 2–3 days after each visit asking

how the patient is feeling and reinforcing their home care instructions. Takes 90 seconds with AI. Patients feel supported. Drop-off rates fall.

• The Care Plan Progress Email: At the midpoint of any care plan, AI writes a

progress check-in that recaps improvements, reinforces why continuing matters, and addresses the most common reason patients stop: 'I feel better now.'

• The Wellness Transition Message: When a patient completes their acute care

plan, most chiropractors lose them. AI writes a transition message that introduces maintenance/wellness care naturally — not as an upsell, but as the logical next step.

• The Missed Appointment Sequence: When a patient misses without

cancelling, AI writes a 2-message sequence: a same-day check-in, and a 3-day

follow-up. Warm, not guilt-inducing. Reschedules more patients than silence

does.

Chapter 5: Building Your Referral Engine With AI

Referrals are the most cost-effective source of new patients for any chiropractic practice. But most chiropractors never ask — not because they don't want to, but because they don't have a system. AI builds that system.

• The Post-Milestone Referral Ask: The best time to ask for a referral is right

after a patient hits a meaningful milestone — reduced pain, improved mobility, first full night's sleep. AI writes a milestone celebration + referral ask in one natural message.

• The Professional Referral Network: Primary care physicians, physical

• The Family Referral Prompt: When a patient mentions a family member with a

Chapter 6: Social Content That Educates and

Attracts New Patients Patients research chiropractors online before they ever call. The practices with consistent, educational social content get more inquiries, more trust, and more patients who show up already sold on care. AI makes that content effortless.

• The 3-Post Formula: One educational post (condition, technique, or wellness

tip), one trust post (patient story, behind the scenes, your philosophy), one engagement post (myth-bust, question, seasonal tip). Post Mon/Wed/Fri.

• High-Converting Topic Categories: Posture and desk ergonomics, headache

and migraine relief, sports injury recovery, pregnancy and pediatric chiropractic, sciatica and lower back pain, sleep and chiropractic, myth-busting (cracking knuckles, adjustments are dangerous, etc.).

• Batch Creating in 30 Minutes: Use AI to generate a full month of captions in

one session. Your 90-Day Calendar has the post ideas — the monthly AI prompt generates all 12 captions at once.

Chapter 7: Your 30-Day AI Implementation Plan

Don't try to implement everything at once. This plan gets you from reading to real results in 30 days without disrupting your clinical schedule.

• Week 1 — Patient Communication Foundation: Set up Claude or ChatGPT.

Write your new patient welcome email and appointment reminder sequence. Build your no-show recovery message.

• Week 2 — Retention System: Build your between-visit check-in template. Write

your care plan progress email. Create your wellness transition message.

• Week 3 — Referrals and Reviews: Write your post-milestone referral ask. Set

up your review request message. Draft 2 professional referral outreach emails.

Calendar. Batch captions with the monthly AI prompt. Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri.
