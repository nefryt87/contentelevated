HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your patient's name, condition, and your practice

details.

• Tone is intentionally warm and human — patients respond to empathy, not

clinical formality.

• These work as emails, SMS messages, and DMs — shorten to 2–3

sentences for text.

• Always personalize the first line — AI can do this in seconds using patient

context.

• Save your top performers as templates in your practice management

software or Gmail.
