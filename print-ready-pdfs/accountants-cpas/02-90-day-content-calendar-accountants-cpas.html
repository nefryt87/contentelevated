12 weeks of Mon/Wed/Fri content for LinkedIn, Facebook, and email — with monthly AI post prompts

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

LinkedIn is your primary platform — business owners, executives, and referral partners live there. Facebook targets local small business owners and individual clients. Email is

your highest-retention channel — send a monthly newsletter to your entire client and prospect list. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Use the monthly AI prompt to batch all captions in one 10-minute session.

MONTH 1: AUTHORITY & TAX EDUCATION

Write 8 LinkedIn posts for a CPA specialising in [specialty]. Topics: the most common tax mistake your target client makes, a recent tax law update in plain language, what advisory accounting looks like vs. compliance, a client outcome

story (anonymised), a question post for business owners, a myth about taxes debunked, your philosophy on proactive planning, a direct consultation CTA. Under 200 words each.
