HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your client's name, goal, and your business info.

• Keep the tone conversational — these work because they sound human,

not corporate.

• Personalize the first line using AI: 'Add a personalized opening line for

[name] who wants to [goal].'

• Text message versions: most of these work as SMS or DMs — just shorten

to 2–3 sentences.

• Use your CRM or Gmail templates to save the top performers for reuse.
