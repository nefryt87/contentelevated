ATTORNEY & LAW FIRM

Free Mini Guide 5 practice development strategies that convert more consultations and build a referral engine Note: All communication strategies should be reviewed for compliance with your state bar's advertising and

solicitation rules before use. The attorneys building the most sustainable practices master communication as rigorously as they master law. The Post-Consultation Conversion Email

The consultation that doesn't convert in the first 48 hours rarely converts at all. The single highest-ROI communication habit for attorneys is a personalized same-day follow-up after every consultation.

 Summarize their legal situation in their own words (plain language, not legal jargon)  Outline your recommended approach in 2-3 clear sentences  Propose one specific, frictionless next step — not a vague 'let's think about it'

Write a post-consultation follow-up email for a [practice area] attorney. The prospect [Name] came in about [brief situation]. Summarize their situation in plain language, outline the recommended legal approach in 2-3 sentences, and propose a

clear next step to engage services. Professional but warm. Compliance-review-ready. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name]. The CPA Referral Partnership

One CPA relationship maintained well can send 5-15 qualified referrals per year. The difference between a CPA who refers occasionally and one who refers consistently is a monthly value touch that keeps you top of mind.

 Professional introduction: brief, focused on value for their clients  Monthly value touch: a relevant legal update, planning resource, or mutual referral opportunity  Post-referral thank-you: personal, within 24 hours, every single time

Write a monthly value touch email from a [practice area] attorney to a CPA named [Name] at [Firm]. Include one brief legal insight relevant to their clients (e.g., a recent law change, an estate planning consideration, a business formation tip).
