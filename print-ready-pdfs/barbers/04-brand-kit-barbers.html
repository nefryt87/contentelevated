WHY YOUR BRAND IS WHAT MAKES CLIENTS

CHOOSE YOUR CHAIR

Clients choose a barber on TikTok and Instagram before they ever step through the door. Your brand — the way your profile looks, your aesthetic, your visual consistency — communicates your skill level, your vibe, and whether you are the right barber for

them. A distinctive, confident brand builds a following that walks through your door. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for barbers and barbershops.

7 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Before/After Transformation Post

Search 'before after barbershop Instagram' or 'hair transformation post'. Two-panel: before on left, after on right. Your name and barbershop small at the bottom. Clean border treatment. This is your most important recurring content type — post every

transformation with client consent. These drive more DMs than any other format.

Template 2: TikTok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'TikTok thumbnail barber' or 'Reel cover template'. The first frame determines whether people click or scroll. Bold text overlay on a dark background: 'WATCH THIS FADE' or 'THE PERFECT SKIN FADE' or 'HOW I DO THIS CUT'. Your barbershop

name small at the bottom. Create one master template and apply to every video.

Template 3: Price List / Services Card

Search 'barbershop price list' or 'salon menu Instagram'. Clean, bold layout. Your services listed with prices. Your barbershop name and booking info at the bottom. Use your accent colour (Gold or Copper) for service names. Post as a pinned Instagram

post — clients check this before DMing.

Template 4: Availability / Flash Booking Story

Search 'booking available story' or 'appointment open Instagram story'. Vertical. Dark background. Bold text: 'SPOTS OPEN [DAY]' + time slots. Your barbershop name. Your

booking contact. Post this every time slots open up — it drives immediate bookings from

your existing followers.

Template 5: 5-Star Review Post

Search 'barber review Instagram' or 'client testimonial post'. Feature your best review line. Client first name or '@handle if they consent'. Your barbershop name. These build local trust. Post every new 5-star review — aim for one per week.

Template 6: Educational Style Guide

Search 'hair guide Instagram' or 'style tip post barber'. 'Find your fade' or 'Which cut suits your face shape?' — clean, informative, highly shareable. One tip or insight per

slide in a carousel. These save at very high rates and attract new followers who are in the research phase.

Template 7: Google Business Profile Post Template

Search 'local business announcement' or 'service update post'. Clean. Your barbershop name, service or availability update, booking link. Post to GBP weekly — every post improves your ranking when people search 'barber near me' in your area. 10 minutes
