WHY YOUR BRAND IS THE FIRST MAGIC TRICK

Parents choose a party planner based on Instagram before they ever make contact. Your brand — your colour palette, your photography aesthetic, how your grid looks — communicates whether you create the kind of magic their child deserves. A distinctive,

joyful, and polished visual identity attracts clients who value what you do and are ready to invest in it. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for party planners.

Typography

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — editorial luxury. Elegant and celebratory without being childish. BODY: Jost — clean and modern. Readable across Instagram, email, and printed materials.

ACCENT: Pinyon Script — for event names, milestone ages, headline moments in a styled graphic. Use sparingly.

Typography

HEADINGS: Nunito ExtraBold — friendly, rounded, joyful. Works perfectly for both kids parties and fun adult events. BODY: Poppins Regular — modern and clean. Reads beautifully across all digital formats.

ACCENT: Nunito ExtraBold ALL CAPS — for theme names, ages ('TURNING 5!'), party announcements.

Template 1: Party Reveal Post

Search 'party reveal Instagram' or 'event setup reveal post'. The finished party space — balloons, tablescapes, backdrop, every element in frame. Your business name small at the bottom. This is your most powerful content type. Every reveal post attracts new

followers and drives direct inquiry DMs. Post every single event you plan.

Template 2: Before / After Setup Transformation

Search 'transformation before after Instagram' or 'event setup time-lapse'. Two images: empty room left, decorated party right. Or use a split screen. Caption: 'This is what [X

hours] of setup looks like.' These perform exceptionally well on TikTok as videos and on Instagram as carousels.

Template 3: Theme Inspiration Carousel

Search 'party theme ideas Instagram' or 'kids birthday inspiration carousel'. 5-7 slides: one theme per slide with a beautiful image and brief description. Final slide: your name and CTA. Create one for each of your most popular themes — parents save these and

return to them when planning. High-save-rate content.

Template 4: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'party planner testimonial post' or 'client review celebration'. Feature the most emotional line from a parent's review — something about their child's face, the magic of

the moment, how stress-free it felt. First name and event type below. Your business name. Post every new testimonial.

Template 5: Instagram Story — Booking Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'party planner story template'. Vertical. A beautiful party photo as background. Text: 'NOW BOOKING [SEASON/MONTH] PARTIES' + 'DM to enquire.' Your business name. Post every time you open new availability —

generates immediate DMs.

Template 6: TikTok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'TikTok first frame template' or 'Reel cover birthday party'. For every video: your hook text in large font on a branded background. 'Watch how I transformed this room in
