PARTY PLANNER

90-Day Content Calendar 12 weeks of Mon/Wed/Fri content for Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest — with monthly AI caption prompts

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

Instagram is where parents discover and save party planning inspiration — event reveals, theme ideas, and transformation content drive DMs and bookings. TikTok reaches a wider audience and is unmatched for viral party transformation videos and

reaction content. Pinterest is the long-tail SEO engine — parents pin party ideas months in advance, and your pins drive consistent discovery. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly.

MONTH 1: PORTFOLIO & BRAND STORY

Write 8 Instagram captions for a party planner. Topics: your story and why you love creating celebrations, your most memorable event, a theme you are obsessed with right now, what parents say after you plan their child's party, your planning process

from first call to party day, a budget tip that makes any party feel magical, a vendor you love working with, a direct booking CTA. Under 150 words each, warm and visual.

WEEK 1: YOUR STORY & STYLE

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram Your party planning journey: what drew you to this work, what you believe every celebration should feel like, who you love creating events for. Wed Pinterest Portfolio pin: '[Theme] birthday party ideas' — keyword-rich, pin

to your themed party board. Fri TikTok 'My favourite party I have ever planned — the moment the birthday girl walked in.' Hook: the reaction. Story: the setup.

WEEK 2: PORTFOLIO & REVEALS

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon Instagram Party reveal: describe a recent event — theme, setup, the guest of honour's reaction. Specific and visual. Wed Pinterest Full party board: pin every photo from a recent party with keyword-rich descriptions for each pin.

Fri Instagram Setup transformation: empty venue vs. decorated party space. Caption: what went into the transformation.
