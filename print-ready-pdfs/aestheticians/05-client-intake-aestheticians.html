SECTION 3: CONSULTATION SCRIPT GUIDE

How to Structure a Productive Skin Consultation

✓ BEFORE THE CONSULTATION:

✓ Review the intake form completely — note any contraindications or areas to explore

✓ Prepare your treatment menu and pricing for likely recommendations

✓ Ensure your magnifying lamp and analysis tools are ready

✓ OPENING (2-3 minutes):

✓ 'Thank you so much for coming in — I'm really looking forward to taking a look at

your skin today. Can you tell me a bit more about what's been bothering you most?'

✓ [Listen fully before responding. Don't offer solutions before you've heard the full

picture.]

✓ SKIN ANALYSIS (5-10 minutes):

✓ Ask permission before touching the skin

✓ Use magnification to assess: texture, tone, pore size, hydration, sensitivity,

sebaceous activity

✓ Share observations in plain language: 'What I'm seeing here is...' / 'This tells me

that...'

✓ Validate their concern: 'This is really common and completely treatable.'

✓ TREATMENT RECOMMENDATION (5-10 minutes):

✓ Recommend 1 primary treatment and 1 supporting treatment maximum — too many

options creates decision paralysis

✓ Frame recommendations around their goal, not the treatment: 'To address the

hyperpigmentation you mentioned, here is what I would suggest...'

✓ Be honest about expected outcomes and timeline: 'After a series of 3, most clients

see significant improvement. After one session, you will see [realistic expectation].'

✓ INVESTMENT CONVERSATION (3-5 minutes):

✓ Present the investment clearly and confidently — apologising for pricing undermines

confidence

✓ 'For a series of 3 sessions, the investment is $[amount], which works out to $[per

session] — significantly less than individual sessions. I can book all 3 now so you have your spots.'

✓ [Offer a payment plan if you have one.]

✓ CLOSE:

✓ 'Do you have any other questions before I put together your plan?'

✓ Always close with a next action: 'I can book your first session today' or 'I'll send you

a summary email and a booking link this afternoon.'

✓ [A consultation that ends without a clear next step rarely converts.]
