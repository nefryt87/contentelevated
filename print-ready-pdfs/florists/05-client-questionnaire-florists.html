SECTION 3: FLOWER CARE CARD TEMPLATE

[STUDIO NAME] — FLOWER CARE GUIDE

TO KEEP YOUR FLOWERS LOOKING BEAUTIFUL:

• On arrival: trim the stems at a 45-degree angle with sharp scissors or

secateurs

• Remove any leaves that sit below the waterline — they cause bacterial

growth

• Place in a clean vase with fresh, cool water

• Add the flower food sachet (if provided) to the water

DAILY CARE:

• Top up the water daily — flowers drink a surprising amount

• Re-trim the stems every 2-3 days to maximise water uptake

• Change the water completely every 3-4 days

PLACEMENT:

• Keep out of direct sunlight and away from heat sources

• Keep away from fruit — ethylene gas significantly shortens vase life

• A cool room overnight (below 18°C) will extend the life considerably
