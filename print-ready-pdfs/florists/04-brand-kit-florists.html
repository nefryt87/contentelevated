FLORIST

Brand Kit + Canva Template Guide

Two signature palettes, botanical typography, 8 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND IS THE FIRST FLOWER

YOUR CLIENT FALLS IN LOVE WITH

Brides and event clients fall in love with a florist's aesthetic before they ever speak to them. Your brand — your palette, your typography, how your Instagram grid looks — communicates whether you are the right artist for their vision. A distinctive, cohesive

visual identity attracts the clients who are already aligned with your style. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for florists and floral designers.

Typography

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — the definitive botanical luxury font. Romantic, editorial, instantly premium. BODY: Jost — clean and warm. Reads beautifully across Instagram, email, and proposals.

ACCENT: Pinyon Script — for studio name in logos, seasonal campaign titles, pull quotes. Use with restraint.

Typography

HEADINGS: Libre Baskerville — authoritative serif with elegance. Clean and editorial. BODY: Nunito — warm and friendly. Approachable for client communications. ACCENT: Josefin Sans Light (wide tracking, ALL CAPS) — for labels, seasonal tags,

minimal graphic overlays.

8 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Floral Arrangement Reveal Post

Search 'floral arrangement Instagram' or 'flower photography post'. Your best arrangement photography fills the frame. Minimal text overlay: studio name in small serif type, bottom corner. Clean and editorial. Let the flowers speak. Create 10-15 of these

with consistent overlay treatment and your feed will look immediately cohesive and professional.

Template 2: Wedding Florals Carousel

Search 'wedding Instagram carousel' or 'floral slideshow'. 8-10 slides: atmosphere shot of the venue, ceremony flowers, bridal bouquet, table arrangements, detail shots, and a final slide with your studio name and inquiry CTA. Use this for every wedding you

photograph. It is your most powerful portfolio format.

Template 3: Seasonal Flower Guide Carousel

Search 'flower guide carousel' or 'botanical education slides'. 5-7 slides: one seasonal flower per slide — name, peak season, what style it suits, how long it lasts in water. A genuinely useful resource that saves at very high rates and attracts brides in the

research phase.

Template 4: Pinterest Wedding Board

Search 'wedding inspiration Pinterest' or 'floral mood board'. Create a board for each season and style you work in: 'Romantic Garden Wedding Florals', 'Modern Architectural Wedding Flowers', 'Wild Meadow Weddings'. Pin your own work and

curated inspiration. A well-optimised Pinterest profile drives consistent inquiry traffic for years.

Template 5: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'wedding testimonial quote' or 'client review floral'. Feature the most evocative line from a client's review — something that makes the reader feel the flowers. Client first name and wedding description below. Your studio name. Schedule one per week

— social proof is your most powerful conversion tool.

Template 6: Instagram Story — Enquiries Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'wedding florist story'. Vertical. A beautiful atmospheric arrangement photo as background. Soft overlay. 'Now booking [season] weddings and events.' + 'Enquire via link.' Your studio name. Post every time you open

new booking windows.

Template 7: Corporate & Home Subscription Post

Search 'office flowers Instagram' or 'home flower delivery post'. Clean, lifestyle-forward layout. A flower arrangement in a beautiful home or office setting. Brief text: 'Weekly

flower subscriptions — for homes, offices, and hotels in [City].' Your contact or booking link. Targets the non-wedding revenue stream.

Template 8: Instagram Highlight Covers

Search 'highlight cover floral' or 'botanical Instagram icons'. Create 8 round covers: Weddings, Events, Corporate, Home Subscriptions, Seasonal, Reviews, About,

Booking. Consistent palette. First thing profile visitors see — make them feel the

aesthetic immediately.
