How to Use

Before You Send

Make every text feel personal.

Each template is ready to copy and paste directly into a text message. Replace anything in brackets with your specific details.

The Rule

Only send these if you mean them. These texts work because they feel personal, not automated. Take 10 seconds to add one detail specific to that client before you hit send.

Best Practice

Keep the tone warm, casual, and direct. You are not chasing the appointment. You are making the next booking feel easy and natural.

01

4-Week Reminder · Color Client

Send four weeks after a color appointment
Hey [name]! It's been about a month since your last color — just wanted to check in and see how it's holding up. I have some openings coming up if you're thinking about coming in. Just reply and I'll send you some times!
Pro tip: Keep it casual. Don't say “your roots are showing.” Say “how's it holding up” and let them come to that conclusion themselves.

02

4-Week Reminder · Cut Client

Best for shorter cuts and precision styles
Hey [name]! Just thinking about you — it's been about a month since your cut and I know [her style] starts to lose its shape right around now. Want to get you back in before it gets too grown out. I have some spots open — does [day] or [day] work?
Pro tip: Reference the specific style if you remember it. Even a simple mention like “your style” feels personal enough.
03

6-Week Overdue · Any Service

Warm and slightly playful, without guilt
Hey [name]! Miss seeing you in the chair. It's been a little while — [service] tends to start showing some wear right around now. I've got a couple spots open this week if you want me to freshen it up.
Pro tip: Make the message feel like hospitality, not pressure. The best rebooking texts sound like care.

Relationship-Based Retention

04

3-Month Lapsed Client

Send when a client has not booked in 3+ months
Hey [name]! It's been a minute — hope everything's been great with you. Whenever you're ready to come back in, just reach out and I'll get you sorted. I always have time for you.
Pro tip: No offer. No discount. No pressure. Just a warm, genuine check-in. This alone can bring back clients who simply forgot to book.
05

After a Big Transformation

Send 24–48 hours after a major color, cut, or style change
Hey [name]! Still thinking about your [transformation] from yesterday — you looked absolutely incredible. How are you feeling about it today? I have a feeling you're going to love it more every day. Let me know if you have any questions about your routine!
Pro tip: This reinforces their confidence, opens a homecare conversation, and makes them feel like more than just a client.
