Chapter 1: Why AI Is the Plumber's Business

Edge Most plumbing businesses run almost entirely on reactive marketing — customers call when something breaks. That model works, but it leaves money on the table every single day: estimates that never convert, emergency customers who never become

maintenance clients, reviews that never get requested, and slow weeks that could have been filled with proactive outreach. AI fixes all of that.

• The Estimate Conversion Gap: The average plumber converts 40-60% of

estimates — meaning 40-60% of the time you spent diagnosing and quoting resulted in zero revenue. A systematic follow-up sequence recovers a significant portion of those.

• The Emergency-to-Recurring Opportunity: Every emergency call is an

opportunity to turn a one-time customer into a recurring drain maintenance, water heater check, or annual inspection client. Most plumbers never make that offer — because they never have a system for it.

• The Google Review Machine: Plumbers with 50+ Google reviews get called

first. Full stop. The phone doesn't lie — high-review contractors get more calls, better leads, and can charge more. AI writes the review request that actually gets sent.

• The Seasonal Revenue Opportunity: Winterization campaigns, water heater

flush reminders, spring drain cleaning — plumbers who run proactive campaigns fill slow weeks before they become slow. AI writes the campaign in minutes.

Chapter 3: Converting Estimates and Filling Your

Schedule The most profitable plumbing businesses don't just wait for the phone to ring. They have systems that fill slow weeks proactively.

• The 3-Message Estimate Follow-Up: Day 2 check-in, Day 5 value-add

(financing, warranty info, what happens if they wait), Day 14 final outreach. Three messages recovers significantly more jobs than zero.

• The Seasonal Outreach Calendar: Winter: pipe freeze prevention, water heater

checks. Spring: drain cleaning, outdoor plumbing inspection. Fall: winterization, sump pump check. AI writes all four campaigns — your customer list receives them automatically.

• The Lapsed Customer Win-Back: For customers who haven't called in 2+

years, AI writes a win-back message tied to a seasonal offer or a system age check: 'Your water heater from [year] is now [X] years old — here's what to watch for.'

• The Referral Neighbor Drop: After every job, the neighboring 5-6 homes get a

door hanger or a text: 'We just helped your neighbor at [street]. If you've been meaning to get [drain/water heater/pipes] checked, now's a great time.' AI writes it. Your tech delivers it.

Reactive Industry The plumbing business runs on emergencies — but the most profitable plumbing companies convert emergencies into recurring relationships.

• The Annual Maintenance Plan: Offer customers a simple annual plan: drain

Chapter 5: Reviews, Referrals, and Local

Dominance In plumbing, local trust is everything. The contractor with the most reviews and the fastest response gets the call — especially for emergencies.

• The Post-Job Review System: Every job, every time. A personal text within 24

hours asking for a Google review. The link goes directly to your GBP. Contractors who do this consistently become the most-reviewed plumber in their market within 12-18 months.

• The Referral Program: Happy customers refer. They just need to be reminded

and rewarded. AI writes the referral ask and — if you offer a referral incentive — the thank-you confirmation when they send someone your way.

Chapter 6: Content That Builds Trust and Drives

Calls Plumbers who post educational content on Google Business Profile and Facebook consistently get more calls from better customers — because trust is established before they even dial.

• What Actually Works for Plumbers: Before/after job photos (the simpler the

better — burst pipe repaired, water heater replaced, drain cleared), 'is this a real emergency?' educational posts that save customers from panic, seasonal tips, and honest pricing content ('what does a water heater replacement actually cost in [city]?').

• Short-Form Video: 30-60 second videos explaining common plumbing issues

perform exceptionally well. 'Why your toilet keeps running,' 'What that dripping sound means,' 'How to shut off your water in an emergency.' AI writes the script. Your phone records it.

• Google Business Profile: For plumbers, GBP is non-negotiable. Weekly posts,

responding to every review, keeping photos current, and maintaining accurate service area information drives significant organic call volume.

• The Emergency Content Hook: 'If [this] is happening right now, here's what to

do while you wait for a plumber' — this type of content drives emergency calls directly to you. Customers search for the problem, find your content, and call you.
