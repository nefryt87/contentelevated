SECTION 1: POSITIONING

HVAC is a trust business. Homeowners are inviting you into their homes, often during a

stressful breakdown, and handing over thousands of dollars. Your brand — your truck,

your uniform, your website, your Google profile — communicates whether you're

someone worth trusting before they ever speak to you.

Your Brand Promise

We help [homeowners / property managers / local businesses] in [City/area] [stay

comfortable year-round / avoid system breakdowns / maximize the life of their

HVAC investment] through [honest, reliable, expert service with no surprises].

Example: 'We keep [City] families comfortable year-round — with expert HVAC

service, honest pricing, and a guarantee that the job is done right the first time.'

Service Positioning Angles

Residential comfort and reliability: 'Your family deserves to be comfortable —

whatever the weather outside'

Emergency service: 'When your system fails, we answer the phone and show up

fast'

Energy efficiency: 'Lower energy bills without replacing your system — expert

efficiency upgrades'

Service agreements: 'Prevent the breakdown before it happens — proactive

maintenance saves you money'

New construction: 'Reliable HVAC for homebuilders who can't afford callbacks'

Commercial: 'Keeping your business comfortable so your team can focus on

what matters'

The Door Hanger Play

After every job, leave door hangers on the 5-6 nearest neighbors

Message: 'We just serviced your neighbor at [street/neighborhood] — if you

haven't had your system checked this [season], now's a great time'

AI writes the message. Canva formats it. Your tech leaves it. One of the highest-

ROI local marketing tactics in home services.
