Template 1: Before & After Lash Post

Search 'before after beauty Instagram' or 'eye transformation post'. Two-panel: bare eye on left, lashed eye on right. Minimal text: your studio name and the lash style used. This

is your most important content type — it proves results instantly. Create one for every style you offer (classic, hybrid, volume, lift) and rotate them throughout your content calendar.

Template 2: Lash Style Comparison Carousel

Search 'comparison carousel Instagram' or 'beauty guide slides'. 4-5 slides comparing classic vs. hybrid vs. volume lashes. Each slide: style name, brief description, who it suits, a relevant photo. Final slide: your booking CTA. These save at extremely high

rates — potential clients use them to decide which style to book.

Template 3: Lash Education Graphic

Search 'eye tutorial graphic' or 'beauty education Instagram post'. A single educational fact or tip about lash care. 'Oily skin and lash retention: here is what I do differently.'

Clean, informative, easy to read at a glance. Share regularly — these build authority and trust over time.

Template 4: Instagram Story — Availability Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'beauty appointment story'. Vertical. A soft lash close-up photo as background. Dark or light overlay panel. 'LASH FILLS AVAILABLE [MONTH]'. Your DM or booking link. Post every time you open new fill slots — at least weekly.

Template 5: Testimonial Graphic

Search 'client review beauty' or 'testimonial quote lash'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a review — something about their morning routine changing, their

confidence, or their reaction to seeing the results. First name below. Your studio name. Schedule one per week.

Template 6: TikTok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'beauty TikTok thumbnail' or 'Reel first frame template'. For the first frame of every video: your hook text in large font ('I tried mega volume for the first time', 'The

Template 7: Lash Aftercare Card

Search 'beauty aftercare card' or 'client card template'. Design a digital (and optionally printable) aftercare reference card: the do's and don'ts for the first 24-48 hours, ongoing

care, what's normal to expect. Share via email and stories after every appointment. Clients who follow aftercare have better retention and better outcomes.

Template 8: Instagram Highlight Covers

Search 'highlight cover beauty' or 'lash studio icons'. Create 8 round covers: Portfolio, Before/After, Reviews, Services, Aftercare, Booking, About Me, Brows (if you offer brow services). Consistent palette. First thing profile visitors see.
