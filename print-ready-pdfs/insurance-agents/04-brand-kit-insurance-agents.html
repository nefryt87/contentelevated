BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a professional, consistent brand that earns trust before you say a word.

SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING

Your brand is not your logo. It's how people feel when they interact with you. For insurance agents, trust is the product. Everything visual should reinforce that you are reliable, clear, and human. Your Brand Promise (Fill in the Blank)

I help [target client] protect [what matters most to them] so they can [emotional benefit — e.g., 'sleep at night knowing their family is covered']. Example: 'I help young families protect their home and income so they can focus

on building their life — not worrying about what could go wrong.'

Brand Personality (Pick 2–3)

• Trustworthy — You do what you say

• Approachable — No jargon, no pressure

• Expert — You know your stuff and share it freely

• Local — You're part of the community

• Proactive — You reach out before clients have to ask

SECTION 5: LOGO & HEADSHOT GUIDELINES

Headshot Best Practices For insurance agents, your headshot IS your brand. It's the first thing people see on Google, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Follow these guidelines:

• Background: Solid color or clean office setting — no busy backgrounds

• Outfit: Business casual or business professional — avoid busy patterns

• Expression: Genuine smile — approachable, not stiff

• Lighting: Natural light from a window or a simple ring light — avoid harsh

overhead lighting

• Framing: Head and shoulders, slightly off-center — professional but not

passport-photo stiff

• Consistency: Use the SAME headshot across all platforms for brand recognition
