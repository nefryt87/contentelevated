HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your patient's name, condition, and practice

details.

• Keep the tone warm, encouraging, and clinically authoritative — patients

trust practitioners who sound like they care.

• Works as emails, patient portal messages, and SMS — shorten to 2–3

sentences for texts.

• Always personalize the first line — AI does this in seconds.

• Save top performers in your practice management system (WebPT,

Clinicient, etc.) for fast reuse.
