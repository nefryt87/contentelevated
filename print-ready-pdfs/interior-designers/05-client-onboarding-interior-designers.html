SCRIPT — FEE PUSHBACK CONVERSATION

YOU: I hear you — it's more than you were expecting. Can I ask: is it the total number, or is it a question of what's included? [Let them answer. The reason shapes your response.] [If total number:]

YOU: One thing that sometimes helps is approaching the project in phases. We could start with [Phase 1 — e.g., 'the main living areas'] at a reduced fee of $[amount], and treat the rest as a second phase. That way you see the process and the results before
