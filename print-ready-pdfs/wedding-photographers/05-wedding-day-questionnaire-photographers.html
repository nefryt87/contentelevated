SECTION 2: WEDDING DAY QUESTIONNAIRE

Send this 6-8 weeks before the wedding. The more detail you have, the less you have to ask on the day — and the more invisibly you can work.

✓ LOGISTICS

✓ Final ceremony start time:

✓ Final reception start time:

✓ Address of getting-ready location:

✓ Expected finish time for photography coverage:

✓ Is there a venue curfew or noise restriction that affects timing?

✓ PEOPLE

✓ Names of both partners (and correct spelling for any cards, albums, or social posts):

✓ Names of bridal party members:

✓ Who is the best person to coordinate family formals with you on the day?

✓ Are there any family members with mobility limitations we should know about for

group photos?

✓ Are there any family dynamics (separated parents, estrangements) I should be

aware of? (confidential)

✓ MOMENTS

✓ Ceremony: Are there any specific vows or readings you want captured closely?

✓ First dances: List all first dances and who they are with:

✓ Speeches: Who is speaking and approximately how many?

✓ DETAILS

✓ Are there any specific detail items you want photographed? (heirlooms, custom

elements, meaningful gifts)

✓ Is there a wedding hashtag?

✓ Any other venue restrictions I should know about? (no flash in ceremony, restricted

areas)
