Brand Kit + Canva Template Guide

Two signature palettes, refined typography, 8 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND POSITIONS YOU BEFORE

YOUR PORTFOLIO DOES

Couples booking high-end wedding photographers make their shortlist based on brand and first impression before they deeply examine the portfolio. A cohesive visual identity communicates: this photographer is professional, consistent, and worth their fee. This

kit gives you two complete palettes, typography pairings, and Canva template strategies built specifically for wedding photography.

8 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Wedding Feature Carousel

Search 'wedding photography carousel' or 'wedding story Instagram'. 8-10 slides: cover (couple names + date), 6-7 wedding photos in consistent colour treatment, final slide with your name and CTA. Use same font and colour overlay throughout. Save as your

master carousel template.

Template 2: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'testimonial quote wedding' or 'couple review Instagram'. Feature the most emotional line from a review. Couple's first names below. Your studio name and Instagram handle in corner. Blush or Warm Cream background works best.

Template 3: Pinterest Wedding Board Cover

Search 'Pinterest board cover minimalist'. Square format. One per board: 'Ceremonies', 'Couple Portraits', 'Getting Ready Details', 'Receptions', 'Venues', 'Engagement Sessions'. Consistent palette across all boards. Serif font for board name.

Template 4: Instagram Story — Inquiry CTA

Search 'Instagram story booking' or 'photography enquiry story'. Vertical. Full-bleed couple photo as background. Semi-transparent overlay panel. Text: 'Now booking [year] weddings' + 'swipe up / link in bio'. Your studio name bottom of overlay.

Template 5: Educational Tip Graphic

Search 'Instagram tip post' or 'educational carousel photography'. Clean layout with a headline question ('Should you do a first look?'), 3-4 answer points, your name bottom right. These save and share more than portfolio posts.

Template 6: Venue Spotlight Graphic

Search 'location feature Instagram' or 'venue photography post'. Feature a specific venue: one striking image, venue name in elegant type, your studio name. Pin-friendly format — also works as a Pinterest graphic with keyword description.

Template 7: LinkedIn / Facebook Professional Banner

Search 'photographer LinkedIn banner' or 'professional Facebook cover'. 1584×396px for LinkedIn, 820×312px for Facebook. Your best image, your name, your location, your website. Clean and professional — this is for your vendor network, not couples.

Template 8.
