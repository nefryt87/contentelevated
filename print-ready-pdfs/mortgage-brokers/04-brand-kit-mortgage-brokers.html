BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a brand that earns trust before the first conversation. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • 7-day launch checklist

SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING

Borrowers don't just choose the lowest rate — they choose the lender they trust most to get them to the closing table. Your brand communicates that trust before you ever speak. Your Brand Promise

I help [target client — e.g., first-time buyers / move-up buyers / self-employed professionals] [achieve outcome — e.g., get into their first home / access the equity they've built] without [pain point — e.g., the confusion and stress that

usually comes with it]. Example: 'I help first-time buyers in [City] get into their home with confidence — no confusing jargon, no surprises at the closing table.'

Brand Personality Traits (Pick 2–3)

• Trustworthy — You deliver what you promise

• Transparent — No hidden fees, no confusing fine print

• Responsive — You answer calls and return messages

• Expert — You explain complex things simply

• Local — You know this market like no national lender can

SECTION 2: COLOR PALETTES

Mortgage branding should feel stable, credible, and approachable. Avoid aggressive colors (red, orange) that trigger caution. Lean into navy, deep teal, warm white, and gold.

SECTION 3: TYPOGRAPHY

USE FONT NOTES

Headlines Playfair Display Bold Conveys authority and sophistication — perfect for mortgage professionals. Free on Google Fonts. Subheadings Raleway SemiBold Clean, modern, builds hierarchy without competing with headlines.

Body Copy Source Sans Pro Highly legible at all sizes — great for digital and print. CTA / Emphasis Raleway Bold Use for buttons, CTAs, and key stats only — sparingly.
