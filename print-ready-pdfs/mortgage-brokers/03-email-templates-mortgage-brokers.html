HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your borrower's name, loan type, and your

NMLS/company info.

• The tone is intentionally warm and conversational — resist making them

more formal.

• Personalize the opening line for highest response rates — AI can do this in

reuse.
