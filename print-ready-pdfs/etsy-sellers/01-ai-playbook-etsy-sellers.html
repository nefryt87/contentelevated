ETSY SELLER

The AI Playbook for Etsy Sellers Optimise your listings for search, convert more browsers into buyers, generate consistent 5-star reviews, and build the social presence that drives traffic to your shop

INTRODUCTION

The Etsy sellers generating consistent sales and growing year-on-year aren't just the most talented makers or designers. They write listings that Etsy's search algorithm surfaces and that browsers can't scroll past. They respond to customer messages within

hours in a way that turns hesitant browsers into confident buyers. They follow up after every order to generate the 5-star reviews that build social proof. They run seasonal campaigns and sales that create urgency at the right moments. And they post

consistently on Pinterest and Instagram in ways that drive external traffic directly to their shop. This playbook gives you 7 chapters covering every high-leverage growth scenario — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

CHAPTER 1: ETSY SEO & LISTING

OPTIMISATION

How Etsy Search Actually Works Etsy's search algorithm — called Etsy Search — ranks listings based on a combination of keyword relevance, listing quality score, recency, customer experience score, and

shipping price. Understanding this means you can systematically improve every listing to rank higher and convert better.

PROMPT — ETSY LISTING TITLE OPTIMISATION

Write 5 optimised Etsy listing title options for my product: [describe your product in detail — material, style, size, what it is, who it's for]. My current title is: [paste current

title if you have one]. I want titles that: (1) lead with the most searched keywords, (2) include specific descriptors buyers use when searching (material, style, occasion, recipient), (3) are under 140 characters, (4) feel natural to read. Include a mix of

approaches — some leading with the product type, some leading with the occasion or recipient. Provide each title on its own line.

CHAPTER 3: REVIEW GENERATION SYSTEM

The Post-Purchase Follow-Up System 5-star reviews are the single most powerful growth lever on Etsy. Listings with more reviews rank higher in search, convert at higher rates, and attract repeat buyers. Most

sellers never systematically ask for reviews. The ones who do grow significantly faster.

PROMPT — POST-DELIVERY FOLLOW-UP MESSAGE

Write a post-delivery follow-up Etsy message for my shop. The customer [Name] ordered [product] and it should have arrived approximately [X days ago]. I want to: (1) check in and make sure they received it and are happy, (2) provide any relevant

care or use instructions briefly, (3) make a warm, personal ask for a review — explain why it matters to a small shop, (4) invite them to contact me if anything isn't perfect. Warm and genuine. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Shop Name].

PROMPT — PACKAGING INSERT / THANK YOU CARD TEXT

Write the text for a printed thank-you card to include with every Etsy order. I want it to: (1) thank them personally for their purchase, (2) mention my shop name, (3) make a warm ask for a 5-star review with a reason why reviews matter to small

makers, (4) invite them to share a photo on Instagram tagging [your handle], (5) let them know I'm always available if anything isn't perfect. Warm, personal, and brief — fits on a small card. Max 100 words. Shop name: [Shop Name].
