SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING

The most booked MUAs aren't necessarily the most skilled — they're the most clearly positioned and consistently visible. Your brand communicates who you are, who you serve, and why you're the only choice before a client ever reaches out.

Your Beauty Brand Promise I help [client type — e.g., brides / women of color / editorial models / prom queens] feel [emotional outcome — e.g., confident / timeless / bold / like the most beautiful

version of themselves] through [your approach — e.g., luxurious, long-wear makeup tailored to their features and vision]. Example: 'I help brides in [City] feel effortlessly beautiful on their wedding day —

with a look they'll love in photos for the rest of their life.' Niche Options — The More Specific, The More Bookable

• Mature skin and age-positive beauty

• Airbrush specialist

• Bold, avant-garde, and creative looks

• On-location and destination weddings
