BRIDAL HAIR & MAKEUP ARTIST

Brand Kit + Canva Template Guide

Two signature palettes, editorial typography, 8 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND BOOKS BRIDES BEFORE

YOU SPEAK TO THEM

Brides decide on their shortlist of hair and makeup artists based almost entirely on Instagram — before they ever send a message. Your brand is the first impression they form, and it either communicates 'this artist is premium, trusted, and right for me' or it

doesn't. A cohesive visual identity signals taste, professionalism, and a level of quality that justifies your fees. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva

template strategies built specifically for bridal hair and makeup artists.

Typography Pairing

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — the definitive bridal luxury font. Editorial, romantic, instantly premium. BODY: Jost — clean geometric sans-serif that balances the decorative heading perfectly.

ACCENT: Pinyon Script or Alex Brush — for names, taglines, quote cards. Use sparingly for maximum impact.

Typography Pairing

HEADINGS: Playfair Display — a sophisticated serif with editorial weight. Widely used across luxury wedding brands. BODY: Nunito — friendly and warm. Reads beautifully on mobile, which is where most brides discover you.

ACCENT: Josefin Sans Light (wide tracking, caps) — for labels, price presentation, and clean overlay text.

Template 1: Bridal Reveal Carousel

Search 'wedding photography carousel' or 'bridal slideshow Instagram'. 8-10 slides: cover slide with a strong atmospheric image and your studio name, 6-7 photos showing the full look from multiple angles, final slide with your Instagram handle and booking

CTA. Save as your master carousel template and apply to every wedding.

Template 2: Before & After Transformation

Search 'before after makeup Instagram' or 'beauty transformation post'. Two-panel layout. Left: bride before (or arriving — with permission). Right: finished look. Your studio name and 'Bride: [Name, with permission]' at bottom. These generate more DMs

than any other single post type.

Template 3: Client Testimonial Quote Graphic

Search 'wedding testimonial quote' or 'bride review graphic'. Feature the most emotional line from a review. 'Bride: [First Name], [Month Year]' below. Your studio name in corner. These build trust before any message is sent.

Template 4: Instagram Story — Inquiry CTA

Search 'Instagram story booking' or 'beauty booking story'. Vertical. A soft atmospheric photo as background (getting ready flat lay or close-up makeup). Semi-transparent overlay panel. Text: 'Now booking [season] weddings' + '[X] dates remaining'. Your

name and handle. Link sticker.

Template 5: Pinterest Bridal Board Cover

Search 'Pinterest board cover elegant'. Square format, one per board: 'Bridal Makeup', 'Bridal Hair', 'Getting Ready Details', 'Bridesmaid Beauty', 'Real Weddings'. Consistent

palette. A well-organised Pinterest profile with branded covers appears more

professional and is more likely to be followed.

Template 6: Instagram Highlight Covers

Search 'Instagram highlight cover bridal' or 'beauty highlight icons'. Create 8 round covers: Brides, Bridesmaids, Reviews, Trial Runs, Behind the Scenes, Booking, About Me, Contact. These are the first thing profile visitors see — make them on-brand and

instantly clear.

Template 7: Educational Tip Graphic

Search 'beauty tip Instagram' or 'skin care carousel post'. '5 Things to Do Before Your Bridal Trial' or 'How to Prep Your Skin for Wedding Day Makeup'. Clean, readable, shareable. These save at extremely high rates — brides bookmark them and come back.

Template 8: TikTok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'TikTok text overlay' or 'Reel thumbnail template'. For the first frame of every video: your key message in large text, your studio name small at bottom, your brand colour background or a soft atmospheric photo. Viewers see this before pressing play

— make it irresistible.
