SECTION 1: NANNY RATE GUIDE

Understanding and Setting Your Rate

EXPERIENCE LEVEL TYPICAL RATE

(USD/HR)

WHAT GETS YOU HERE

Entry Level / New Nanny $15 — $20 1-2 years experience, basic first aid, comfortable with a single child. Experienced Nanny $20 — $28 3-5 years, first aid certified, experience with multiple ages, strong references.

Professional Nanny $28 — $38 5+ years, early childhood qualification or equivalent, stellar references, speciality experience. Specialist / Senior Nanny $38 — $50+ SEN experience, newborn specialist, bilingual, Montessori trained, or nanny manager role.

Rates vary by location — major metro areas (NYC, SF, LA, London) command 20- 40% higher rates than national averages. Always research your local market on Care.com, UrbanSitter, and local nanny Facebook groups. Your rate is a floor, not a

ceiling — negotiate upward based on your unique value.

SECTION 3: INTERVIEW QUESTION

FRAMEWORK

Questions Nannies Should Ask Families

✓ ABOUT THE CHILDREN:

✓ What are your children's current favourite activities?

✓ Do they have any specific routines I should know about?

✓ How do they typically respond to new adults?

✓ Are there any developmental areas you are currently focusing on?

✓ How do they handle transitions?

✓

✓ ABOUT THE ROLE:

✓ What does a typical day look like in this role?

✓ What are the most important qualities you are looking for in a nanny?

✓ Have you had a nanny before? What worked well? What did not?

✓ How much sole charge would I have?

✓ What is your approach to screen time, diet, and discipline?

✓

✓ ABOUT PRACTICAL MATTERS:

✓ What are the exact hours and days required?

✓ Is there any flexibility, and how would overtime be handled?

✓ What is your approach to holiday and sick leave?
