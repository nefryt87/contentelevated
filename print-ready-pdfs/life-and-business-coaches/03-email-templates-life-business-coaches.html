25 done-for-you templates for every stage of the coaching relationship Lead nurture • Enrollment • Retention • Launch • Referrals

HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your client's name, goals, and your coaching

program details.

• The tone is intentionally warm, direct, and human — coaches who sound

like humans get better responses than coaches who sound like marketers.

• Personalize the first line of every email using AI — it takes 30 seconds and

dramatically improves open and response rates.

• These work as emails, Voxer messages, and WhatsApp/text follow-ups —

adapt length for the medium.

• Save your top performers as email templates in Gmail or your CRM for fast

reuse.
