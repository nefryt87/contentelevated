# Content Elevated Design Lab

This folder is for design exploration before assets are committed to the live storefront.

## Priority Assets

1. Logo system
   - Primary mark: premium CE monogram, not a generic sparkle/star.
   - Wordmark: CONTENT ELEVATED in a luxury tech style.
   - Small icon: favicon/social avatar version that still reads at tiny sizes.

2. Hero image
   - Goal: cinematic product-suite visual that immediately communicates premium AI growth systems.
   - Direction: dark editorial desk, floating bundle mockups, gold rim light, subtle blue tech glow, real package imagery composited into a high-end brand scene.
   - Avoid: generic stock laptop, random AI robot imagery, cluttered collage, unreadable tiny product covers.

3. About image
   - Goal: position Content Elevated as a premium digital growth studio.
   - Direction: abstract studio command center, strategic documents, product systems, polished interface layers, warm luxury lighting.
   - Avoid: founder photo placeholder unless a real founder image is supplied.

## Storefront UX Decision

The homepage should stay curated:

- Feature 6 high-intent bundles.
- Let category pages handle full catalog discovery.
- Product cards should open internal product pages.
- Product pages should own the checkout CTA and Payhip trust line.

## Working Rule

Draft visual concepts here first, then move only approved assets into the website.
