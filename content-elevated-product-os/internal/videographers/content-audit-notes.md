# Videographers Content Audit Notes

## 2026-05-26 Flagship Rebuild Notes

Videographers is now treated as a flagship product lane.

## Original Issue

The existing visual direction had strong cinematic energy, but the generated source was not launch-ready. Several pages contained extraction fragments and awkward card splits, including prompt endings isolated into their own cards.

Example issue:

- A full inquiry prompt was split, leaving a standalone fragment like `[Studio Name].`

## What Changed

- Rebuilt the full buyer-facing source set from scratch.
- Added a new `Start Here - Videographer Growth System` guide.
- Expanded prompt count and template/system count.
- Reframed the product around wedding, corporate, and commercial videography.
- Rewrote the AI Playbook to cover inquiry conversion, package strategy, pre-production, vendor relationships, content, delivery, reviews, and implementation.
- Split the 90-day content calendar into 3 monthly files.
- Rebuilt communication templates around inquiry, booking, package, production, delivery, reviews, partnerships, and commercial development.
- Rebuilt the Brand Kit with cinematic dark and modern commercial palettes.
- Rebuilt the Questionnaire and Business Development Kit.
- Rebuilt the lead magnet with 5 strong AI prompts.

## Design Direction

Cinematic modern creator/studio aesthetic:

- deep black and charcoal foundations
- electric blue/cyan/violet accents
- strong editorial uppercase headlines
- clean light working pages for readability
- dark feature pages for cinematic mood
- restrained panels and export-safe card density

Avoid:

- generic wedding-template styling
- fake luxury gold overload
- broken prompt cards
- dense fixed pages that can collide in PDF export

## Launch Status

Source is rebuilt and packaged. Needs external PDF export, validation, visual contact-sheet review, and human proof before Payhip upload.
