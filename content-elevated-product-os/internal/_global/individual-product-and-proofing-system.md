# Individual Product And Proofing System

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Use this when turning bundle assets into standalone Payhip products and website pages.

## Purpose

Content Elevated currently sells complete growth bundles on Payhip. Later, individual assets can become lower-priced standalone products:

- 90-day content calendars
- AI playbooks
- Prompt libraries
- Brand kits
- Email / SMS / communication templates
- Client intake, onboarding, retention, referral, and rebooking systems
- Calculators and spreadsheets
- Lead magnets

The goal is to reuse the existing product library without re-reading the whole chat or rebuilding strategy from scratch.

## Source Of Truth

1. Product folder: `rebranded-products-sample-direction/{slug}/`
2. Bundle inventory: `content-elevated-product-os/data/bundle-inventory.json`
3. Product master tracker: `content-elevated-product-os/data/product-master.csv`
4. Payhip copy fields: `content-elevated-product-os/internal/{slug}/listing-copy.md`
5. Website copy fields: `content-elevated-product-os/internal/{slug}/website-product-copy.md`

## Standalone Listing Structure

Each standalone product should get an internal file at:

`content-elevated-product-os/internal/{slug}/standalone-products.md`

Use this structure for every item:

1. Product name
2. Product type
3. Target buyer
4. Short promise
5. What is included
6. Page count
7. Prompt/template/spreadsheet count
8. Best use case
9. Payhip short description
10. Payhip long description
11. Website card description
12. SEO title
13. SEO meta description
14. Suggested price range
15. Upload files
16. Notes / dependencies

## Copy Rules

- Be specific about what the buyer receives: number of PDFs, pages, spreadsheets, templates, prompts, and systems.
- Do not overpromise business outcomes.
- Use niche language, not generic AI-tool language.
- Always mention that files are digital downloads.
- If templates contain placeholders, describe them as customizable.
- Avoid claims like guaranteed clients, guaranteed revenue, guaranteed bookings, or guaranteed growth.
- Use “designed to help,” “built to support,” “helps you,” and “gives you a system for” instead of absolute claims.

## Proofing Workflow

For each product or standalone asset:

1. Mechanical scan
   - Check weird glyphs, placeholders, internal/admin wording, UK spellings, broken page counts, and risky claims.
2. Content scan
   - Check titles, page order, repeated sections, duplicated prompts, missing introductions, and inconsistent product names.
3. Niche accuracy scan
   - Confirm the content fits the business model, customer journey, and buyer expectations for that niche.
4. Readability scan
   - Check dark pages, small text, crowded footers, overly decorative sections, and mobile/print previews where relevant.
5. Listing scan
   - Confirm Payhip and website descriptions match the actual files and inventory counts.

## Research Workflow

When deeper research is needed for a niche:

1. Research the buyer journey and common services for that niche.
2. Research common marketing channels and client objections.
3. Research compliance-sensitive language if the niche touches health, legal, finance, childcare, insurance, or medical claims.
4. Convert findings into editing notes, not long research dumps.
5. Apply only practical improvements to copy, templates, prompts, and listing language.

Use citations in a separate internal research note when internet research is performed.

## Launch Priority

1. Finish bundle PDF export and Payhip re-upload for Phase 1.
2. Add/confirm Phase 1 website product data.
3. Finish next approved batch upload package.
4. Review parked samples and apply approved designs.
5. Create standalone product listings from the strongest assets.
6. Build launch campaigns for bundles first, then standalone products as entry offers.

## Reusable Standalone Product Ideas

These are likely best as first standalone products because they are easy to understand and useful without the full bundle:

- 90-day content calendar
- AI playbook
- Client onboarding kit
- Rebooking / retention templates
- Brand kit
- Prompt library
- Profit or pricing calculator where available

## Required Before Publishing Standalone Products

- Confirm the exact file count and page count from inventory.
- Export final PDFs.
- Create standalone cover image.
- Write Payhip listing copy.
- Add website product/card data.
- Confirm whether the standalone product should link to an upsell for the full bundle.
