# Credit-Efficient Production SOP

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Use this SOP whenever working on Content Elevated so the project keeps moving toward sales without repeating expensive analysis.

## Decision Rule

If the task does not directly help one of these outcomes, park it:

1. Upload upgraded products to Payhip.
2. Connect Payhip products to the website.
3. Improve product listing conversion.
4. Fix a quality issue that would hurt trust or sales.

## Fast Resume Flow

1. Read `launch-command-center.md`.
2. Read `work-session-checkpoint.md` only if a detail is missing.
3. Open the specific batch board for the current workstream.
4. Avoid opening individual product files until the board says which file needs work.

## Batch Strategy

Phase 1 is the sales unlock. Do not start another big creative pass until Phase 1 is export-proofed and ready for Payhip.

Next approved batch is second. It already has approved visual direction and clean audits.

Standard queue is third. It is packaged and mechanically clean, but still needs final proofing, price confirmation, and some Payhip URL/cover confirmations.

## Review Strategy

Use three levels of review:

- Automated scan: catches mechanical issues such as placeholders, internal labels, broken glyphs, and risky claims.
- Representative visual proof: inspect one full exported product from a design family.
- Spot-check proof: inspect first page, one interior page, and final page for the remaining products in that family.

## What To Avoid

- Do not re-open all files to “understand the project.”
- Do not redesign approved products without a specific issue.
- Do not build standalone products until bundle uploads are operational.
- Do not perform deep industry research for every niche before sales launch.
- Do not pause for approval if the task is cleanup, packaging, syncing, or documentation.

## End Of Session Checklist

- Rebuild any affected package/audit/handoff.
- Update `work-session-checkpoint.md` if status changed.
- Update `launch-command-center.md` if priority changed.
- Sync to `/Users/tomasz/Documents/Codex/Content-Elevated`.
- Leave the next action obvious.
