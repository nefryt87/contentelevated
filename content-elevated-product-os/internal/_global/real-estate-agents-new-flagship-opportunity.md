## Design Direction

Premium real estate, not generic broker stock.

Visual lane:

- Architectural editorial
- Bright luxury interiors
- Clean off-white, charcoal, soft blue-gray, warm stone, muted green
- Crisp typography
- Premium listing presentation energy
- Minimal iconography: key, floor plan, home, calendar, message, map pin, handshake

Avoid:

- Fake gold overload
- Cartoon houses
- Busy real estate flyer look
- Generic smiling agent stock-photo clutter

## Compliance / Claims Notes

Avoid promising income, transactions, lead volume, or listing wins.

Use language like:

- designed to help
- built to support
- templates for
- systems to improve consistency
- follow-up assets

Avoid:

- guaranteed listings
- guaranteed leads
- double your closings
- compliant MLS/advertising claims unless verified
