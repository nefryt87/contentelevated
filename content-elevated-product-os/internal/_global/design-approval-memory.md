## Standing Design Rules

- Each niche needs its own aesthetic. Do not use one universal template across the catalog.
- Avoid generic concept docs; use a real sample document with real content.
- No visible `Print-ready HTML` or similar production-language labels in final customer-facing files.
- Avoid weird decorative graphics on cover pages unless they genuinely fit the niche.
- Make footer/page-number areas breathe; do not let border lines crowd footer text.
- Prioritize readability over visual complexity.
