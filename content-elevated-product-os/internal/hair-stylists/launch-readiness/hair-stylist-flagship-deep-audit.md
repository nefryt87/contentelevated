## Launch Blockers

### 1. Extraction Artifacts Inside Buyer-Facing Pages - Fixed In Active Hair Source

Some generated pages accidentally turned sentence fragments, footer text, or page numbers into content cards.

Detected examples:

- `5-free-rebooking-text-templates.html`
  - `Conclusion Themselves.`
  - numeric-only script cards
- `90-day-salon-content-calendar-month-2.html`
  - `Why — And What Makes It So Satisfying To Execute Well.`
  - `Client In My Chair Going Forward. Staying Sharp Is A Commitment.`
- `salon-ai-playbook.html`
  - `Content Ideas In Under A Minute...`
  - `Playbook Teaches.`
  - `Prompt Or Custom Instruction...`
  - footer/page content appearing as cards
- `salon-ai-prompt-library.html`
  - footer/page content appears as cards in multiple places

This was the biggest quality issue. A buyer would have noticed it. The active Hair Stylist source package now uses the clean source set and passes the artifact scan.

### 2. Too Many Repeated Production Labels

Repeated labels like `Action Template`, `Customize before use`, and the same studio note appear heavily across the bundle. Some repetition is useful, but right now it can feel generated and less premium.

Recommendation: keep guidance where helpful, but rename section labels to buyer-useful language:

- `Copy + Customize`
- `Use This When`
- `Salon Tip`
- `Client Experience Note`
- `Prompt`
- `Send This`
- `Post This`

### 3. No Flagship Quick-Start Guide Yet - Added

The buyer needs one clean roadmap that tells them what to do in the first 60 minutes, first week, and first 30 days.

Added a new short PDF source:

`Start Here - Hair Stylist Growth System`

Included pages:

1. Cover / first 60 minutes
2. What is included
3. First-hour fast path
4. First-week setup
5. What to open for each business problem
6. Professional-use notes

This will make the bundle feel easier and more premium immediately.

### 4. Calendar Needs Stronger Daily Execution Cues

The calendar has volume, but some entries would be more useful with a consistent structure:

- Platform
- Post format
- Hook
- Caption starter
- Visual direction
- CTA
- Salon pro tip

That structure would make it easier for a busy stylist to use without thinking.

### 5. Missing Compliance / Professional Boundaries Note

Add a simple customer-safe note:

- Get permission before sharing client photos, names, or testimonials.
- Do not make medical/scalp treatment claims beyond your license and local regulations.
- Confirm promo rules, discounts, and policies match your actual salon policies.
- For SMS/email, only message clients who have consented to receive appointment or marketing messages.

This protects the product and makes it feel more professional.
