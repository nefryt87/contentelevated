# Product Internal Folder Template

Use this structure for every product/niche.

## Files

- `payhip-listing-copy.md`: copy pasted into Payhip.
- `website-product-copy.md`: copy used on the storefront product page.
- `launch-marketing-copy.md`: launch posts, email, short video scripts.
- `upload-checklist.md`: exact files to upload, internal files to exclude, Payhip/website tasks.
- `content-audit-notes.md`: proofread notes, content issues, extraction weirdness, final blockers.
- `internal-source-notes.md`: source file notes, internal strategy, pricing ideas, things not meant for buyers.
- `payhip-url-and-price.md`: final live Payhip product link, price, cover status.

## Upload Rule

Only upload buyer-facing product files from `rebranded-products-sample-direction/[slug]/` after checking `upload-checklist.md`.
