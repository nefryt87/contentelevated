# Content Elevated Launch Plan

## Goal

Start selling quickly while building a long-term product system that can scale across Payhip, the website, email, and social content.

## Launch Priorities

### Phase 1: Sell Soon

Pick 5-8 strongest bundles and make them launch-ready first.

Recommended first launch set:

- Hair Stylists
- Med Spas
- Dentists
- Nutritionists
- HVAC Contractors
- Accountants & CPAs
- Wedding Photographers
- Etsy Sellers

### Phase 2: Category Landing Pages

Create category pages or website filters:

- Beauty
- Health & Wellness
- Home Services
- Professional Services
- Creatives & Events
- Coaches & Educators
- E-Commerce

### Phase 3: Content Engine

Weekly content themes:

- Before/after business system transformation
- Niche-specific pain points
- What is inside each bundle
- Founder/build-in-public updates
- Quick prompts from the bundles
- Client workflow breakdowns

## Weekly Marketing Rhythm

- Monday: niche pain point post
- Tuesday: product feature post
- Wednesday: free prompt/template sample
- Thursday: proof/authority post
- Friday: bundle spotlight
- Weekend: founder/building-the-library update

## Sales Assets To Create

- Payhip product descriptions
- Website product pages
- Product mockup screenshots
- Category page copy
- Email welcome sequence
- Launch email sequence
- Instagram carousel templates
- Short-form video scripts
- Free lead magnet landing page

## Minimum Viable Launch

Before public launch, each featured bundle needs:

- Proofread files
- Payhip listing copy
- Correct Payhip checkout URL
- Website product page
- One product mockup/image
- One launch post
- One email
