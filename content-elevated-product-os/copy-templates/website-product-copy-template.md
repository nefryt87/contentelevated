# Website Product Copy Template

## Product Page Hero

- Eyebrow: `AI-Powered Growth Bundle`
- H1: `[Niche] Growth Bundle`
- Subhead: one clear promise tied to the niche.
- Primary CTA: `Get the Bundle`
- Secondary CTA: `See What's Inside`

## Product Summary

One paragraph explaining who it is for, what is inside, and what it helps them do.

## Bundle Inventory

- PDF guides/workbooks:
- Total pages:
- Spreadsheets:
- AI prompts:
- Templates/scripts/systems:

## What's Inside

Lead with concrete product inventory:

- `[X]` polished PDF guides/workbooks
- `[Y]` total pages
- `[Z]` spreadsheets, if included
- `[N]` AI prompts, if detected/confirmed
- `[M]` templates/systems, if detected/confirmed

Then use 5-7 bullets matching the actual files included.

## Why It Works

Four benefit blocks:

- Content system
- AI playbook
- Client conversion templates
- Brand/positioning system

## Who It Is For

Short list of ideal users.

## Product Details

- Format: digital download
- Delivery: Payhip secure checkout
- Access: instant after purchase
- Updates: note only if true

## FAQ

Use the global FAQ, then add niche-specific questions if needed.
