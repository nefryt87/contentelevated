Template 1: Wedding Feature Carousel

Search 'wedding photography carousel' or 'wedding story Instagram'. 8-10 slides: cover (couple names + date), 6-7 wedding photos in consistent colour treatment, final slide with your name and CTA. Use same font and colour overlay throughout. Save as your master carousel template.

03

Studio asset

Template 2: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'testimonial quote wedding' or 'couple review Instagram'. Feature the most emotional line from a review. Couple's first names below. Your studio name and Instagram handle in corner. Blush or Warm Cream background works best.

04

Studio asset

Template 3: Pinterest Wedding Board Cover

Search 'Pinterest board cover minimalist'. Square format. One per board: 'Ceremonies', 'Couple Portraits', 'Getting Ready Details', 'Receptions', 'Venues', 'Engagement Sessions'. Consistent palette across all boards. Serif font for board name.
