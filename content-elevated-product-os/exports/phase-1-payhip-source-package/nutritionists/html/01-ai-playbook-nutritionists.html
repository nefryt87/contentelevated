Competitive Edge The nutrition and dietetics space has never been more crowded — or more in demand. They're the most responsive, the most consistent, and the most visible. AI makes all three effortless. • The Consistency Gap: Clients who hear from their nutritionist or dietitian between sessions stay on track longer, achieve better outcomes, and renew their packages at far higher rates.
