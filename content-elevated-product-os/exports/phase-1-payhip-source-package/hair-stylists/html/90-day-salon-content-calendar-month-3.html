5 Rules for Making This Work

1 Batch your content

Set aside 2 hours each week to write captions for the next 7 days. Schedule with Buffer, Later, or

Meta Business Suite.

2 Adapt, don't copy

These captions are starting points. Add your personality, local references, and client stories to

make them yours.

3 Repurpose everything

Every Reel can become a Story. Every Story can become a caption. Every caption can become a

Facebook post.

4 Track what works

After 30 days, note which post types get the most saves, comments, and DMs. Double down on

those.

5 Your story is the product

Clients don't just book a service. They book a person. The more of yourself you show, the more

they trust you.
