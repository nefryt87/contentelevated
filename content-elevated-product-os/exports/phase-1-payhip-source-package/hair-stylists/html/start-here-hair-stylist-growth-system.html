Professional use

Use it well

Make every asset sound like your chair, not a template.

01

Personalize before sending

Add the client's name, service, timing, and one detail from the appointment whenever possible.

02

Use real availability

Only create urgency when it is true. Trust is more valuable than a forced deadline.

03

Get consent

Ask permission before sharing client photos, names, testimonials, or transformation stories.

04

Stay inside your scope

Keep hair and scalp education practical. Avoid medical claims and follow your local license, salon, and platform rules.

05

Track one number weekly

Choose one metric: rebookings, consultation requests, reviews, referrals, or average ticket. A premium business is built through patterns, not panic.
