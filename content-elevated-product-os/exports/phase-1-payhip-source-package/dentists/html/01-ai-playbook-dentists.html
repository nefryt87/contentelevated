Chapter 1: Why AI Is The Dental Practice's

Growth Engine Many dental practices lose momentum through preventable communication gaps: missed appointments, inactive patients, unscheduled treatment plans, and referrals that never get asked for. These are not only clinical workflow issues. They are patient communication issues, and AI can help the practice respond with more consistency, clarity, and care. • The No-Show Gap: missed appointments create lost chair time and schedule stress. Better reminders, preparation messages, and recovery notes help patients know what to expect and make rebooking easier.

Chapter 2: Your First 3 AI Wins — This Week

Pick one of these and implement it this week. Chapter 3: Reducing No-Shows And Last-Minute

Cancellations No-shows create schedule stress, lost chair time, and a less predictable patient experience. Patients are more likely to arrive prepared when reminders are timely, clear, and specific. The barrier usually is not wanting to fix this — it is having the time to do it consistently. AI helps remove that barrier. • The 3-Touch Reminder System: 72-hour reminder (confirm or reschedule), 24- hour reminder (logistics + anticipation), morning-of reminder (parking, what to bring, what to expect). Each one short, warm, and specific. • The Cancellation Recovery Message: When a patient cancels, most practices just update the schedule.
