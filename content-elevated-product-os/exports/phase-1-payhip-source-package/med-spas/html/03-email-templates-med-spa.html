How to Use

Before You Use It

Every touchpoint should feel cared for.

Customize the brackets, treatment names, provider credentials, policies, contraindications, and local rules before sending anything to a client.

The Rule

Med spa marketing has to feel elevated and medically responsible. Use the system to educate, reassure, and invite the next step - never to overpromise results.

Safety Note

All treatment-specific claims, prep instructions, aftercare language, and review requests should be reviewed by the practice owner or qualified provider.

Safety Language

Safety Language

Use this before sending treatment messages.

01

Provider review line

Add when needed
Please follow the preparation and aftercare guidance provided by your licensed provider. If anything feels unclear or unusual, contact the practice before your appointment.
02

Public review rule

Privacy first
Thank clients for feedback without referencing treatment details, medical history, complications, or outcomes in public replies.
03

Result language rule

No overpromising
Use phrases like 'typical timeline,' 'individual results vary,' and 'we will review your goals during consultation.'
