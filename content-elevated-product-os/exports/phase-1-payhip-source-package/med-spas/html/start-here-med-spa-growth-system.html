How to Use

Before You Use It

Trust is the conversion engine.

Customize the brackets, treatment names, provider credentials, policies, contraindications, and local rules before sending anything to a client.

The Rule

Med spa marketing has to feel elevated and medically responsible. Use the system to educate, reassure, and invite the next step - never to overpromise results.

Safety Note

All treatment-specific claims, prep instructions, aftercare language, and review requests should be reviewed by the practice owner or qualified provider.

Provider Review Checklist

Provider Review Checklist

The buyer-facing safety layer that protects the brand.

01

Treatment prep

Review before use
  • No blanket medical advice.
  • Include 'check with your provider' where appropriate.
  • Match instructions to the actual device, injectable, product, or protocol.
02

Before and after content

Consent required
  • Use written consent.
  • Do not edit results in a misleading way.
  • Avoid claims that every client will see the same outcome.
03

Reviews and testimonials

Privacy first
  • Never mention protected details in public replies.
  • Move unhappy reviews offline.
  • Keep testimonial requests simple and pressure-free.
