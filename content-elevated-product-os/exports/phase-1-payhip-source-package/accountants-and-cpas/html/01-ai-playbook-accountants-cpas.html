Introduction

The CPA practices growing fastest aren't just technically superior — they communicate better. They follow up on consultations before the prospect has time to contact anyone else. They reach out proactively when a tax law changes that affects their clients. They maintain attorney and financial advisor relationships with a monthly value touch that generates referrals year-round. And they show up digitally in ways that make their ideal clients find them before they ever search a competitor. This playbook gives you 7 chapters covering every high-leverage communication scenario — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

03

Client system

Prospect Nurturing

The Post-Consultation Follow-Up System The consultation that doesn't convert in 48 hours rarely converts at all. Most prospects are contacting 2-3 CPAs. The first professional, specific, and action-oriented follow-up wins a disproportionate number of engagements.

04

Client system

Prompt — Post-Consultation Follow-Up Email

Write a post-consultation follow-up email for my CPA practice. The prospect [Name] came in about [matter type — e.g., business tax planning / personal tax return / estate planning / new business setup]. Key things they shared: [list 2-3 things from the consultation]. I want to: (1) summarize their situation in plain language showing I listened, (2) outline my recommended approach in 2-3 sentences, (3) present a clear next step and fee expectation. Professional but warm. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name]. Note: review for compliance with your state bar and professional standards.

Life Events

The Life Event Outreach System CPAs who build lifelong client relationships show up at the right moments — without being asked. Five life events trigger the most valuable tax and planning conversations: marriage, divorce, new child, inheritance, and business sale. Most CPAs wait for clients to call them. The best ones reach out first.

Leadership

LinkedIn Content for CPAs LinkedIn is one of the strongest content platforms for accounting professionals. Your audience — business owners, executives, attorneys, financial advisors — is there daily. One strong LinkedIn post reaches hundreds of qualified prospects and referral sources with zero ad spend.

02

Client system

Prompt — Linkedin Post Batch

Write 5 LinkedIn posts for a CPA specializing in [specialty]. Topics: (1) the most common tax mistake [business owners / high-income individuals] make that costs them money — be specific, (2) a recent IRS or tax law update that affects [your target client type] — explain it in plain language, (3) what advisory-level accounting actually looks like vs. just filing returns, (4) a client outcome story (anonymised) — the situation, what we did, the result, (5) a question post — something your followers genuinely debate. Each under 200 words, educational and specific, ending with a question or insight. Firm name: [Firm Name].
