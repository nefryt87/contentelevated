Irs Notice Triage — What To Tell Clients Immediately

✓ CP2000 — Underreporter inquiry: IRS matching return data. Common, not an audit. Respond within 60 days. ✓ CP503 / CP504 — Balance due notices: Taxes owed. Address promptly to avoid lien. ✓ CP90 / 1058 — Intent to levy: Urgent. Contact client immediately. ✓ Letter 3219 — Notice of deficiency: 90-day letter. Do not ignore. ✓ Letter 525 / 531 — Audit examination: Respond through proper channels. ✓ CP01 / CP01A — Identity protection: Identity theft or PIN issue. ✓ [Rule: Never let clients respond to IRS notices directly without speaking to you first. The response matters as much as the issue.]
