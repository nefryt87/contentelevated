Impression With Every Booking Decision

Event organizers and meeting planners evaluate a speaker's brand before they watch their reel. Your website, your LinkedIn profile, your one-sheet — these communicate your credibility, your positioning, and whether you are the right speaker for their event before they have seen you speak. A polished, cohesive visual identity adds perceived value and justifies premium fees. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for professional speakers.

Template 1: Speaker One-Sheet

Search 'professional speaker one sheet' or 'speaking press kit template'. Single page (A4 / Letter). Your headshot top left. Talk title(s) and brief description(s). 3 key audience outcomes (bullets). Social proof — 2-3 testimonial quotes or client logos. Your bio in 75 words. Booking contact and website. This is the document every organizer asks for — it needs to communicate your entire value proposition in 30 seconds.

02

Speaker asset

Template 2: Linkedin Banner / Background Image

Search 'LinkedIn banner professional speaker' or 'thought leader LinkedIn cover'. 1584×396px. Your best on-stage or professional photo. Your name. Your speaking topic in a punchy phrase: 'Leadership | Resilience | Performance' or 'Helping [audience] to [outcome].' Your website. This is seen by every organizer who clicks your profile.

03

Speaker asset

Template 3: Quote / Insight Graphic

Search 'thought leader quote Instagram' or 'professional insight post'. A compelling one- liner from your talk or your body of work. Your name and speaking topic below. Clean, bold typography. Post this type regularly on LinkedIn and Instagram — these get shared widely when the insight is genuinely striking.

04

Speaker asset

Template 4: Speaking Testimonial Graphic

Search 'speaker testimonial graphic' or 'professional review post'. Feature the most specific and authoritative testimonial you have — include the person's name, title, and company. Your name. These are your most powerful marketing asset. Create one for every new testimonial you receive.
