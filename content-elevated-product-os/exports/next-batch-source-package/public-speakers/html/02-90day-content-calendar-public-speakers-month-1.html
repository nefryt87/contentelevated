How To Use This Calendar

LinkedIn is your primary booking channel — event organizers, HR directors, L&D managers, and conference chairs live there and discover speakers through content. Instagram builds personality and behind-the-scenes connection. YouTube hosts your speaker reel and talk excerpts, which event organizers watch to make booking decisions. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch LinkedIn posts monthly with the AI prompt — they take 30 minutes for a full month's content.
