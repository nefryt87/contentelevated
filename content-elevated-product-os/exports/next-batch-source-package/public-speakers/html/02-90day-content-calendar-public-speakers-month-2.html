03

Speaker asset

Education

Write 8 LinkedIn posts for a professional speaker on [your topic]. Topics: the biggest misconception in your field, a framework or model from your talk, a practical action your audience can take today, an industry trend and your take on it, a story that illustrates your key message, why most [your topic] initiatives fail, what the research shows vs. what most people do, a speaking engagement story. Under 200 words each.
