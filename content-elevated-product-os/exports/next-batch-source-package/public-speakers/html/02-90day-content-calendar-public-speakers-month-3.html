Day Platform Content Idea

Mon LinkedIn Opinion post: 'Hot take on [topic] — I know this is controversial but...' Clear position, invites debate, drives engagement. Wed Instagram 'Something nobody tells you about professional speaking' — behind-the-scenes truth. Relatable for other speakers and aspirational for bookers. Fri LinkedIn 'I'm curious — [question related to your topic]?' — engagement- driving question post. Use responses for future content.

02

Speaker asset

Consolidation

Write 8 LinkedIn posts for a professional speaker. Topics: your availability for the coming season, the ROI of investing in professional speakers, a specific audience or industry you speak well for, your virtual speaking capability, a new talk or updated keynote, a Q4 conference season post, a strong speaking testimonial, a year-in- review or reflection post. Under 200 words each.

Mon LinkedIn 'Now booking Q[X] and [season] speaking engagements — here is what topics and formats I am taking on.' Direct and specific. Fri LinkedIn 'The ROI of investing in a professional keynote speaker — what event organizers tell me they see.' Value education for bookers.
