Section 1: Talk Development Template

Use this framework to develop any new talk from a clear brief. A well-developed talk is far easier to sell, easier to customize, and delivers better audience outcomes. ✓ TALK TITLE: [Working title — change this as the talk evolves] ✓ ✓ THE CORE PREMISE (one sentence): What is the single most important thing your audience will leave knowing? Write it in one sentence. If you cannot, your talk is not yet clear enough. ✓ ✓ THE AUDIENCE PROBLEM: What problem does your audience have that this talk addresses? Be specific — 'leaders who manage hybrid teams and struggle with accountability' is better than 'business leaders'.
