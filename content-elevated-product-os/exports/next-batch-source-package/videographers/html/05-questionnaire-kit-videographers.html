Wedding Questionnaire

Wedding Questionnaire

Capture the emotional priorities.

Use this before final timeline planning.

01

Core details

Client intake

  • Names, date, venue, planner, photographer, ceremony timing.
  • Coverage start/end time.
  • Direct day-of contact who is not the couple.
02

Story priorities

Creative direction

  • Top 3 moments they care about.
  • People who must be included.
  • Vows, speeches, dances, cultural or family traditions.
03

Logistics

Risk control

  • Venue restrictions, drone limits, audio restrictions.
  • Lighting notes, prep locations, travel times.
  • Surprises or sensitive family considerations.

Corporate Brief

Clarify the business objective.

Use this before quoting or pre-production.

Deliverables

Approvals

Workflow

  • Decision maker.
  • Revision rounds.
  • Brand guidelines, legal review, launch date.
