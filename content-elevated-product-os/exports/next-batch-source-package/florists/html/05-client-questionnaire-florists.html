To Keep Your Flowers Looking Beautiful:

• On arrival: trim the stems at a 45-degree angle with sharp scissors or secateurs • Remove any leaves that sit below the waterline — they cause bacterial growth • Place in a clean vase with fresh, cool water • Add the flower food sachet (if provided) to the water

• Top up the water daily — flowers drink a surprising amount
