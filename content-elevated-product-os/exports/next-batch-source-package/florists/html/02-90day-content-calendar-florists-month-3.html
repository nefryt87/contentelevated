Experience asset

Day Platform Content Idea

Mon Instagram Vendor appreciation: tag a wedding planner, photographer, or venue you love working with. Specific and genuine. Wed Pinterest 'Romantic / wild / modern [choose your style] wedding inspiration' — curate a mood board in your aesthetic. Fri Facebook Corporate and event floral post: 'We also create florals for corporate events, product launches, and restaurant and hotel installations. Here is some of our work.' Targets the business audience.

02

Experience asset

Conversion

Write 8 Instagram captions for a florist. Topics: seasonal campaign announcement, a specific occasion or event type you specialise in, the investment framing for wedding florals, a full wedding reveal, gift florals for an occasion, a client testimonial, a subscription or corporate offer, a strong inquiry CTA. Under 150 words each.

03

Experience asset

Day Platform Content Idea

Wed Pinterest Seasonal wedding inspiration board refresh — add 10 new pins with updated keywords for the new season. Fri Facebook '[Season] availability — I have [X] wedding dates remaining this season and limited corporate slots. Here is how to enquire.' CTA.

04

Experience asset

Day Platform Content Idea

Mon Instagram Full wedding reveal carousel: installation photo, individual arrangements, final atmosphere shots. Emotional caption. Wed Pinterest Create a dedicated board for a full wedding — every image, keyword-rich descriptions, link to any relevant content.
