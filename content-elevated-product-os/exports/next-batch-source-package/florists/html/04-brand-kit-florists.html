Template 1: Floral Arrangement Reveal Post

Search 'floral arrangement Instagram' or 'flower photography post'. Your best arrangement photography fills the frame. Minimal text overlay: studio name in small serif type, bottom corner. Clean and editorial. Let the flowers speak. Create 10-15 of these with consistent overlay treatment and your feed will look immediately cohesive and professional.

04

Experience asset

Template 2: Wedding Florals Carousel

Search 'wedding Instagram carousel' or 'floral slideshow'. 8-10 slides: atmosphere shot of the venue, ceremony flowers, bridal bouquet, table arrangements, detail shots, and a final slide with your studio name and inquiry CTA. Use this for every wedding you photograph. It is your most powerful portfolio format.

Template 3: Seasonal Flower Guide Carousel

Search 'flower guide carousel' or 'botanical education slides'. 5-7 slides: one seasonal flower per slide — name, peak season, what style it suits, how long it lasts in water. A genuinely useful resource that saves at very high rates and attracts brides in the research phase.

06

Experience asset

Template 4: Pinterest Wedding Board

Search 'wedding inspiration Pinterest' or 'floral mood board'. Create a board for each season and style you work in: 'Romantic Garden Wedding Florals', 'Modern Architectural Wedding Flowers', 'Wild Meadow Weddings'. Pin your own work and curated inspiration. A well-optimized Pinterest profile drives consistent inquiry traffic for years.
