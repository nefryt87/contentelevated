Chapter 1: Why AI Is The Chiropractor's Practice

Growth Tool Most chiropractors are exceptional at patient care — and overwhelmed by everything else. Appointment reminders, follow-up calls, re-engagement outreach, social media, patient education, review requests — the list never ends. AI doesn't treat patients. But it handles every task that prevents you from focusing on the ones who need treating. • The No-Show Problem: The average chiropractic practice loses 15–20% of appointments to no-shows and last-minute cancellations. AI-powered reminder and reconfirmation messages cut that number significantly.

Referrals are the most cost-effective source of new patients for any chiropractic practice. But most chiropractors never ask — not because they don't want to, but because they don't have a system. AI builds that system. • The Post-Milestone Referral Ask: The best time to ask for a referral is right after a patient hits a meaningful milestone — reduced pain, improved mobility, first full night's sleep. AI writes a milestone celebration + referral ask in one natural message. • The Professional Referral Network: Primary care physicians, physical therapists, personal trainers, and massage therapists are your best referral sources.
