How To Use This Calendar

Instagram is where parents discover and save party planning inspiration — event reveals, theme ideas, and transformation content drive DMs and bookings. Pinterest is the long-tail SEO engine — parents pin party ideas months in advance, and your pins drive consistent discovery. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly.
