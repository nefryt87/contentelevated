How To Use This Calendar

Instagram is your primary booking platform — it's where clients discover you, vet your portfolio, and DM to inquire. TikTok is your fastest reach growth channel and attracts a younger audience actively considering their first or next tattoo. Pinterest drives long-tail SEO discovery — 'blackwork forearm tattoo ideas' and similar searches. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly.

Write 8 Instagram captions for a tattoo artist with a [style] aesthetic. Topics: a piece you're most proud of recently, your journey into tattooing, your specialty and who you work best with, a piece that challenged you and what you learned, your studio environment, your consultation process, aftercare advice, a direct booking CTA.
