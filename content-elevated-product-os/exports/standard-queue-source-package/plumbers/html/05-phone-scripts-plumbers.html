Standard Service Call

03

Operator asset

Dispatcher Script — Standard Inbound

DISPATCHER: [Company Name], this is [Name] — what's going on for you today? [Listen fully. Don't interrupt. Let them describe the problem completely.] DISPATCHER: Got it — that's something we can take care of. Let me get a few details so I can send the right person. [Collect: name, address, callback number, problem, urgency — running water? wet area?] DISPATCHER: We have [time available]. Our plumbers are licensed and insured — they'll call 30 minutes before arrival. Does that work? [Confirm everything. Read back the address.] Emergency Burst Pipe / Active Leak

04

Operator asset

Dispatcher Script — Emergency Leak

DISPATCHER: [Company Name] — sounds like an emergency. Where are you located? [Get address immediately. Water damage is time-sensitive. Every minute matters.] DISPATCHER: First thing — if you can safely reach your main water shutoff, turn it off now. It's usually near the water meter, under a sink, or in the basement. Do you know where yours is? [Guide them through shutoff while dispatching the nearest tech simultaneously] DISPATCHER: I'm reaching out to our nearest tech right now. We'll have someone heading your way within [timeframe]. [Call back within 5 minutes with confirmed ETA.

Section 3: Upsell Conversation Guide

Post-Repair Maintenance Plan Offer

02

Operator asset

Tech Script — Maintenance Plan Offer

TECH: Before I head out — can I mention something quickly? What I fixed today is exactly what we catch early on maintenance visits before it becomes an emergency. TECH: We offer an annual maintenance plan for $[amount] — covers a full inspection, water heater flush, drain test, and priority booking so you're not waiting during busy season. TECH: Most customers say it pays for itself in peace of mind alone. Want me to set it up while I'm here? [Present as professional advice, not a sales pitch. You're a plumber recommending good maintenance — that is your job.] Water Heater Replacement Conversation

03

Operator asset

Tech Script — Water Heater Replacement

TECH: I want to be straight with you about this water heater. It's [X] years old and this is the second time we've been out here for it. TECH: I can repair it — about $[amount] — but honestly at this age you're probably looking at another call within [timeframe]. A new unit is $[amount] installed with a [X]- year warranty. The math usually favors replacement past [X] years. TECH: But it's your call — I'll do whichever you decide. I just want you to have the full picture. [Always present both options honestly. Customers who feel informed don't have regret — and they refer.] Soft Add-On During Service

04

Operator asset

Tech Script — Soft Add-On

TECH: I noticed your drain is running a bit slow while I was working nearby. Not an emergency — just something worth knowing. TECH: I can clear that out for $[amount] while I'm already here. Takes 20 minutes and saves you the service call fee later. Totally up to you. [Only mention genuine observations. Never manufacture problems. Honest add-ons build loyalty.]
