Subject Quick Note On Your Home Exercises — [First Name]

Hi [First Name], I know home exercises can be hard to stick with — life gets in the way and I understand that completely. I do want to be honest with you: the research on your condition is really clear that HEP adherence is one of the strongest predictors of recovery. The work we do in the clinic is valuable — but the daily work at home is what locks in the progress. If there's something making it hard — time, pain, not remembering, not understanding a specific exercise — let's fix that at your next session. I'd rather adjust the program to something you'll actually do than keep prescribing something you won't. See you [date].
