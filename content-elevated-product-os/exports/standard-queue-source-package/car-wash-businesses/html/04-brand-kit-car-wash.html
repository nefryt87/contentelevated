Template 1: Before/After Transformation Post

Search 'before after car detail Instagram' or 'vehicle transformation post'. Two-panel: dirty car left, clean car right. Your business name and location at the bottom. A compelling caption about the difference. These are your highest-performing content type — post every great transformation result. Before/afters drive more inquiries than any other content for a detailing business.

03

Operator asset

Template 2: Membership Value Card

Search 'membership card Instagram' or 'subscription offer post'. Clean layout: your membership name, key benefits as bullet points, monthly price, 'SAVE $[X]/MONTH' callout. Your business name and sign-up link. Use your accent colour (blue or yellow) for the price. Post this monthly as a pinned post and update seasonally.

04

Operator asset

Template 3: Google Business Profile Weekly Post

Search 'local business update post' or 'Google business post template'. Clean, simple. Your service, a CTA, your address. Post every Monday — each GBP post improves your ranking for 'car wash near me'. 10 minutes per week, significant long-term impact. Include a photo of your facility or a clean car every time.
