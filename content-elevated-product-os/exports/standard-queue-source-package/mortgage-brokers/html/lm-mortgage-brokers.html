How To Use These Prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace [brackets] with your details. Each prompt takes under 5 minutes to run — and replaces 30-60 minutes of writing. Lead Nurture Prompts Pre-Approval Follow-Up Write a follow-up email for a mortgage lead named [Name] who got pre-approved [X] days ago but hasn't found a property yet. Mention current rate environment in one sentence, share one practical house-hunting tip, and keep the relationship warm. Under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Company Name]. Rate Drop Alert Write a rate alert email for my mortgage leads who have been in the pipeline for [X] months.
