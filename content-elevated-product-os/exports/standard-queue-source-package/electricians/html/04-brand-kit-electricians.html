Template 1: Google Business Profile Post

Search 'business update post' or 'service announcement'. Clean layout: your company colours, a bold headline (e.g., 'Now offering EV charger installation in [City]'), 2-3 bullet points, your phone number, and license number. GBP posts improve local search ranking — post weekly.

Operator asset

Template 2: Facebook Job Completion Post

Search 'before after service post' or 'completed job announcement'. Two-column: left side 'The Challenge', right side 'The Result'. Include job type, neighbourhood, your company name. These drive more engagement and inquiries than any other Facebook post type.
