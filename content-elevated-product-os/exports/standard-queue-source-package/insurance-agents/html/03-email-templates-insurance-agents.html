How To Use These Templates

• Replace all [brackets] with your client's name, policy type, and your agency info. • Personalize the opening line — AI can help: 'Add a personalized first sentence for [name] who [context].' • Templates are intentionally conversational — resist the urge to make them more formal. • A/B test subject lines — small changes in subject lines can double open rates. • Save your top performers as templates in Gmail or your CRM for one-click sending.
