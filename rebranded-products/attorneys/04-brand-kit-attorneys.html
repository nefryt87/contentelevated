ATTORNEY & LAW FIRM

BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a legal practice brand that commands trust, attracts ideal clients, and positions you as the obvious choice in your market. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Practice area positioning • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: POSITIONING

Legal clients don't just hire the most qualified attorney — they hire the one they trust most. Your brand communicates, before a single conversation, whether you're someone worth trusting with their most pressing legal matter. Your Practice Brand Statement

We help [client type — e.g., families / small businesses / individuals / entrepreneurs] navigate [legal situation] so they can [outcome — e.g., protect what matters most / move forward with confidence / build on solid legal foundations].

Example: 'We help small business owners in [City] protect their companies, their contracts, and their futures — with practical legal counsel that doesn't require a law degree to understand.' Practice Area Positioning Angles

• Family Law: 'Protecting your family through life's most difficult transitions'

• Estate Planning: 'Peace of mind for you and the people who depend on you'

• Business Law: 'Legal foundations that let your business grow without surprises'

• Personal Injury: 'Fighting for the compensation you deserve — not what's

convenient'

• Immigration: 'Navigating the path forward, together'

• Criminal Defense: 'Your freedom deserves a serious defense'

• Real Estate: 'Every transaction protected from contract to closing'

• Employment Law: 'Standing up for workers' rights — and employers who do right

by their people'

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Days 1-2 — Foundation

✓ Choose color palette (A or B) and select fonts in Canva Brand Kit

✓ Write your Practice Brand Statement

✓ Define your primary practice area positioning angle

✓ Identify your top 3 referral source categories

Days 3-4 — Build Templates & Update Profiles

✓ Create LinkedIn post template and update LinkedIn banner

✓ Build firm overview PDF for referral sources

✓ Update Google Business Profile — photos, description, practice areas

✓ Update website attorney bio and practice area pages if applicable

Days 5-7 — First Week of Content

✓ Batch-create 3 LinkedIn posts using the 90-Day Calendar

✓ Generate post copy using the monthly AI content prompt

✓ Have posts reviewed for bar compliance

✓ Schedule Mon/Wed/Fri and engage with every comment

The best legal brand is built on one thing: consistent, trustworthy communication. AI helps you show up that way — every single week.
