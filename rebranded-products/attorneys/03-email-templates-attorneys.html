24 done-for-you, ethics-aware templates for every stage of the client relationship

Intake • Consultation follow-up • Client retention • Referral network • Reviews

Important: All templates should be reviewed for compliance with your state bar's rules on advertising, solicitation, and client communication before use.

HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with client name, matter details, and firm information.

• Review every template for compliance with your state bar's advertising and

solicitation rules before sending.

• Works as email and through client portals (Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther,

etc.).

• The tone throughout is professional, warm, and clear — clients hire

attorneys they trust and understand.
