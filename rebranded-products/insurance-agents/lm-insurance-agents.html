AI Prompt Cheat Sheet 10 AI prompts to follow up faster, retain more clients, and get more referrals

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace [brackets] with your details. Each prompt takes under 5 minutes to run — and replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

Client Retention Prompts

Annual Review Outreach Write a warm annual review email for my insurance client [Name] whose policy renews on [date]. Their current coverage: [brief summary]. Mention 1-2 life changes that might affect their coverage needs (marriage, new home, new baby). Invite them to a

15-minute review call. Sign as [Your Name], [Agency Name]. Policy Change Follow-Up Write a follow-up email to my client [Name] who recently [had a baby / bought a home / started a business]. Explain why this life event typically means they should review

their [life / home / business] coverage. Soft CTA to schedule a call. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name].

Referral & Prospecting Prompts

Post-Claim Thank-You + Referral Ask Write a post-claim follow-up for my client [Name] whose [claim type] claim was just resolved. Thank them for their patience, acknowledge what a stressful experience it

was, and gently ask if they know anyone who might benefit from a review of their coverage. Warm, not salesy. Under 150 words. Cold Prospect Intro Email Write a prospecting email to [Name], a [profession] in [city]. I'm an independent
