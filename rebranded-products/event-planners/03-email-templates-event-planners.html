HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your client's name, event details, and your

business information.

• Keep the tone aspirational, professional, and genuinely excited — clients

hire planners who make them feel their event is the most important one on your calendar.

• Always personalize the opening line using AI for the best first impression.

• Works as email and as messaging through planning platforms like Aisle

Planner or Honeybook.
