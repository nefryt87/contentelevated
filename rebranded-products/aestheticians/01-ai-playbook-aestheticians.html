MED SPA AESTHETICIAN

The AI Playbook for Med Spa Aestheticians Fill your books with returning clients, upsell treatments with confidence, build your personal

brand, and create a client experience that drives referrals

INTRODUCTION

The aestheticians with full appointment books aren't just the most skilled. They follow up after every treatment to check how the skin is responding. They reach out before the

client even thinks about rebooking. They recommend the right next treatment at exactly

the right time. They build a personal brand on Instagram that makes clients feel like they already know and trust them before they ever book. And they create a client experience so consistently excellent that referrals happen naturally. This playbook gives you 7

chapters covering every high-leverage communication scenario — with copy-paste AI prompts throughout.

CHAPTER 1: NEW CLIENT CONVERSION &

CONSULTATION

The New Client Inquiry Response System First impressions set the tone for the entire client relationship. A warm, specific, knowledgeable response to a new client inquiry — whether via DM, email, or phone —

converts at dramatically higher rates than a generic 'we'd love to help you!'

PROMPT — NEW CLIENT INQUIRY RESPONSE

Write a warm, professional inquiry response email for my aesthetician practice. The potential client [Name] reached out about [treatment type — e.g., HydraFacial / chemical peel / Botox consult / microneedling / lash lift]. They mentioned: [paste key

details — their skin concern, what they're hoping to achieve, any questions they have]. I want to: (1) acknowledge their specific concern and show I understand it, (2) briefly explain how [treatment] addresses it, (3) mention what a consultation looks

like and what they can expect, (4) invite them to book. Warm and knowledgeable, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

CHAPTER 2: REBOOKING & TREATMENT

SERIES SYSTEM

The Post-Treatment Follow-Up & Rebooking System The single most effective thing an aesthetician can do for their business is follow up 3-5 days after every treatment. Most don't. The ones who do have the fullest books,

because clients feel cared for — and they rebook before they've even finished healing.

PROMPT — POST-TREATMENT CHECK-IN (3-5 DAYS)

Write a post-treatment check-in message for my aesthetician practice. Client [Name] had [treatment type] on [date]. I want to: (1) check in on how their skin is responding, (2) ask about any specific side effects they're noticing, (3) give one

helpful tip relevant to where they'll be in their healing, (4) plant the seed for their next appointment without being pushy. Warm and caring. Under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

PROMPT — REBOOKING REMINDER (4-6 WEEKS)

Write a rebooking reminder message for my aesthetician practice. Client [Name] had [treatment] on [date]. Their recommended rebooking window is [X] weeks. I want to: (1) reference their treatment specifically, (2) mention that now is the optimal

time to book their next session for best results, (3) make it easy to book. Brief and warm. Under 100 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name]. Treatment Series Selling Clients who commit to a treatment series achieve dramatically better results and

generate more revenue per client. The conversation about a series is best had immediately after the first treatment — when the client is excited and you've just demonstrated your value.

PROMPT — TREATMENT SERIES RECOMMENDATION

Write an email recommending a treatment series to a client after their first session.

Client [Name] just had their first [treatment]. Their skin concern: [concern]. I want to:

(1) compliment their skin's response to today's treatment, (2) explain why a series of [X] sessions produces significantly better results than a single treatment, (3) present the series investment vs. individual session pricing, (4) offer to book all sessions

now to guarantee availability. Warm and educational. Under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio/Spa Name].

CHAPTER 4: PERSONAL BRAND & INSTAGRAM

STRATEGY

Content Pillars That Build Client Trust and Book Appointments

Instagram is where most new aesthetician clients discover and vet their treatment provider. The accounts that consistently drive bookings post across 4 pillars: education (skin tips, treatment explanations, myth-busting), transformation (before/afters, results),

personal brand (your face, your process, your voice), and social proof (reviews, client reactions).

PROMPT — INSTAGRAM CAPTION BATCH

Write 5 Instagram captions for an aesthetician at a med spa. Topics: (1) a common skin myth you want to debunk — specific and backed by your expertise, (2) what clients with [skin type/concern — e.g., congested skin / ageing skin / sensitive skin]

should know before booking any treatment, (3) a before/after result story (anonymised, describe the transformation in words if you can't show the photo), (4) a behind-the-scenes look at your consultation process — what you look for and why,

(5) a direct booking CTA with a limited availability hook. Each under 150 words, warm and authoritative. Spa name: [Spa/Studio Name].

CHAPTER 7: 30-DAY IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

Week 1: New Client Conversion System

• Build your new client inquiry response template using Chapter 1 prompt

• Write your post-consultation follow-up email

• Set a reminder: follow up within 24 hours of every consultation

Week 2: Rebooking System

• Build your post-treatment check-in message (3-5 days) for your top 3 most-

booked treatments

• Write your rebooking reminder for each treatment's optimal window

• Identify past clients overdue for rebooking — send the Chapter 2 reminder to 10

of them this week

Week 3: Instagram Content Batch

• Use Chapter 4 Instagram prompt to batch 10 captions in one session

• Plan 3 Reels using the Chapter 4 Reel concept prompt

• Schedule 4-5 posts per week for the next 3 weeks

Week 4: Review & VIP System

• Identify clients who haven't reviewed — send personal Google review requests

• Identify your top 5 VIP clients — send the Chapter 5 milestone email

• Set post-treatment review request as a standard step 3-5 days after every
