paying for. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva template strategies built specifically for med spa aestheticians and skin specialists.

Template 1: Before & After Result Post

Search 'before after skin Instagram' or 'skincare transformation post'. Two-panel layout: before skin on left, after on right (or describe in text if not showing client photos). Brief description of treatment and timeframe. Your name and studio. HIPAA/consent note:

always get explicit written consent before sharing any client photos. These are the highest-converting post type for aestheticians.

Template 2: Skin Education Carousel

Search 'skincare tips carousel' or 'educational slides Instagram'. 5-7 slides: cover slide with bold question or hook ('Is your SPF actually working?'), one tip or insight per slide, final CTA slide with your name and booking link. Carousels with genuine educational

value save at very high rates — clients bookmark them and share them.

Template 3: Treatment Spotlight Post

Search 'beauty treatment Instagram' or 'facial treatment post'. Clean layout: treatment name in large heading, a brief plain-language description, who it is for, the outcome, your booking link. Create one of these for each of your treatments and use them as

evergreen content throughout the year.

Template 4: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'testimonial quote wellness' or 'client review skin'. Feature the most specific and emotional line from a review. First name and treatment type below. Your studio name. These build trust before any conversation starts — schedule one per week.

Template 5: Instagram Story — Booking CTA

Search 'Instagram story booking wellness' or 'appointment available story'. Vertical. A soft, clean treatment room or product flat lay as background. Text: 'Now booking [month] appointments' + 'limited availability'. Your name and booking link. Post every

time you open new booking windows.

Template 6: Google Business Profile Post

Search 'Google business post template' or 'local business update'. Clean, professional. Treatment name, brief benefit, price point, booking link. Every GBP post improves your local search ranking — post weekly. A 10-minute investment per post generates

Template 7: Skincare Myth Graphic

Search 'myth vs fact social media' or 'debunk graphic'. Bold 'MYTH' header, the myth in quotes, the truth below in clear language. Your name. These attract significant shares and saves — skin misinformation is everywhere and clients appreciate an expert voice

that's clear and evidence-based.

Template 8: Instagram Highlight Covers

Search 'highlight cover wellness' or 'spa Instagram icons'. Create 8 round covers: Treatments, Reviews, Before & After, Skin Tips, About Me, Booking, Homecare, Skincare FAQs. Consistent palette. First thing profile visitors see after your photo.
