5 Free AI Prompts to Grow Your Practice Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Post-Consultation Follow-Up Email

Write a post-consultation follow-up email for my CPA practice. The prospect [Name] came in about [matter type — e.g., business tax planning / personal tax return / new business setup]. Key things they shared: [list 2-3 things]. I want to: (1) summarize their

situation in plain language showing I listened, (2) outline my recommended approach in 2-3 sentences, (3) present a clear next step. Professional but warm, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

PROMPT 2: Monthly Referral Partner Value Touch

Write a monthly value email from a CPA to a referral partner — [attorney / financial advisor] named [Name] at [Firm]. Include: (1) one brief tax or accounting insight relevant to their clients right now, (2) a soft mention of any relevant case type I'm available

for. Professional, genuinely useful, under 150 words. Should feel like useful information from a knowledgeable colleague. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].

PROMPT 3: Tax Season Kickoff Email

Write a tax season kickoff email for my CPA practice to send to clients in January or February. Cover: (1) our timeline for the season, (2) what documents clients need to gather and submit, (3) our deadline for receiving documents to guarantee on-time filing,

(4) how to submit documents. Professional, warm, and organised, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Firm Name].
