5 Free AI Prompts to Fill Your Books Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Instagram DM Inquiry Response

Write a warm, professional Instagram DM response for my lash studio. The client [Name] reached out about [service — e.g., classic lash extensions / hybrid / volume / lash lift]. They mentioned: [paste key details]. My speciality is [your style or technique]. I want

to: (1) make them feel like I genuinely read their message, (2) briefly describe how I would approach their lashes, (3) ask one qualifying question if needed or invite them to book. Warm and knowledgeable, under 120 words. Sign as [Your Name / Studio Name].

PROMPT 2: New Client First Appointment Email

Write a first appointment information email for a new lash extension client. Client [Name], appointment on [date] at [time]. Cover: (1) what to expect, (2) how to prepare — clean face, no eye makeup, contact lenses removed, (3) how long the appointment takes, (4)

what aftercare looks like immediately after, (5) reassure any common anxieties. Warm and informative, under 250 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 3: Rebooking Reminder at Optimal Window

link. Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name / Studio Name].
