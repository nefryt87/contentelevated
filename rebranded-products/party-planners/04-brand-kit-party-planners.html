WHY YOUR BRAND IS THE FIRST MAGIC TRICK

Parents choose a party planner based on Instagram before they ever make contact. Your brand — your colour palette, your photography aesthetic, how your grid looks — communicates whether you create the kind of magic their child deserves. A distinctive,

joyful, and polished visual identity attracts clients who value what you do and are ready to invest in it. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings, and Canva strategies built specifically for party planners.

Typography

HEADINGS: Cormorant Garamond — editorial luxury. Elegant and celebratory without being childish. BODY: Jost — clean and modern. Readable across Instagram, email, and printed materials. ACCENT: Pinyon Script — for event names, milestone ages, headline moments in a

styled graphic. Use sparingly.

Typography

HEADINGS: Nunito ExtraBold — friendly, rounded, joyful. Works perfectly for both kids parties and fun adult events. BODY: Poppins Regular — modern and clean. Reads beautifully across all digital formats.

ACCENT: Nunito ExtraBold ALL CAPS — for theme names, ages ('TURNING 5!'), party announcements.

8 CANVA TEMPLATE STRATEGIES

Template 1: Party Reveal Post

Search 'party reveal Instagram' or 'event setup reveal post'. The finished party space — balloons, tablescapes, backdrop, every element in frame. Your business name small at the bottom. This is your most powerful content type. Every reveal post attracts new

followers and drives direct inquiry DMs. Post every single event you plan.

Template 2: Before / After Setup Transformation

Search 'transformation before after Instagram' or 'event setup time-lapse'. Two images: empty room left, decorated party right. Or use a split screen. Caption: 'This is what [X hours] of setup looks like.' These perform exceptionally well on TikTok as videos and on

Instagram as carousels.

Template 3: Theme Inspiration Carousel

Search 'party theme ideas Instagram' or 'kids birthday inspiration carousel'. 5-7 slides: one theme per slide with a beautiful image and brief description. Final slide: your name and CTA. Create one for each of your most popular themes — parents save these and

return to them when planning. High-save-rate content.

Template 4: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'party planner testimonial post' or 'client review celebration'. Feature the most emotional line from a parent's review — something about their child's face, the magic of the moment, how stress-free it felt. First name and event type below. Your business

name. Post every new testimonial.

Template 5: Instagram Story — Booking Open

Search 'booking available story' or 'party planner story template'. Vertical. A beautiful party photo as background. Text: 'NOW BOOKING [SEASON/MONTH] PARTIES' + 'DM to enquire.' Your business name. Post every time you open new availability — generates immediate DMs.

Template 6: TikTok / Reel Cover Frame

Search 'TikTok first frame template' or 'Reel cover birthday party'. For every video: your hook text in large font on a branded background. 'Watch how I transformed this room in 3 hours' or 'This is what a [theme] party actually looks like'. Your business name small.

Makes viewers click rather than scroll past.

Template 7: Party Theme Mood Board

Search 'party mood board Pinterest' or 'event inspiration board'. A collage of images representing a specific theme — colours, balloons, cake, decorations, linens, activities. Your business name. Create one for each of your signature themes and pin them to

Pinterest. Parents discover party planners through theme-specific searches.

Template 8: Instagram Highlight Covers

Search 'highlight cover party' or 'celebration Instagram icons'. Create 8 round covers: Portfolio, Kids Parties, Milestone Events, Baby Showers, Reviews, Themes, Behind the Scenes, Booking. Consistent palette. Every profile visitor sees these — they

communicate your range and professionalism instantly.

7-DAY BRAND LAUNCH CHECKLIST

✓ Day 1: Choose your palette and set up Canva brand kit.

✓ Day 2: Update Instagram bio, profile photo, and create 8 highlight covers using

Template 8.

✓ Day 3: Create 10 party reveal post overlays using Template 1 — apply to your 10

best party photos.

✓ Day 4: Create a theme inspiration carousel for your most popular theme using

Template 3.

✓ Day 5: Create 5 testimonial graphics using Template 4 — schedule one per week

for 5 weeks.

✓ Day 6: Create a TikTok cover frame template using Template 6 — apply to your

next 5 videos.

✓ Day 7: Set up or refresh Pinterest — create 5 themed boards and pin 10 images to

each with keyword-rich descriptions.

✓ [Instagram and Pinterest are where parties are discovered. A professional,

beautiful, consistent brand presence across both platforms is the difference between clients who find you already convinced and clients who need to be persuaded.]
