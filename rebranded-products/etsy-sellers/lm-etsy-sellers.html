ETSY SELLER

5 Free AI Prompts to Grow Your Etsy Shop Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Etsy Listing Title Optimisation (5 options)

Write 5 optimised Etsy listing title options for my product: [describe your product in detail — material, style, size, what it is, who it is for]. My current title is: [paste current title if you have one]. I want titles that: (1) lead with the most searched

keywords, (2) include specific descriptors buyers use when searching — material, style, occasion, recipient, (3) are under 140 characters, (4) feel natural to read. Include a mix — some leading with product type, some with occasion or recipient. Provide each title on

its own line.

PROMPT 2: Etsy Listing Description

Write a compelling Etsy listing description for my product: [describe product — what it is, materials, dimensions, variations, what makes it special]. Target buyer: [describe your ideal customer — e.g., a bride looking for personalised wedding gifts / a home

decorator who loves minimalist style]. I want the description to: (1) open with a hook addressing the buyer's need or occasion, (2) describe the product clearly with all key details, (3) answer the most common buyer questions, (4) include relevant keywords

naturally, (5) end with a reassuring next step. Under 400 words. Shop name: [Shop Name].

PROMPT 3: Post-Delivery Follow-Up Message (Review Request)

Write a post-delivery follow-up Etsy message for my shop. The customer [Name] ordered [product] and it should have arrived approximately [X days ago]. I want to: (1) check in and make sure they received it and are happy, (2) provide any relevant care or use

instructions briefly, (3) make a warm personal ask for a review explaining why it matters to a small shop, (4) invite them to contact me if anything is not perfect. Warm and genuine, under 150 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Shop Name].
