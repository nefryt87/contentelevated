5 Free AI Prompts to Book More Couples Copy-paste into Claude or ChatGPT — replace [brackets] — done in under 5 minutes each

How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: Same-Day Inquiry Response

Write a warm, enthusiastic inquiry response email for my wedding photography studio. The couple [Names] reached out about their wedding on [date] at [venue]. They mentioned: [paste 2-3 details]. My style is [your style]. I want to: (1) make them feel like I

genuinely read their inquiry, (2) briefly describe how I'd approach their specific wedding, (3) invite them to a discovery call with a specific time offered. Warm, excited, under 200 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 2: Post-Booking Welcome Email

Write a post-booking welcome email for my wedding photography studio. Couple [Names] just signed their contract for their wedding on [date] at [venue]. I want to: (1) celebrate with genuine excitement, (2) briefly outline what they can expect — key touchpoints from

now to wedding day, (3) tell them the best way to reach me, (4) invite them to start a Pinterest inspiration board. Warm and personal, under 250 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 3: Gallery Delivery Email

Write a gallery delivery email for my wedding photography studio. Couple [Names], wedding [date] at [venue]. Gallery contains [number] images via [platform]. I want to: (1) build anticipation before giving the link, (2) share 2-3 personal observations from their day,

(3) explain how to download and share, (4) plant the seed for a review ask without asking yet. Emotional, personal, specific. Under 300 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Studio Name].

PROMPT 4: Venue Coordinator Introduction
