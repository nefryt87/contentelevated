Free Mini Guide 5 communication strategies that reduce first-timer anxiety, improve retention, and fill your treatment schedule The practices building waitlists aren't just delivering better results — they communicate better before, during,

and after every appointment. Here are five systems that make the biggest difference. The Pre-Treatment Education Email First-time injectable and laser clients cancel primarily due to anxiety — not scheduling conflicts or budget. A

pre-appointment education email sent 48 hours before first treatments reduces cancellations significantly.  What to do before the appointment (and what to avoid)  What the treatment actually feels like — honest and reassuring

 What to expect immediately after (and when results peak)  A personal note: 'You're always in control — we go at your pace' Write a pre-treatment education email for a med spa client [Name] who has a [Botox /

filler / laser] appointment on [date] at [time]. Include: prep instructions, what the treatment feels like, what results to expect and when, and one reassuring closing sentence. Warm, educational. Under 200 words. Sign as [Practitioner Name], [Spa Name].

The Post-Treatment Rebooking System Most med spa clients intend to return — but only 40-50% actually rebook without a follow-up prompt. A post-treatment email sent 48 hours after every appointment is the single highest-ROI communication habit in aesthetics.

 A warm check-in on how they're feeling and how results are progressing  One specific aftercare reminder relevant to their treatment  The results timeline (when to expect full results)  A gentle prompt to book their next appointment or maintenance visit

Write a post-treatment follow-up email for a med spa client [Name] who had a [treatment] on [date]. Check in on how they're feeling, give 2 specific aftercare tips for their treatment, share the results timeline, and suggest rebooking in

[timeframe]. Warm, under 150 words. Sign as [Practitioner Name], [Spa Name]. The Maintenance Reminder System Botox clients who aren't reminded to rebook simply drift away — often to a competitor who followed up. A

treatment-specific maintenance reminder sent at the right time is your most reliable retention tool.  Botox: reminder at 3-4 months (before results fully wear off)  Filler: reminder at 6 months  Laser packages: mid-series encouragement and next-session prompt
