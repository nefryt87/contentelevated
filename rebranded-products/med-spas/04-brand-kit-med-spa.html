MED SPA & AESTHETIC PRACTICE

BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build an aesthetic brand that commands premium investment, earns deep trust, and makes clients feel like insiders before they ever book. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • Treatment positioning • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: POSITIONING

Med spa clients aren't just buying a treatment — they're investing in how they feel about themselves. Your brand communicates whether you're a clinical factory or a practice that sees them, knows them, and delivers results they'll love for years. Your Aesthetic Brand Promise

We help [client type — e.g., women / men / adults / professionals] achieve [aesthetic outcome] through [your approach — e.g., evidence-based, personalized aesthetic medicine that enhances your natural beauty without erasing what makes you, you].

Example: 'We help [City] women look refreshed, confident, and completely themselves — through personalized aesthetic treatments that enhance your natural beauty without the frozen or overdone look.' Treatment Focus Positioning Angles

• Injectables: 'Natural-looking results that enhance — never replace — your

features'

• Laser & Skin: 'Medical-grade skin transformation, not just surface-level treatment'

• Body Contouring: 'Science-backed body results without surgery or downtime'

• Bridal Aesthetics: 'Look like the most radiant version of yourself on your most

important day'

• Anti-Aging Focus: 'Age gracefully — on your terms, at your pace'

• Men's Aesthetics: 'Results-focused treatments for men who want to look their

best'

• Holistic Wellness: 'Aesthetic medicine as part of your overall health and wellbeing

practice'
