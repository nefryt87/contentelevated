SECTION 2: EMERGENCY PROTOCOL CARD

[BUSINESS NAME] — EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

IF A DOG IS INJURED OR UNWELL:

• Remain calm — dogs read your energy

• Remove the dog from any hazard immediately

• Assess the situation — is it minor or serious?

• Contact the owner immediately

• If urgent: contact the emergency vet — [Local Emergency Vet Name +

Phone]

IF A DOG GOES MISSING:

• Search the immediate area thoroughly for 10 minutes

• Contact the owner immediately

• Contact local dog warden / animal control

• Post on local Facebook groups and Nextdoor

• Check if the dog is microchipped — contact the chip registry

IF THERE IS A DOG FIGHT:

• Never reach between two fighting dogs with your hands

• Use a loud noise, water, or a barrier to interrupt

• Check both dogs thoroughly for injuries

• Document the incident immediately

• Contact the owner of both dogs
