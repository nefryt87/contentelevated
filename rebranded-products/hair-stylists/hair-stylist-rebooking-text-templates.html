HOW TO USE

BEFORE YOU SEND

Make every text feel personal. Each template is ready to copy and paste directly into a text message. Replace anything in brackets with your specific details.

THE RULE

Only send these if you mean them. These texts work because they feel personal, not automated. Take 10 seconds to add one detail specific to that

client before you hit send.

BEST PRACTICE

Keep the tone warm, casual, and direct. You are not chasing the appointment. You are making the next booking feel easy and natural. 01

SEND FOUR WEEKS AFTER A COLOR APPOINTMENT

Hey [name]! It's been about a month since your last color — just wanted to check in and see how it's holding up. I have some openings coming up if you're thinking about coming in. Just reply and I'll send you some times!

Pro tip: Keep it casual. Don't say “your roots are showing.ˮ Say “how's it holding upˮ and let them come to that

conclusion themselves.
