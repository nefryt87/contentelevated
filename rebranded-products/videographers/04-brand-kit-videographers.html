VIDEOGRAPHER

Brand Kit + Canva Template Guide

Two signature palettes, cinematic typography, 7 Canva template strategies, and a 7-day brand launch checklist

WHY YOUR BRAND BOOKS CLIENTS BEFORE

YOUR REEL DOES

Clients vet a videographer's brand before they ever watch a frame of footage. A cohesive, intentional visual identity communicates taste, professionalism, and a level of craft that justifies premium pricing. This kit gives you two palettes, typography pairings,

and Canva strategies built specifically for videographers working across wedding, corporate, and commercial markets.

PALETTE 1: CINEMATIC DARK

For videographers with a moody, cinematic, editorial aesthetic. Think film grain, deep shadows, candlelight, and intentional darkness.

• Deep Navy (#1A2744): logo, headlines, primary text — premium and established

white

Typography

HEADINGS: Raleway Bold — modern and architectural. Works for both print and digital. BODY: Lato — clean, professional, highly readable. Widely trusted across commercial creative businesses. ACCENT: Raleway ExtraBold ALL CAPS — for package names, award callouts, and key stats.

Template 1: Film Still + Caption Post

Template 2: Client Testimonial Graphic

Search 'testimonial quote dark' or 'client review post'. Feature the most emotional line from a review. Client first name and project type below. Your studio name in the corner. Build trust before any conversation starts. Create 5-10 of these from your best reviews

and schedule them throughout the year.

Template 3: Instagram Story — Inquiry CTA

Search 'Instagram story video booking' or 'cinematographer story template'. Vertical. A beautiful video still as background. Dark overlay. Text: 'Now booking [season] weddings' or 'Taking on new corporate clients'. Your studio name. Link sticker. Post to stories

weekly during peak inquiry season.

Template 4: YouTube Thumbnail

Search 'YouTube thumbnail cinematic' or 'video thumbnail template'. 1280×720px. A strong emotional still from your film, the title in large readable text, your logo small in a corner. YouTube thumbnails are your most important piece of graphic design — they

determine whether people click your films.

Template 5: LinkedIn Professional Banner

Search 'LinkedIn banner videographer' or 'video production LinkedIn cover'. 1584×396px. Your best commercial film still, your studio name, your speciality, your city. This is seen by every business contact and potential corporate client who visits your profile.

Template 6: Package Comparison Graphic

Search 'pricing table comparison' or 'service package table'. Three columns, your three packages. Each column: package name, key inclusions as bullet points, price. Your studio name at top. Use this in proposals, on your website, and in Instagram carousels.

Removes price uncertainty before a consultation.
