SECTION 3: WEDDING MORNING ARTIST

CHECKLIST

Pre-Wedding Night Kit Check

✓ MAKEUP KIT:

✓ Foundation: exact shade match(es) for each client in party

✓ Concealers: colour-correctors, under-eye, blemish

✓ Contouring: cream and powder options

✓ Eyes: full palette — neutrals, mid-tones, darks, shimmer

✓ Brows: pomade, pencil, gel, scissors, tweezers

✓ Lips: bride's approved shade (at least 2 backups), lip liner, gloss

✓ Setting: setting spray x2, loose powder, blotting papers

✓ Lashes: bride's approved style x2, party lash options, glue x2

✓ HAIR KIT:

✓ Heat tools: straightener, curling wand(s) x2 sizes, tongs

✓ Pins and grips: bobby pins (x50+), U-pins, kirby grips — all colours

✓ Elastics: multiple sizes and colours

✓ Products: volumising spray, texture spray, shine spray, hairspray (x2), dry shampoo

✓ Accessories: any pre-agreed hair accessories (confirm with bride)

✓ HYGIENE & PROFESSIONAL:

✓ Brush cleaner + sanitising spray

✓ Disposable applicators (wands, sponges, spatulas)

✓ Hand sanitiser

✓ Face masks if preferred

✓ Palette knives

✓ ADMIN:

✓ Signed contract copy

✓ Balance due reminder (if applicable)

✓ Shot list of looks (from trial sign-off form)

✓ Photographer/planner contact numbers

✓ Emergency contact for bride

✓ [Review this checklist the night before every wedding. The item you forget will be

the one you need.]

SECTION 4: VENDOR CO-MARKETING

PROPOSAL

Photographer + Bridal Artist Styled Shoot Proposal

SUBJECT A collaboration idea — [Your Name] + [Photographer Name]

Hi [Name], I've been thinking about our shared audience and I wanted to put a specific idea on the table. What I'm proposing: a styled bridal shoot collaboration. I handle all hair and makeup styling for [X] looks, you photograph them. We both walk away with a

stunning portfolio set, co-branded marketing material, and a genuine referral relationship built on working together. Concept I have in mind: [brief description — venue, mood, season, number of looks, any dress/florist already in mind].

What I can offer: complete hair and makeup styling, portfolio usage rights, credit in all publications, and a genuine referral partner who actively recommends you to every bride I book. What I'm looking for: your photography talent and creative vision.

Would a quick call to explore this work for you? [Your Name] | [Studio Name] | [Phone] | [Instagram]
