SECTION 2: SCOPE CONVERSATION SCRIPTS

Presenting Your Fee — The Budget Conversation

SCRIPT — PRESENTING YOUR FEE CONFIDENTLY

YOU: Before I put together the proposal, I want to have an honest conversation about investment — because it shapes everything we'll do together. YOU: For a project like yours — [brief description of scope] — you're typically

looking at a design fee in the range of $[X] to $[X], depending on complexity. That covers [brief description of what's included]. It doesn't include the cost of furniture, finishes, and trades, which we'd discuss separately.

YOU: The reason I'm sharing this before the proposal is that I'd rather have this conversation now than send you something that doesn't work. Does that range feel aligned with what you were expecting? [Listen. Don't jump in. Let them respond before you say anything else.]

[If yes: 'Great — I'll put together a full proposal within [timeframe].'] [If hesitation: see below] When the Client Pushes Back on Your Fee
