How to use these prompts

Copy and paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace every [bracket] with your details. Each prompt replaces 30-60 minutes of writing.

PROMPT 1: New Client DM Response

Write a warm, professional DM response for my barbershop. A potential new client [Name] messaged asking about [booking / prices / availability / a specific style]. I want to: (1) make them feel welcome and confident they are coming to the right place, (2) answer their

question directly, (3) make it easy to book. My booking process: [how clients book]. My price range: $[X]-$[X]. Under 100 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

PROMPT 2: 24-Hour Appointment Reminder

Write a 24-hour appointment reminder message for my barbershop. Client [Name], appointment tomorrow [date] at [time]. I want to: (1) remind them warmly, (2) confirm the key details, (3) mention my cancellation policy briefly, (4) share my direct number. Under 60 words.

Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

PROMPT 3: Rebooking Reminder at Optimal Window

Write a rebooking reminder message for a barbershop client. Client [Name] had their last cut [X weeks ago]. I want to: (1) mention their hair is at the point where a fresh cut will look sharp again, (2) make it easy to book, (3) feel personal not automated. Under 70

words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].

PROMPT 4: Google Review Request

Write a post-appointment Google review request message for my barbershop. Client [Name] just had [service]. I want to: (1) thank them for coming in, (2) make a warm personal ask for a Google review, (3) include the direct link, (4) make it feel personal not automated.

Under 80 words. Sign as [Your Name], [Barbershop Name].
