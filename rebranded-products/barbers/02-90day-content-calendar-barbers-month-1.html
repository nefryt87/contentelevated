BARBER

90-Day Content Calendar 12 weeks of Mon/Wed/Fri content for TikTok, Instagram, and Google Business Profile — with monthly AI caption prompts

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

TikTok is your fastest reach growth channel — transformation videos and technique

content go viral in the barbering niche regularly. Instagram builds loyalty and is where

clients DM to book. Google Business Profile posts improve your local search ranking — every post makes you more visible when someone searches 'barber near me'. Post Mon/Wed/Fri. Batch captions monthly with the AI prompt.

MONTH 1: BRAND & TRANSFORMATION

CONTENT

Write 8 Instagram captions for a barber. Topics: your barbering story and why you do this, your most popular cut explained, a transformation reveal story, a technique you love, what clients say about their experience, your shop vibe and culture, a style

tip for maintaining cuts at home, a direct booking CTA. Under 150 words each, confident and personal.

WEEK 1: YOUR STORY & BRAND

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon TikTok 'Why I became a barber' — your origin story. Hook: 'The moment I knew this was what I was meant to do.' Raw and genuine. Wed Instagram Your shop introduction: who you are, your speciality, your vibe. The post new clients find when they click your profile.

Fri Google Profile First GBP post: 'Now booking at [Barbershop Name] in [City]' — your services, booking link. GBP posts improve local search ranking.

WEEK 2: TRANSFORMATION CONTENT

DAY PLATFORM CONTENT IDEA

Mon TikTok Before/after transformation — show the client arriving, the cut in progress, the reveal. Music + text overlay. Wed Instagram Transformation carousel: before photo, in-progress shot, final result. Caption tells the story of what the client asked for.

Fri TikTok Technique breakdown: 'How I do the perfect skin fade' — close- up, educational, high watch time.
