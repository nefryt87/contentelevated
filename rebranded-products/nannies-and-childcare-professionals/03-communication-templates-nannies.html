SECTION 1: FAMILY INQUIRIES & INTERVIEWS

T1 — Family Inquiry Response

SUBJECT Re: Nanny inquiry — [Your Name]

Dear [Name], Thank you so much for reaching out — your family sounds wonderful and the role you've described is exactly the kind of placement I love being part of. [2-3 sentences showing you've read their inquiry carefully — reference their

children's ages, the hours, anything specific they mentioned.] A few questions before we go further: [2-3 specific questions — e.g., 'What does a typical day look like for your children?' / 'Are there any specific

routines or approaches that are important to you?' / 'Is there anything particular you are looking for beyond the basics?'] I would love to arrange a call to learn more about your children and your family's needs. I am available [two specific times] this week.

Warm regards, [Your Name] | [Phone] | [Email] T2 — Platform First Message

SUBJECT Care platform — send directly

Hello [Name]! I came across your listing and I think I could be a wonderful fit for your family. [One specific sentence showing you read their listing — e.g., 'I have extensive experience with the [age] stage and your approach to routine really resonates with me.']

A little about me: [2-3 sentences — experience, qualifications, approach]. I would love to learn more about your children and discuss the role. Would a call this week work? [Your Name] T3 — Interview Confirmation

SUBJECT Looking forward to meeting you — [Your Name]

Dear [Name], I'm so looking forward to our meeting on [date] at [time] at [location / video call]. I'll make sure to bring: [list — e.g., my CV, reference letters, first aid certificate, DBS/background check].

Is there anything specific you'd like me to prepare or think about before we meet? I want to make the most of our time together. See you [day]! [Your Name] | [Phone] T4 — Post-Interview Follow-Up

SECTION 2: RATES & AGREEMENTS

T5 — Rate Discussion Email

SUBJECT Rate discussion — [Your Name]

Dear [Name], I am delighted to be in conversation about this role — I wanted to address the rate question directly so we are both clear. My rate is $[amount] gross per hour / $[amount] gross per week. This reflects

[brief explanation — e.g., my [X] years of experience with children of your children's ages, my first aid certification, and my commitment to ongoing professional development]. For this rate you can expect: [brief description — hours covered, activities,

any additional duties included]. Before I can give you a final number, it would help to know your budget range — that way we can be honest with each other about whether this is likely to work. Warm regards, [Your Name] T6 — Working Agreement Introduction

SUBJECT Working agreement — let's make this formal

Dear [Name], I am so excited to be moving forward with this role — I genuinely believe we are going to work wonderfully together. Before we start, I would love for us to put a simple working agreement in place. It does not need to be complicated — it just means we are both clear

from day one on hours, duties, pay, holiday entitlement, sick leave, and notice period. This protects both of us and prevents the small misunderstandings that can sometimes arise in even the best nanny-family relationships.

SECTION 5: CHALLENGING SITUATIONS

T14 — Scope Creep Conversation

SUBJECT Our arrangement — [Your Name]

Dear [Name], I hope you know how much I value our relationship — working with your children has been one of the genuine privileges of my professional life. I wanted to have a straightforward conversation about something before it

becomes a source of friction. Over the past [timeframe], my duties have expanded to include [specific examples — e.g., school run for an additional child / additional household management / weekend cover on top of my contracted hours].

I am happy to continue with these additional duties — I just think it would be fair for us to formally update my working agreement to reflect them. Could we find 20 minutes to discuss this week? I want this arrangement to work brilliantly for all of us, long term.

Warm regards, [Your Name] T15 — Notice of Resignation
