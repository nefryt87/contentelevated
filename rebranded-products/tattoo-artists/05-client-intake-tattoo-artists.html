SECTION 2: CONSULTATION PROCESS GUIDE

How to Run a Productive Consultation

✓ BEFORE THE CONSULTATION:

✓ Review the intake form completely — come with specific questions

✓ Look up any reference styles or artists the client mentioned

✓ Have your portfolio ready to show relevant examples

✓ Prepare rough size/pricing estimates for the type of work

✓

✓ THE CONSULTATION (20-30 minutes):

✓ 1. Start with open questions: 'Tell me more about the idea — what drew you to it?'

✓ 2. Show relevant portfolio examples: 'This is similar to what you're describing —

does this feel right?'

✓ 3. Discuss placement together: 'For this type of design, these placements work

particularly well because...'

✓ 4. Talk size honestly: 'For the level of detail you want, this would work better at [X

size] — here's why...'

✓ 5. Cover timeline: 'A piece like this would take approximately [X] hours. We could do

it in one session or split it.'

✓ 6. Give the quote: 'For everything we've discussed, I'd quote $[amount]. This

includes [what's covered].'

✓ 7. Address concerns: 'Any questions before you decide?'

✓ 8. Next steps: 'To confirm your booking, I'll need a deposit of $[amount] via

[method]. I'll send you a design preview [X days] before the appointment.'

✓

✓ AFTER THE CONSULTATION:

✓ Send the post-consultation follow-up email within 24 hours

✓ Hold the spot for [X days] maximum before opening to others

SECTION 3: FLASH DAY PLANNING KIT

Flash Day Checklist

✓ 4 WEEKS BEFORE:

✓ Design your flash sheet — [X] designs, each to be done once only

✓ Photograph or scan designs clearly

✓ Set your pricing range for each design

✓ Choose your date and confirm studio availability

✓ 2 WEEKS BEFORE:

✓ Announce on Instagram and TikTok — post flash preview images

✓ Open bookings: DM, online form, or first-come-first-served walk-in

✓ Email past clients with early access announcement

✓ 1 WEEK BEFORE:

✓ Post daily countdowns on stories

✓ Share individual design previews — one per day

✓ Confirm booked appointments and send prep instructions

✓ DAY OF:

✓ Arrive early — set up organised workspace

✓ Have flash sheet visible and accessible

✓ Prep stencils in advance for booked designs

✓ Track which designs are done — mark them off publicly on stories

✓ AFTER THE DAY:

✓ Post photos of completed flash work (with permission)

✓ Thank clients publicly — story or post

✓ Request reviews from flash day clients 4-6 weeks later

✓ [Flash days are one of the most effective ways to fill a calendar, create urgency,

and attract new clients — run one quarterly at minimum.]

[STUDIO NAME] — TATTOO AFTERCARE

IMMEDIATE CARE (first 2-3 hours):

• Leave wrap on for 2-3 hours (or as instructed)

• Remove wrap, wash gently with fragrance-free soap and warm water

• Pat dry with a clean paper towel — never rub

DAYS 1-7:

• Wash 2x daily with fragrance-free soap

• Apply a thin layer of [recommended product] 3-4x daily

• Keep clean and dry between applications

WHAT'S NORMAL:

• Redness, swelling, and soreness for 24-48 hours

• Peeling and flaking from day 4-14 — DO NOT PICK

• Itching as it heals — this is normal

AVOID:

• Direct sunlight on the fresh tattoo

• Swimming, soaking in baths, or hot tubs

• Tight clothing on the tattooed area

• Picking, scratching, or peeling

Questions or concerns: [Phone / DM / Email] Healed photo: send it in 4-6 weeks — we'd love to see it!
