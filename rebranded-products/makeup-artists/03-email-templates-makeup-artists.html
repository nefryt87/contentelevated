EMAIL & CLIENT COMMUNICATION

TEMPLATE PACK

22 done-for-you templates for every stage of the client journey Inquiries • Bridal booking • Retention • Reviews • Vendor partnerships

HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

• Replace all [brackets] with your client's name, event, and your business

details.

• Keep the tone warm, professional, and excited — clients want to feel

special, not processed.

• Works as email, DM, and SMS — shorten to 2–3 sentences for text

messages.

• Always personalize the first line using AI — 30 seconds, huge difference in

response rate.

- Services: [list key services]

- Investment: Starting from $[amount] for [service]

- Availability: I'm currently [available/checking] for [date/season]

Pre-Appointment Prep Email

SUBJECT How to prep for your appointment — [First Name]

BODY Hi [First Name], Your [bridal trial / appointment] is coming up on [Date] at [Time] — I'm so excited! To help us get the most out of our time together, here's how to prepare:

- Moisturize well the night before and morning of (no heavy serums right

before)

- Come with a clean, product-free face

- Wear a front-opening top or button-down

- Bring any inspo photos you love

- If you have any skin sensitivities or allergies, please let me know in advance

See you soon — this is going to be beautiful. [Your Name] Post-Appointment Follow-Up

SUBJECT How did everything go, [First Name]?

BODY Hi [First Name], I've been thinking about you since [your appointment / your big day] — I hope everything went absolutely perfectly! I'd love to know how it all went and whether you were happy with your look.

If you have a moment, a review on [Google / Instagram / The Knot] means the world to me — it helps other brides and clients find me. Here's the link: [link] And if you have any friends or family getting married or celebrating a special

occasion, I'd be honoured if you sent them my way. Thank you for trusting me with such an important moment. [Your Name]
