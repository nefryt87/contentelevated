FINANCIAL ADVISOR

BRAND KIT & CANVA TEMPLATE GUIDE

Build a financial advisory brand that earns trust before the first meeting. Color palettes • Fonts • Canva templates • LinkedIn guide • 7-day checklist

SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING

Financial advisory is the ultimate trust business. Clients are handing you their life savings, their retirement security, and their family's financial future. Your brand must communicate — before a single word is spoken — that you are credible, honest, and

genuinely on their side. Your Advisory Promise I help [specific client — e.g., business owners / families in their 40s–50s / corporate executives] [achieve financial outcome — e.g., build wealth that creates

choices / retire with confidence / preserve and transfer wealth to the next generation] by [your approach — e.g., creating a comprehensive financial plan tailored to their life / cutting through complexity with a clear, personalized strategy].

Example: 'I help business owners in [City] build the wealth and the exit strategy that makes their next chapter possible — not just their portfolio, but their whole financial life.' Ideal Client Profile Options

• Pre-retirees (55–65) with $500k–$2M investable assets

• Business owners planning for exit or succession

• Corporate executives with complex equity compensation

• Mass affluent families (40–55) building toward retirement

• Widows and divorcees navigating financial transitions

• Inheritors managing a first significant wealth event

SECTION 3: TYPOGRAPHY

USE FONT NOTES

Headlines Playfair Display Bold Distinguished and authoritative — signals premium positioning. Free on Google Fonts. Subheadings Source Sans Pro SemiBold Clean and readable — bridges authority with clarity.

Body Copy Source Sans Pro Regular Highly legible across all formats — excellent for reports and client communications. CTA / Emphasis Source Sans Pro Bold For clear calls to action and key data points. Use purposefully.
