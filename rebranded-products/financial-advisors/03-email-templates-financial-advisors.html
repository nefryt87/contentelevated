CLIENT COMMUNICATION TEMPLATE

PACK

25 done-for-you templates — compliance-review ready Prospect nurture • Client retention • Life events • Referrals • Market updates

COMPLIANCE NOTE

All templates in this pack are designed as starting points — not final communications. Every template should be reviewed and approved through your firm's compliance process before use. Templates avoid specific investment

recommendations and focus on education, planning concepts, and relationship- building. Always apply your firm's disclosure requirements to outgoing communications.

SECTION 1: PROSPECT NURTURE SEQUENCE

Post-Meeting Follow-Up — Same Day

SUBJECT Great meeting today — a quick summary

BODY Hi [First Name], It was a genuine pleasure meeting with you today. I wanted to send a quick summary of what we discussed while it's fresh: Your primary goals: [1–2 goals from the meeting] Key concerns you raised: [1–2 concerns]

What I'd recommend as a next step: [specific recommendation] I'll follow up in [timeframe] with [specific deliverable — e.g., a sample financial

plan outline / investment proposal / estate planning checklist].

In the meantime, feel free to reach out with any questions. [Your Name] | [Firm] | [Phone] | [Email] Note: This communication is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice. Prospect Follow-Up — Day 7 (Value Add)
